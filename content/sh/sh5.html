5: To be shy:

 

Istahyaa  اِستِحيا , istankaf  اِستَنكَف are the words used in the Quran.

 

1.        Istahyaa اِستِحيا :hiya حَيِى , yahyaa يحي ,hayaat  حيوةً means to be alove. He حي ,yahyee يحى ,haya حياء means to be embarrassed or ashamed or be shy.baab istafaal istahya  باب استفعال استحيا is derived from both the words & means to leave someone alive or to be shy of someone.Both these meanings are used in the Quran.istahya  استحيا means to be shy or be reluctant.It is both used for meaning & material.

 

The example of the material use of this word  is:

As per the Quran:28/25: Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص

 

فَجَاءَتْهُ إِحْدَاهُمَا تَمْشِي عَلَى اسْتِحْيَاءٍ قَالَتْ إِنَّ أَبِي يَدْعُوكَ لِيَجْزِيَكَ أَجْرَ مَا سَقَيْتَ لَنَا ۚ فَلَمَّا جَاءَهُ وَقَصَّ عَلَيْهِ الْقَصَصَ قَالَ لَا تَخَفْ ۖ نَجَوْتَ مِنَ الْقَوْمِ الظَّالِمِينَ

Then one of the two women came to him walking with shyness. She said, "Indeed, my father invites you that he may reward you for having watered for us." So when he came to him and related to him the story, he said, "Fear not. You have escaped from the wrongdoing people."

When this word is used for its meaning,then the following is a good example:

As per the Quran:6/26: Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يَسْتَحْيِي أَن يَضْرِبَ مَثَلًا مَّا بَعُوضَةً فَمَا فَوْقَهَا ۚ فَأَمَّا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا فَيَعْلَمُونَ أَنَّهُ الْحَقُّ مِن رَّبِّهِمْ ۖ وَأَمَّا الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا فَيَقُولُونَ مَاذَا أَرَادَ اللَّهُ بِهَٰذَا مَثَلًا ۘ يُضِلُّ بِهِ كَثِيرًا وَيَهْدِي بِهِ كَثِيرًا ۚ وَمَا يُضِلُّ بِهِ إِلَّا الْفَاسِقِينَ

 

Indeed, Allah is not timid to present an example - that of a mosquito or what is smaller than it. And those who have believed know that it is the truth from their Lord. But as for those who disbelieve, they say, "What did Allah intend by this as an example?" He misleads many thereby and guides many thereby. And He misleads not except the defiantly disobedient,

2.        Istinkaf اِستَنكَف : Nakif  نَكِف means show attitude. And istankaf  اِستَنكَف means to think of something as having little value because one is proud or thinks very high of himself.

 

Allah says in the Quran: 4/172: Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء

لَّن يَسْتَنكِفَ الْمَسِيحُ أَن يَكُونَ عَبْدًا لِّلَّهِ وَلَا الْمَلَائِكَةُ الْمُقَرَّبُونَ ۚ وَمَن يَسْتَنكِفْ عَنْ عِبَادَتِهِ وَيَسْتَكْبِرْ فَسَيَحْشُرُهُمْ إِلَيْهِ جَمِيعًا

 

Never would the Messiah disdain to be a servant of Allah , nor would the angels near [to Him]. And whoever disdains His worship and is arrogant - He will gather them to Himself all together.

 

Summary

Istahya  اِستِحيا means someone being shy because of shame & istankaf  اِستَنكَف is a disliked verb which means to be reluctant,to think of something as having less value because of undue pride.
