8. Partner:

 

Khaleet خَليط  , shareeq   شَريك  & andaadan  اَندادً are the words used.

 

1.        Khaleet خَليط  . This means to mix/combine one thing with another. (opposite khalas  خلص) Al-khalt  اَلخَلط means a social person. Al –khult  اَلخُلط  means partnership. Khaleet خَليط  are those people who have same interests. Meaning the participants that have some individual interests & some common interests. An example would be that X & Y own one flock of sheep each but have rented one place to keep them (combined rental) or have kept one shepherd to take care of both the flocks & pay him together. So such partners like X & Y are called khaleet خَليط  . As per hadeeth, zakat is to be calculated & deducted from the combined wealth of khaleet خَليط  .

As per Quran: 38/24: Surat Şād (The Letter "Saad") - سورة ص

 قَالَ لَقَدْ ظَلَمَكَ بِسُؤَالِ نَعْجَتِكَ إِلَىٰ نِعَاجِهِ ۖ وَإِنَّ كَثِيرًا مِّنَ الْخُلَطَاءِ لَيَبْغِي بَعْضُهُمْ عَلَىٰ بَعْضٍ إِلَّا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ وَقَلِيلٌ مَّا هُمْ ۗ وَظَنَّ دَاوُودُ أَنَّمَا فَتَنَّاهُ فَاسْتَغْفَرَ رَبَّهُ وَخَرَّ رَاكِعًا وَأَنَابَ

[David] said, "He has certainly wronged you in demanding your ewe [in addition] to his ewes. And indeed, many associates oppress one another, except for those who believe and do righteous deeds - and few are they." And David became certain that We had tried him, and he asked forgiveness of his Lord and fell down bowing [in prostration] and turned in repentance [to Allah ]

 

2.        Shareeq شَريك : Meaning partners that cannot be separated from each other. Partnership could be material or meaningful. Material in the situation when two persons are partners in a business & the absence of causes disruption in the business. In other words business cannot be carried on if one partner is absent.

In this context, Allah says: 17/111: Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء

وَقُلِ الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي لَمْ يَتَّخِذْ وَلَدًا وَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُ شَرِيكٌ فِي الْمُلْكِ وَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُ وَلِيٌّ مِّنَ الذُّلِّ ۖ وَكَبِّرْهُ تَكْبِيرًا

And say, "Praise to Allah , who has not taken a son and has had no partner in [His] dominion and has no [need of a] protector out of weakness; and glorify Him with [great] glorification."

 

 

& the example of  meaningful partnership would be between horses & humans. They both share the characteristic of  beastliness.( حيوانيت). Allah is devoid of these characteristics. As has been proved by many aayats.

3.        Andaadan اَندادً : (its the plural of nidd  نِدّ ) . It means to detest/hate  & run away. Naadah  نَدّ means he opposed..It also means to be a partner in someones quality or personality. So Nidd  نِدّ  does not mean partner, but actually means opponent. Meaning , some or all qualities of one are present in the other/others.(Nazeer نظير -means example/misaal  مثال). Means opponent as well as competitor.

 

Allah says in the Quran : (2/22) : Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

الَّذِي جَعَلَ لَكُمُ الْأَرْضَ فِرَاشًا وَالسَّمَاءَ بِنَاءً وَأَنزَلَ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ مَاءً فَأَخْرَجَ بِهِ مِنَ الثَّمَرَاتِ رِزْقًا لَّكُمْ ۖ فَلَا تَجْعَلُوا لِلَّهِ أَندَادًا وَأَنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ

[He] who made for you the earth a bed [spread out] and the sky a ceiling and sent down from the sky, rain and brought forth thereby fruits as provision for you. So do not attribute to Allah equals while you know [that there is nothing similar to Him].

Summary:

 

1.        Khaleet  خَليط  :temporary partner.

2.        Shareeq شَريك : Have complete partnership in something

3.        Nadd  نَدّ : partner in personality or characteristics. Opponent or competitor.
