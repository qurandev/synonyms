14. City/colony :

Madinah مَدِينة , balad  بَلد ,misr مِصر ,diyaar  دِيار ,& qarya قَرية are the words used.

1.        Madinah  مَدِينة: Madan  مَدَن means to live,to start living in  a city. Tamaddan تَمَدَن  means respectful & well mannered. Madinah  مَدِينة is such a city where people live together & follow rules & discipline.

Allah says in the Quran: 36/20 : Surat Yā-Sīn (Ya Sin) - سورة يس

وَجَاءَ مِنْ أَقْصَى الْمَدِينَةِ رَجُلٌ يَسْعَىٰ قَالَ يَا قَوْمِ اتَّبِعُوا الْمُرْسَلِينَ

And there came from the farthest end of the city a man, running. He said, "O my people, follow the messengers.

 

2.        Balad بَلد : All those areas where people live & have a marked surrounding(border)

As per the Quran: 90/1 : Surat Al-Balad (The City) - سورة البلد

لَا أُقْسِمُ بِهَٰذَا الْبَلَدِ

I swear by this city, Makkah –

3.Misr مِصر : means limit/border. (plural-masoor) . They say ishtara fulaan ad daar bemasooreha. Meaning X bought that house with its surroundings(border). Misr مِصر means area which has border. Some scholars are of the opinion that Misr مِصر is such a  city where donations are distributed.& some scholars think that misr مِصر is an area/city with border. It is also a name of a country.

As per the Quran: 2/61 : Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

وَإِذْ قُلْتُمْ يَا مُوسَىٰ لَن نَّصْبِرَ عَلَىٰ طَعَامٍ وَاحِدٍ فَادْعُ لَنَا رَبَّكَ يُخْرِجْ لَنَا مِمَّا تُنبِتُ الْأَرْضُ مِن بَقْلِهَا وَقِثَّائِهَا وَفُومِهَا وَعَدَسِهَا وَبَصَلِهَا ۖ قَالَ أَتَسْتَبْدِلُونَ الَّذِي هُوَ أَدْنَىٰ بِالَّذِي هُوَ خَيْرٌ ۚ اهْبِطُوا مِصْرًا فَإِنَّ لَكُم مَّا سَأَلْتُمْ ۗ وَضُرِبَتْ عَلَيْهِمُ الذِّلَّةُ وَالْمَسْكَنَةُ وَبَاءُوا بِغَضَبٍ مِّنَ اللَّهِ ۗ ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّهُمْ كَانُوا يَكْفُرُونَ بِآيَاتِ اللَّهِ وَيَقْتُلُونَ النَّبِيِّينَ بِغَيْرِ الْحَقِّ ۗ ذَٰلِكَ بِمَا عَصَوا وَّكَانُوا يَعْتَدُونَ

 

And [recall] when you said, "O Moses, we can never endure one [kind of] food. So call upon your Lord to bring forth for us from the earth its green herbs and its cucumbers and its garlic and its lentils and its onions." [Moses] said, "Would you exchange what is better for what is less? Go into [any] settlement and indeed, you will have what you have asked." And they were covered with humiliation and poverty and returned with anger from Allah [upon them]. That was because they [repeatedly] disbelieved in the signs of Allah and killed the prophets without right. That was because they disobeyed and were [habitually] transgressing.

3.        Diyaar  دِيار : (singular- Daar دار. Another plural is duur دور) : Duur دور means something surrounded from all sides.Daar دار means house. Dair   دَير means home of a scholar. & daar دار as per a dwelling  has a wider meaning .& includes colony,town,city & even country. Daarul haub  دَارُالحوب means enemy country. Daar ul Qaraar دارالقرار means hereafter. Daar ud dunniya دارالدنيا  means home in the world.(the whole world) & daaraan داران & daarain  دارين  means this life or hereafter.

As per the Quran: 17/5: Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء

فَإِذَا جَاءَ وَعْدُ أُولَاهُمَا بَعَثْنَا عَلَيْكُمْ عِبَادًا لَّنَا أُولِي بَأْسٍ شَدِيدٍ فَجَاسُوا خِلَالَ الدِّيَارِ ۚ وَكَانَ وَعْدًا مَّفْعُولً

So when the [time of] promise came for the first of them, We sent against you servants of Ours - those of great military might, and they probed [even] into the homes, and it was a promise fulfilled.

 

4.        Qaryaa قَرية : ( plural Quraa قُرى) it means a) Colony & b) residents of the colony. This word can be used individually as well as a group. May the colony be big or small, city or a village ,it is used for all. Some say its only used for a colony.

As per the Quran: 65/8 : Surat Aţ-Ţalāq (The Divorce) - سورة الطلاق

وَكَأَيِّن مِّن قَرْيَةٍ عَتَتْ عَنْ أَمْرِ رَبِّهَا وَرُسُلِهِ فَحَاسَبْنَاهَا حِسَابًا شَدِيدًا وَعَذَّبْنَاهَا عَذَابًا نُّكْرًا

And how many a city was insolent toward the command of its Lord and His messengers, so We took it to severe account and punished it with a terrible punishment.

 At another place: 18/59: Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف

وَتِلْكَ الْقُرَىٰ أَهْلَكْنَاهُمْ لَمَّا ظَلَمُوا وَجَعَلْنَا لِمَهْلِكِهِم مَّوْعِدًا

And those cities - We destroyed them when they wronged, and We made for their destruction an appointed time.

Summary

1.        Madinah مَدِينة : a city where people live & follow rules

2.        Balad  بَلد . That city which has a border

3.        Misrمِصر : city with a border & faction

4.        Diyaar  دِيار : can be used for a city,town,colony,country or the whole world.

5.        Qarya قَرية : big colony or city & its residents.
