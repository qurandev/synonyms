1.        Branch

 

Faraa فَرَعَ  , Shaa’ab شَعَبَ , & afnan   اَفنَان  are the words used in the Quran

1.        Faraa  فَرَعَ :This word means branch & its plural is faroo’o  فروع .( meaning branches) According to Imaam Raghib, that the upper part of anything that comes from the root is called faraa as shajar  فرع الشجر.

Allah says in the Quran : 14/24 : Surat 'Ibrāhīm (Abrahim) - سورة ابراهيم

أَلَمْ تَرَ كَيْفَ ضَرَبَ اللَّهُ مَثَلًا كَلِمَةً طَيِّبَةً كَشَجَرَةٍ طَيِّبَةٍ أَصْلُهَا ثَابِتٌ وَفَرْعُهَا فِي السَّمَاءِ

Have you not considered how Allah presents an example, [making] a good word like a good tree, whose root is firmly fixed and its branches [high] in the sky?

 

This word is used for both matter & meaning. Faroaaee masaael  فروعى مساءل are those masaael (issues) that are based on some other facts & are assumed on those. There are no examples of this in the Quran.

 

2.        Shoab   شَعَبَ: Its the plural of Shoaba  شُعبة . There are two basic things in the meaning of this word. A) Iftaraq  افتراق  or to be separate/to alienate. & b) Ijmaa  اجماع. But this word does not come from dictionary of opposites. It applies to all those things that divide into many branches. For e.g.tashsha’ab un nahar   تَشَعَّبُ النَّهر meaning branches of river or fingers from palm. Shoab  شَعَبَ is also used for a big tribe.

Allah says in the Quran: 77/30 : Surat Al-Mursalāt (The Emissaries) - سورة المرسلات

 

انطَلِقُوا إِلَىٰ ظِلٍّ ذِي ثَلَاثِ شُعَبٍ

Proceed to a shadow [of smoke] having three columns

3.        Afnaan  اَفنَان: (plural of fanan  فنن  ). Fanan   فنن  means a very thick & long branch of a tree. Shajaratunn fannanunn  شَجَرَةٌ فَنَّان means a tree with thick & long branches.

Quran says: 55/48: Surat Ar-Raĥmān (The Beneficent) - سورة الرحمن

 

ذَوَاتَا أَفْنَانٍ

Having [spreading] branches.

Summary:

1.         Faraa  فَرَعَ : Whatever  comes out of something, other than its original, it called it faraa

2.        Shoaba شَعَبَ :When something divides into parts & is still connected to its origin.

3.        Fanan اَفنَان : This refers to a thick & long branch of a tree.
