4 GHUBAAR

[Dust]

Synonyms: GHABARA(غبرة), NAQA’A(نقعا), HABA’A(هباء)

4.1 ghabara(غبرة): meaning haze/mist. The small particles that float in the air after a dust storm. Allah states that:  

________________

Surat Abasa (He Frowned) - سُوۡرَةُ عَبَسَ

80:40-41

وَوُجُوهٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ عَلَيۡہَا غَبَرَةٌۖتَرۡهَقُهَا قَتَرَةٌ

And other faces, on that day, with dust upon them. Veiled in darkness.

________________

4.2 naqa’a (نقعا): meaning dust of the pathway, that dust which is left behind when a fast moving vehicle passes through some place. These dust particles then settle to the ground slowly because of being heavier than air. It is stated in the Quran:

________________

Surat Al-Adiyat (The Chargers) - سُوۡرَةُ العَادیَات

100:4-5

فَوَسَطۡنَ بِهِۦ جَمۡعًا ۖ فَأَثَرۡنَ بِهِۦ نَقۡعً۬ا

Then, therewith, with their trail of dust. Cleaving, as one, the centre (of the foe).

________________

4.3 haba’a(هباء): habwa(هبوة) meaning the thin particles of dust, those thin dust particles that always remain in air and are not even seen. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Al-Furqan (The Standard) - سُوۡرَةُ الفُرقان

25:23

وَقَدِمۡنَآ إِلَىٰ مَا عَمِلُواْ مِنۡ عَمَلٍ۬ فَجَعَلۡنَـٰهُ هَبَآءً۬ مَّنثُورًا

And We shall turn unto the work they did and make it scattered motes.

________________

Summary:

1.GHABARA(غبرة): dust particles that can be seen.

2. NAQA’A(نقعا): those heavier dust particles that a fast moving vehicle leaves behind

3. HABA’A(هباء): those thing dust particles that are always scattered in the air and cannot be seen.

For gharoob, see gharaba, aqala, and waqaba. To become proud/arrogant, see ‘itraana’.
