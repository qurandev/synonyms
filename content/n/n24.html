24 NIGAH

[sight]

Synonyms: BASAR(بصر), TARAF(طرف)

24.1 basar(بصر): meaning eye as well as the function of the eye i.e. sight, vision and viewing. And the process of viewing is made clear through this word. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Qaf  - سُوۡرَةُ قٓ

50:22

فَبَصَرُكَ ٱلۡيَوۡمَ حَدِيدٌ۬

(And unto the evil-doer it is said): Thou wast in heedlessness of this. Now We have removed from thee thy covering, and piercing is thy sight this day.

________________

24.2 taraf (طرف): its real meaning is the edge or limit of something. Tarf-ul-ain(طرف العين) meaning blink of an eye and tarfatu a’in(طرفة عين) meaning that time or duration which occurs during the blink of an eye. Thus in taraf(طرف), the aspect of viewing is not evident but points towards a very less period of time. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Al-Naml (The Ants) - سُوۡرَةُ النَّمل

27:40

أَنَا۟ ءَاتِيكَ بِهِۦ قَبۡلَ أَن يَرۡتَدَّ إِلَيۡكَ طَرۡفُكَ‌ۚ

One with whom was knowledge of the Scripture said: I will bring it thee before thy gaze returneth unto thee. And when he saw it set in his presence, (Solomon) said: This is of the bounty of my Lord, that He may try me whether I give thanks or am ungrateful. Whosoever giveth thanks he only giveth thanks for (the good of) his own soul; and whosoever is ungrateful (is ungrateful only to his own soul's hurt). For lo! my Lord is Absolute in independence, Bountiful.

________________

then this word became a same-meaning word with basar(بصر) because of its excessive usage as tarfun khafiyun(طرف خفي) means angry look and qadiratu-taraf(قصرت طرف) (37:48) meaning the women with lowered gazes.

________________

Summary:

The word BASARA(بصر)is used to depict sight or vision whereas the word TARAF(طرف) points towards the small amount of time taken in the blink of an eye.

(for ‘nigah, nigah daalna’, see ‘daikhna’)
