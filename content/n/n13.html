13 NARAM HONA – KERNA [soften]

Synonyms: LAANA and ALAANA, ADHANA, AAHA, TALATAF

13.1 LAANA (لاَنَ): Means softening. Opposite of LEEN (لِیْن) is KHASHOONAT (خشونت) (crustiness) means something being flexible. QAWLAL-LEENA قَوْلًا لَّيِّنًا  (20:44) means to talk softly.  Thus this word is used both materially and semantically. In the Quran

Surah Az-Zumar (The Troops) - سورة الزمر 39:23

ثُمَّ تَلِينُ جُلُودُهُمْ وَقُلُوبُهُمْ إِلَىٰ ذِكْرِ اللَّهِ

then their skins and their hearts soften up to the remembrance of God.

And ALAANA (اَلاَنَ) means to soften, Allah says in the Quran

Surah Saba' (Sheba) - سورة سبإ 34:10-11

وَأَلَنَّا لَهُ الْحَدِيدَ أَنِ اعْمَلْ سَابِغَاتٍ وَقَدِّرْ فِي السَّرْدِ

And We made the iron soft for him. Saying: "Make you perfect coats of mail, balancing well the rings of chain armour,

13.2 ADHANA (اَدْھَنَ): DUHN (دُھْن) means oil, and extract or fat. ADHANA (اَدْھَنَ) means to soften something by applying oil to it. When used figurative then this means to deceive someone by using sweet talking but having something else in heart. To get loose. To do flattery; hypocrisy. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Qalam (The Pen):سورۃ القلم  68:9

وَدُّوا لَوْ تُدْهِنُ فَيُدْهِنُونَ

They wish that you would soften [in your position], so they would soften [toward you].

13.3 AAHA (اٰہَ): Means to sigh or groan (in pain) in order to express sadness. AWWAHUN (اَوَّاہٌ) means a softhearted person. To lament while a supplication. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al Tawbah (The repentance): سورۃ التوبھ 9:114

إِنَّ إِبْرَاهِيمَ لَأَوَّاهٌ حَلِيمٌ

most surely Ibrahim was very tender-hearted forbearing.

13.4 TALATAF (تَلَطَّفَ): LATF (لَطْفَ) means to have knowledge about small things and take care of them. And to compensate for the negative factors (See MEHRBAN [Kind]). TALATAF (تَلطَفَ) means to take in to account care and soft handedness while performing a task. In the Quran

Surah Al-Kahf (The Cave): سورۃ الکھف 18:19

فَلْيَأْتِكُم بِرِزْقٍ مِّنْهُ وَلْيَتَلَطَّفْ وَلَا يُشْعِرَنَّ بِكُمْ أَحَدًا

so let him bring you provision from it, and let him behave with gentleness, and by no means make your case known to any one:

Summary:

13.1 LAANA: Soft and flexible.

13.2 ADHANA: Flattery; to flatter despite a foul feeling heart.

13.3 AAHA: Soft hearted.

13.4 TALATAF: To take in account care and soft handedness while performing a task.
