41 MUWAFQAT KARNA [compatibility, to adjust accordingly]

Synonyms: WAFFAQA, WATAA’

41.1 WAFFAQA (وَفَّقَ): ALWAFQ (اَلْوَفْق) means harmony and compatibility between two things. WAFFAQA (وَفَّقَ) (TAWFEEQA (توفیقا)) means Allah setting conditions in favor of some task. This word is never used for negative meanings. Allah says in the Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:35

إِن يُرِيدَا إِصْلَاحًا يُوَفِّقِ اللَّهُ بَيْنَهُمَا

 If they both desire reconciliation, Allah will cause it between them

41.2 WATAA’ (وَطَأ): Means to crush something under one’s feet, to trample or tread down. WATAA’-A’LAL-AMAR (وَطَأ عَلَی الْاَمْر) means to make some task’s compatibility easy for oneself (dictionary al Manjad). And this word is used with reference to human, and usually taken in a negative meaning. Allah says in Quran

Surah Al Tawbah (The repentance): سورۃ التوبھ 9:49

إِنَّمَا النَّسِيءُ زِيَادَةٌ فِي الْكُفْرِ ۖ يُضَلُّ بِهِ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا يُحِلُّونَهُ عَامًا وَيُحَرِّمُونَهُ عَامًا لِّيُوَاطِئُوا عِدَّةَ مَا حَرَّمَ اللَّهُ فَيُحِلُّوا مَا حَرَّمَ اللَّهُ

The postponing (of a Sacred Month) is indeed an addition to disbelief: thereby the disbelievers are led astray, for they make it lawful one year and forbid it another year in order to adjust the number of months forbidden by Allah,

Summary:

41.1 WAFFAQA: Means Allah setting conditions in favor of some task for someone (in a positive way)

41.2 WATAA’: Means to make changes in a task as per one’s own requirements, (in a negative way).
