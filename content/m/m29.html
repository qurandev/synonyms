29 MASHWARA KERNA

[to consult]

Synonyms: SHAWARA(شاور), BAYYAT(بيت), TANAAJA(تناجي)(NAJW(نجو)), I’TAMAR(اءتمر)

29.1 shawara(شاور): meaning to consult and with respect to some matter, some people getting together and going through its various aspects and taking each other’s opinion. This word’s use is common. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Al-E-Imran (Family of Imran) - سُوۡرَةُ آل عِمرَان

3:159

وَشَاوِرۡهُمۡ فِى ٱلۡأَمۡرِ‌ۖ فَإِذَا عَزَمۡتَ فَتَوَكَّلۡ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ‌ۚ

It was by the mercy of Allah that thou wast lenient with them (O Muhammad), for if thou hadst been stern and fierce of heart they would have dispersed from round about thee. So pardon them and ask forgiveness for them and consult with them upon the conduct of affairs. And when thou art resolved, then put thy trust in Allah. Lo! Allah loveth those who put their trust (in Him).

Surat An-Nisa (Women) - سُوۡرَةُ النِّسَاء

4:81

فَإِذَا بَرَزُواْ مِنۡ عِندِكَ بَيَّتَ طَآٮِٕفَةٌ۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ غَيۡرَ ٱلَّذِى تَقُولُ‌ۖ

And they say: (It is) obedience; but when they have gone forth from thee a party of them spend the night in planning other than what thou sayest. Allah recordeth what they plan by night. So oppose them and put thy trust in Allah. Allah is sufficient as Trustee.

bayyat(بيت) has also come in the meaning of discourage as in 27:49, but here, that meaning is not under discussion.

Surat Al-Mujadila (The Pleading Woman) - سُوۡرَةُ المجَادلة

58:8

وَيَتَنَـٰجَوۡنَ بِٱلۡإِثۡمِ وَٱلۡعُدۡوَٲنِ وَمَعۡصِيَتِ ٱلرَّسُولِ

Hast thou not observed those who were forbidden conspiracy and afterward returned to that which they had been forbidden, and (now) conspire together for crime and wrongdoing and disobedience toward the messenger? And when they come unto thee they greet thee with a greeting wherewith Allah greeteth thee not, and say within themselves: Why should Allah punish us for what we say? Hell will suffice them; they will feel the heat thereof - a hapless journey's end!

Surat Al-Qasas (The Story) - سُوۡرَةُ القَصَص

28:20

قَالَ يَـٰمُوسَىٰٓ إِنَّ ٱلۡمَلَأَ يَأۡتَمِرُونَ بِكَ لِيَقۡتُلُوكَ

And a man came from the uttermost part of the city, running. He said: O Moses! Lo! the chiefs take counsel against thee to slay thee; therefor escape. Lo! I am of those who give thee good advice.

________________

Summary:

1. SHAWARA(شاور): it is common, to consult.

2. BAYYAT(بيت): secret consultation at night.

3. TANAAJA(تناجي): secret whispering among two or less persons.

4. I’TAMARA(اءتمر): to be in agreement over some consultation or to conspire for murder.
