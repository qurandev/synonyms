42 MORNA [to turn, to fold]

Synonyms: THANAA, LAWAA

42.1 THANAA (ثَنٰی): ITHNAIN (اِثنَیْن) means two, THANI (ثَانی) means second, and THANAA (ثَنٰی) (YATHNAA (یَثْنِی)) means to turn or fold it to double it (dictionary al Manjad). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Hud (Hud): سورۃ ھود 11:5

أَلَا إِنَّهُمْ يَثْنُونَ صُدُورَهُمْ

No doubt! They did fold up their breasts, that they may hide from Him

42.2 LAWAA (لوٰی): LAW-AL-JABAL (لوی الجبل) to twine a rope, LAWAA YADAH (لوی یدہ) means he twisted his hand. And LIYAN-BIDAENIHI (لِیًّا بِدَیْنِهِ) means procrastination in paying back a loan. LIWAA’ (لِوَاء) means a flag (that flutters around). AL-ALWAA’ (الالواء) means a turn in valley (dictionary al Manjad). Allah says in the Quran

 

Surah Ale- Imran (The family of Imran): سورۃ آل عِمْرن 3:78

وَإِنَّ مِنْهُمْ لَفَرِيقًا يَلْوُونَ أَلْسِنَتَهُم بِالْكِتَابِ

And among them are those who twist the Scripture with their tongues, 

Summary:

42.1 THANAA: To fold something to double it.

42.2 LAWAA: To twine or twist something.
