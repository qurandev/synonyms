Rivals of messangers-58

The dictinory definition of hareef/ حَريف is confrere(A fellow member of a fraternity or profession).however this word can also be applied even if there is similarity between any one aspect. Whenever messengers presented invitation of deen( deen and oness of ALLAh) they were called as magician, soothsayer, poet and insane. We here want to see the main difference between the job of messengers and all these people.

1)saher ساحر means magician :prophets and messengers ,sometimes from their own wish ,sumtimes on demand of people and sometimes because of an urgent requirement were given miracles from Allah. after seeing the miracles the unbelievers used to call them magicians .

Allah says:

اقْتَرَبَتِ السَّاعَةُ وَانشَقَّ الْقَمَرُ وَإِن يَرَوْا آيَةً يُعْرِضُوا وَيَقُولُوا سِحْرٌ مُّسْتَمِرٌّ

(surah 54 al qamar verse 1-2)

Transaltion: The Hour has drawn near, and the moon has been cleft asunder, And if they see a sign, they turn away, and say: "This is continuous magic.

After the first verse in which the miracle of splitting of moon is told in the second verse it is mentioned that it is the job of unbelievers whenever they see a miracle they immediately call it magic. So it is important to understand the difference between magic and miracle.

The main difference between them is:

1) The substance/essence and reality of things does not change in magic. Magic works on the eyes of viewer and they see things what magicians want them to view. When competing with Mosa (a.s) the magicians turned their sticks and ropes into snakes ALLAH says for them.

فَلَمَّا أَلْقَوْا سَحَرُوا أَعْيُنَ النَّاسِ وَاسْتَرْهَبُوهُمْ وَجَاءُوا بِسِحْرٍ عَظِيمٍ

(surah 7 al al aaraaf verse 116)

Translation: “Throw you (first)” So when they threw, they bewitched the eyes of the people, and struck terror into them, and they displayed a great magic.

Whereas, in miracle the substance (essence) and reality of thing is changed.for example the stick of prophet mosa (a.s) used to become a snake when thrown and comes to its original state when held in hand.

2) the newly created things from miracles stays in the same states as it is created like zam zam and the 12 springs or it comes to its original state like splitting of moon, stick of mosa and the white hand of mosa (when mosa showed his hand it was shinning bright ). a miracle cannot be finished or destroyed but magic can be destroyed for example the snake of all the magicians were eaten by the stick(snake) of mosa.

In quran word miracle is not used instead ayah اٰية ayah bayyinahاٰية بينة,,mubsarah مبصرة ,and burhan برهان are used.the way how quran is explained its way of speech and its context explain that the word is used in its original meaning or it means miracle. And sometime among all these words none is used only from the way of explanation from the speech it is understood that its talking about a miracle for example سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي أَسْرَىٰ(Surah 17 AL ISRAA ,verse 1)”in this subhan ALLAh is a word of interjection to explain that the journey of isra wal miraj was not a spiritual journey but was actually a physical journey and thus it was a miracle.

3) Magic is an art that can be acquired. But miracle cannot be acquired and gained. This was the reason that the magician who were on the heights of talent, when came to compete with mosa realized that what mosa has presented is unique then art and that’s why they accepted the true deen.

4) The nature and character of magician is disliked or offensive(detestable) and people wants to be saved from their evil and that’s why they scare from them. but the life of messengers are extremely pure and pious and for this reason they are respected and loved by people.

2) Kahiin soothsayer :messengers also tells the news of coming future and soothsayers also predicts the future. because of this messengers were blamed as soothsayers. The difference between both of them is the source of messenger prediction is from Allah swt and to prove it wrong is impossible. For example at the time of prophet the Romans got defeated and through revelation it was told that within 3 to 9 years they will again be conquerors .although there was no such hope for it. whereas the soothsayer gets news from ghosts and evil spirits who hear the news from the seventh sky and add some lies and truth in it and then give to the soothsayers, so sometimes their news are correct and sometimes their news are wrong.

another difference between messenger and soothsayer is that a women can be a soothsayer but not a prophtess.1 lakh and 24 thousand messengers came but none among them was a female even bibi maryum was not a prophetess. Soothsayer man/ women were given religious respect. And people asked them to take decision in their difficult matters and due to the respect given to them their decisions were accepted as the final decision. In the time of prophet ibn e siaad was one such soothsayer and he is mentioned in saheeh ahadees.instead of scarifying a human life a sacrifice of 100 camels was also a trend settled by soothsayers which was also continued even in Islam.

3)shayer poet: it’s the job of a poet that in an eloquent and justified language describes his thinking and point of view in such a way that his /her listeners are left out in trance. Although the verses in Quran are without prosodic rules ( behr and wazan).but rest all the qualities are present in Quran at its extreme which is not possible in a human speech. Due to this similarity prophet was also called poet. The difference between a poet and messenger is that the imagination of a poet is inspired by the good and bad aspects of life. Poet is influenced by Environment, and the majority of society is misguided so is the imagination of poet.

ALLAH SAYS:

وَالشُّعَرَاءُ يَتَّبِعُهُمُ الْغَاوُونَ أَلَمْ تَرَ أَنَّهُمْ فِي كُلِّ وَادٍ يَهِيمُونَ

(surah 26 as shuaara,verse 224-225)

Translation: As for the poets, the erring ones follow them, See you not that they speak about every subject (praising people - right or wrong) in their poetry?

Whereas, prophet imagination is according to the revelation of Allah and the second difference is, the point of view of a poet keeps on changing as per his/her age or mental level and experiences grows. But whatever a messenger says will always be in favor of his previous talks. The blames of unbelievers were rejected by Allah in Quran:

وَمَا هُوَ بِقَوْلِ شَاعِرٍ ۚ قَلِيلًا مَّا تُؤْمِنُونَ وَلَا بِقَوْلِ كَاهِنٍ ۚ قَلِيلًا مَّا تَذَكَّرُونَ

(surah 69 al haqqah,verse41 and 42)

Transalation: It is not the word of a poet: little it is ye believe! Nor is it the word of a soothsayer: little admonition it is ye receive.

Because of these problems prophet (sal lal la ho alhi wa salim )was not taught poetry.even though prophet was the most eloquent/polite among all Arabs and non Arabs. Even after all these things it is wrong to say that all the poets and poetry is cursed. The imagination of poet can be on right direction. prophet (sal lal la ho alhi wa salim ) in times of iqnorance liked the poetry of Labeedah(may ALLAh be pleased with him) as this poetry was depicting the message of ALLAH.

اَلاَكُلُّ شَىْءٍمَا خلاَالّلٰهَ باطِلُ

وكُلُّ نَعيمٍ لَّامَحَلَةَ زاءلُ

Transaltion:

Know that other then Allah all things are invalid

And all the blessings without doubt will be vanished

4) insane: means unsound mind, a person who does not use mind (aqal pay pardah par janaa).and he/she talks insanely. People of Arab use to call insane (majnoon) because according to their mentality ghost enters into the body of the person and make him/her insane because of which they talk inordinately or talk insanely. The teachings of prophets are not suitable to unbelievers thts y they give them title of insane.

Allah says :

كَذَٰلِكَ مَا أَتَى الَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِهِم مِّن رَّسُولٍ إِلَّا قَالُوا سَاحِرٌ أَوْ مَجْنُونٌ

(surah 51ad dhariyaat,verse 52)

Transaltion: Similarly, no messenger came to the Peoples before them, but they said (of him) in like manner, "A sorcerer, or one possessed.

The root difference between a prophet and an insane person is that even though the teaching of prophet is against the society and trends of society but the prophet stays on his path and act accordingly. And from his purity, good nature and character proves himself to be right.Where as an insane person does not possess any of the above qualities.
