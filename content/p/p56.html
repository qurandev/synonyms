Forehead-56

Synonyms are:

1) جَبيْن

2) جبه

3) ناصية

1)Jabeen جَبيْن :anyone corner of forhead is called jabeen Munjid - منجدor both sides (left and right) of forehead are called as ajbeeyan اجبيَان

Allah says

فَلَمَّا أَسْلَمَا وَتَلَّهُ لِلْجَبِينِ

(suran 37 as-saafaat verse 103)

Transaltion: So when they had both submitted their wills (to Allah), and he had laid him prostrate on his forehead (for sacrifice),

2) jabah جبه :means the width of forehead and its beauty Munjid - منجد. Jabah actually is the front side of forehead which touches the ground at the time of sajdaah(prostration).the center between the two sides of forehead and its plural is jeebaah جِباه.

ALLAH says in quran:

فَتُكْوَىٰ بِهَا جِبَاهُهُمْ وَجُنُوبُهُمْ وَظُهُورُهُمْ ۖ

(surah 9 at tawbah, verse 35)

Transaltion: and with it will be branded their foreheads, their flanks, and their backs.

3) nasiaa ناصية menas forehead or hair on forehead if they are long Munjid - منجد.this word can be used as for hairs on forehead or for forehead itself and its plural is nawasii نَواصى.

Allah says in quran

يُعْرَفُ الْمُجْرِمُونَ بِسِيمَاهُمْ فَيُؤْخَذُ بِالنَّوَاصِي وَالْأَقْدَامِ

(surah 55 ar rahman,verse 41)

Translation: (For) the sinners will be known by their marks: and they will be seized by their forelocks and their feet.

SUMMARY

1)jabeen :its anyone side of forehead the left or right side of forehead.

2)jabaah :the center of forehead.

3)nasiaa:forehead and its hair.
