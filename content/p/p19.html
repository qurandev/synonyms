To hold

اخذ، بطش، تناوش، قبض ،خطف، سطا (سطو)، إعتصم، إستمسك (مسك)، درك all these terms come under the canopy of holding or catching something.

1.اخذ it’s common for holding or taking something. Mean to get something and to surround/encompass it. This word can be used in both metaphorically and literally. Ref literal meaning (20:94). Ref metaphorical meaning (2:93).

2.بطشto hold something with strength and upper handedly. Or to hold powerfully/clench, to take something forcefully. Means tough and strong grip. To hold with scaring toughness. Like اخذ this word can also be used in dual meanings. Ref (26:130).

3.تناوش ناش to hold something on an arm stretch length. To ask for something/ to quest for something. تناوشto get access to the required object. Ref (34:52).

4.قبض to hold something in fist. قبضة implies on fist. Ref (20:96).

5.خطف to get something and leave quickly/wrench away/snatch. Ref (22:31).

6.سطا to attack somebody and take over him/ usurp. إمام راغب says سطا means to hold someone powerfully while attacking. سطوةis البطش بر فع اليدto takeover someone by beating. Sway/controlling influence. Ref (22:72).

7.إعتصم (عصم) to save something. إعتصم to hold tightly with palms/clutch/cling and to claw. Ref (3:103).

8.إستمسك to cling to/ to adhere to. To seize/halt and to stick to. تمسّك , إستمسكto hold on to or to grasp powerfully and not let it go. (مُنجد). Ref (2:265).

9.دركto take over from the back. Rock bottom can also be called so. (36:40).

Summary: اخذ general for, to hold. بطش firm and tough grip. تناوش to hold something placed far. قبض to hold/grab in fist. خطف to grasp and leave hastily/ wrench away. سطاto hold like attacking. إعتصمto clutch. إستمسك to cling to/ to hold on to. درك to take over from the back.
