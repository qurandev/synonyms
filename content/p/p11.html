To regret

ندم, حسِرَ, سُقِطَ فِي يَدِيه are the words which mean to regret/ contrite about.

1.ندم to regret/rue over your bad deeds and its usage is general. Ref (5:52)

2.حسِرَ :Author of فِقه إللُغة says that its شدّ الندَامة means the severity in ruefulness/regret. But author of المُنجِد opines حسِرَ and حسرة is to regret. Ref (6:31)

Page 275

3.سُقِطَ فِي يَدِيه is an idiom means to be embarrassed after being amiss or after being erroneous. (7:149)

Summary:

ندم to regret over one’s own bad deed. حسِرَ to be severely regretful and then express regret. سُقِطَ فِي يَدِيه to be ashamed/ embarrassed over being erroneous.
