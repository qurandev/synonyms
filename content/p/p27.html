To Know (Paihchanna # 27) page 296

 

 

Synonym:

  1. Arafa:   عَرَفَ
  2. Tawassam: تَوَسِّمِ

1. Arafa: عَرَفَ

  1. Opposite of  'Nakira'
  2. Arafa means 'to know' a thing in general
  3. to recognize by its basic signs, its given Names
  4. Maroof is something that is Known and Accepted in the society; the Norm.

[12:58]  Then came Joseph's brethren: they entered his presence, and he knew them, but they knew him not.

 وَجَآءَ إِخْوَةُ يُوسُفَ فَدَخَلُوا۟ عَلَيْهِ فَعَرَفَهُمْ وَهُمْ لَهُۥ مُنكِرُونَ ﴿٥٨

2. Tawassam: تَوَسِّمِ

  1. wasama= to brand
  2. wasm/wissam = something that is branded
  3. brand-name labelled
  4. wasim is a beautiful, sought after brand
  5. Tawassam is to list something by its qualities
  6. to recognize a really good brand by its label

 [15:75]  Surely in that are signs for such who recognize as marked;

 إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَـَٔايَـٰتٍ لِّلْمُتَوَسِّمِينَ ﴿٧٥
