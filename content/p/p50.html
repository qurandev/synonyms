Staying behind:

Synonyums are:

1) تَاخَرَ

2) خَلَفَ

3) غَبَرَ

1)تَاخَرَ takhrra: staying behind with own will.purposley staying behind.

Allah says in quran :

فَمَن تَعَجَّلَ فِي يَوْمَيْنِ فَلَا إِثْمَ عَلَيْهِ وَمَن تَأَخَّرَ فَلَا إِثْمَ عَلَيْهِ

(surah 2 baqrah,verse 203)

Translation: But if any one hastens to leave in two days, there is no blame on him, and if any one stays on, there is no blame on him.

2) خَلَفَ kahlafa to stay behind becoz of a mistake or loss Mufraddat - مفرداتby Imam Raghib =(مف) another meaning of this word is to oppose and khalif خَلِف means the one who stays behind because of his/her own laziness and inefficiency Mufraddat - مفرداتby Imam Raghib =(مف).and خالف khalif meansstupid the one who stay behind after one person is goneMunjid - منجد- Dictionary =(منجد).

Allah says in quran:

إِنَّكُمْ رَضِيتُم بِالْقُعُودِ أَوَّلَ مَرَّةٍ فَاقْعُدُوا مَعَ الْخَالِفِينَ

(surah 9 at tawbah,verse 83)

Transaltion: surely you chose to sit the first time, therefore sit (now) with those who remain behind.

And the feminie of kalif خَلِف is خَلِفَةٌ khilaftoon and usually this word is used for women who stay behind or the women staying behind at their homes at the time of jehad (A war or struggle against unjust unbelievers).And the plural for this is khawaliff خَوَلِف Mufraddat - مفرداتby Imam Raghib =(مف).

Allah says in quran

رَضُوا بِأَن يَكُونُوا مَعَ الْخَوَالِفِ

(Surah 9 at tawbah ,verse 87)

Transaltion: They prefer to be with (the women), who remain behind (at home).

2)ghabra غَبَرَto stay behind from once companions or partners. Mufraddat - مفرداتby Imam Raghib =(مف).

Allah says in quran:

فَأَنجَيْنَاهُ وَأَهْلَهُ إِلَّا امْرَأَتَهُ كَانَتْ مِنَ الْغَابِرِينَ

(surah 7 al aaraaf,verse 83)

Translation: So we delivered him and his followers, except his wife; she was of those who remained behind.

Summary:

Takharaa:to stay behind with own will and desire khalafa to stay behind because of laziness or some fault.

Ghabara to stay behind from ones companions becoz of being faulty.
