To delay or move behind-51

Synonyms are

1) اَخَّرَ

2) ارْجٰي (رجو)

3) اَرجهَ

4) نَساَ

1)akhra :to delay or postpone something from its fixed time (Munjid - منجد - Dictionary - (Arabic to Urdu)=(منجد) (Mufraddat - مفرداتby Imam Raghib =(مف).)the use of this word is usual

Allah says in quran

وَلَن يُؤَخِّرَ اللَّهُ نَفْسًا إِذَا جَاءَ أَجَلُهَا

(surah 63 al munaffiqoun,verse 11)

Transaltion: But to no soul will Allah grant respite when the time appointed (for it) has come.

2)arjaa ارْجٰي : رجَاء means where there is a hope of getting pleasure (Mufraddat - مفرداتby Imam Raghib =(مف).)and the opposite of it is khoof خوف means scared/frighten .raja رَجاء and khoof خوف are correlative For example when there is hope of getting sumthing that is desired there will be threat of losing that thing. and similarly in opposite situation with threats there is hope as well. andarjaa ارْجي means to put aside or delay from a thing from which there is a hope of getting pleasure Allah says in Quran :

تُرْجِي مَن تَشَاءُ مِنْهُنَّ وَتُؤْوِي إِلَيْكَ مَن تَشَاءُ ۖ

(surah 33 al ahzab,51)

Translation: You, [O Muhammad], may put aside whom you will of them or take to yourself whom you will. (usmani)

3)arjahaaha اَرجهَ :rajhaa menas to hold sumthing wid teeth’s and arjahaa al amaar اَرجهَ الامر means to put something on delay Munjid - منجد - Dictionary - (Arabic to Urdu)=(منجد). Means the reason behind the delay in work is to be careful and to work with concern so that one can get expected result. Allah says in quran.

قَالُوا أَرْجِهْ وَأَخَاهُ وَأَرْسِلْ فِي الْمَدَائِنِ

(surah 7 al ahzaab verse 111)

Translation: They said: "Keep him and his brother in suspense (for a while); and send to the cities men to collect.

4) نَساَ nasaa:to delay something . Arabic proverb نَساَكَ الّلهُ في اَجَلِك means Allah give u a long life(Mufraddat - مفرداتby Imam Raghib =(مف).) and nasiaaah نَسيئَةَ means,delay or loan for example in buying and selling to pay the money later .in Quran the word is نَسيئُ nasioo is used in terms of (taqdeem and takheer) Miraatul Quraan – مراة القران (Arabic to Urdu)=(م-ق)hurry and delay and in reality this is what it means .

Meaning to make hurry in one thing and in other there is a delay for example the unbelievers of Makah used to do like this …’they use to make delay and hurry in the days of hajj accordingly with the weather so that their crops get ready to reap and then it will be easy for them to pay gifts to the servants of kabbah “ Allah forbids this kind of hurry and delay in Quran and said :

إِنَّمَا النَّسِيءُ زِيَادَةٌ فِي الْكُفْرِ ۖ يُضَلُّ بِهِ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا يُحِلُّونَهُ عَامًا وَيُحَرِّمُونَهُ عَامًا

(surah 9 al ahzaab,verse 37)

Transaltion: Postponing (of the sacred month) is only an addition in unbelief, wherewith those who disbelieve are led astray, violating it one year and keeping it sacred another

Summary:

1)Akhara the use of this word is usual.

2)Arjaa: a delay in which there is expectation of getting pleasure.to postpone because of being hopeful.

3)Arjahaa:to delay because of precautions so that the task accomplish correctly.

4)Nasiaa: to hurry in one thing and delay the other thing.
