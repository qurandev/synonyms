Leaving behind-49 (NB: wrongly marked 45 in book)

Synonyms are

1) اَخَّرَ

2) خَلَّفَ

1) اَخَّرَ akhra:takheraan تَاخيراً (DELAY) opposite is taqdeem تقديم (PRIOR).used as adverb of time .it means leaving behind or moving sumthing behind. This word is used as in terms of delaying task (to delay the work)and if it is related to sumone else than it means reprieve (to give time/to give chance).

Allah says in quran:

يُنَبَّأُ الْإِنسَانُ يَوْمَئِذٍ بِمَا قَدَّمَ وَأَخَّرَ

(surah 75 al qiyamah,verse 13)

Translation: That Day will Man be told (all) that he put forward, and all that he put back.

2) خَلَّفَ khlafa : it means leaving behin. When adverb of time and location both are related this word is used to describe the situation.

Allah says in quran :

وَعَلَى الثَّلَاثَةِ الَّذِينَ خُلِّفُوا

(surah 9 at tawbah,verse 118)

Translation:

a)and upon those three whom matter was delayed(Fathul Hameed فتح الحميد د(translation is by Moulana Fath Mohammad Jhaalandari.)

to the three who were left behind.Tafseer - Shabbir Ahmed Uthmani تفسير شبير احمد عثماني =(عثماني)

Summary:

akhraa only used as adverd of time and khalafaah can be adverb of time or location both at the same time
