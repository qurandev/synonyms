First, Foremost(Pahla, pahli, pahle # 28 ) page 296

Synonym:

  1.  Awwal:لْأَوَّلِينَ
  2. Saqib:سابق
  3. Qabal: قَبْلِكَ

1. Awwal: لْأَوَّلِينَ

 

  1. First
  2. Feminine form is Oola
  3. Used as a
  1. noun: a person or thing that is  'First' in order, rank or time
  2. adjective: this book is the First edition
  3. adverb: former

56:49] Proclaim, “Without doubt all – the former and the latter.”

 

أَوَءَابَآؤُنَا ٱلْأَوَّلُونَ ﴿٤٨﴾قُلْ إِنَّ ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ وَٱلْـَٔاخِرِينَ ﴿٤٩﴾

2. Sabaq:سَبَقُ

  1. sabaqa means to come first and leave the rest behind
  2. sabiq: first in a race
  3. pioneer, foremost

[59:10]  And those who came after them say: "Our Lord! Forgive us, and our brethren who came before us into the Faith, and leave not, in our hearts, rancour (or sense of injury) against those who have believed. Our Lord! Thou art indeed Full of Kindness, Most Merciful."

 وَٱلَّذِينَ جَآءُو مِنۢ بَعْدِهِمْ يَقُولُونَ رَبَّنَا ٱغْفِرْ لَنَا وَلِإِخْوَٰنِنَا ٱلَّذِينَ سَبَقُونَا بِٱلْإِيمَـٰنِ وَلَا تَجْعَلْ فِى قُلُوبِنَا غِلًّا لِّلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ رَبَّنَآ إِنَّكَ رَءُوفٌ رَّحِيمٌ ﴿١٠

3. Qabal:قَبْلِكَ

  1. opposite of ba'ad
  2. 'before' in respect to time, sequence, order, preference, importance
  3. for example: First I will rest, then I'll come.
  4. for the first time: She first visited New York in December.

[2:4] And who believe in that which is revealed unto thee (Muhammad) and that which was revealed before you, and are certain of the Hereafter.

 وَٱلَّذِينَ يُؤْمِنُونَ بِمَآ أُنزِلَ إِلَيْكَ وَمَآ أُنزِلَ مِن قَبْلِكَ وَبِٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ هُمْ يُوقِنُونَ ﴿٤﴾
