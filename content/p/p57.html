Messenger-57

Synonyms are:

1) نَبى

2) رَسُول (مُرْسِلْ)

3) مَلٰئِكة

1-2)Nabii نَبى , rasool and mursaal رَسُول (مُرْسِلْ) : PROPHET nabii نَبى derivates from the root word nabba نبا which means news. And it means to receive the news of unknown/unseen from ALLAH through revelation. And prophet hood is attributed to the prophet.

The dictionary definition of rasool رَسُول is messenger and that can be anyone. but according to Religious Terminology or Islamic Jurisprudence terminology messenger is the one who through divine revelation and inspiration receive the news of unknown and receive orders and rulings and then deliver them to others. Risallat رسالت messengership is not attributed to messenger instead it is attributed to Allah . Only then a rasool is called a mursail مُرسل when he delivers the message to others. Whereas he is called a rasool ( messenger) at the time when the message is handed over to him Alfurooqul-lughwiyyah - الفروق اللغوية 223.

Some people think there is no difference between nabii and rasool. They say it is two sides of the same coin. And their logic is, that the people Allah referred as in nabi in Quran, most of them are also called rasool as well. But in actual there is a difference between both

And prove/explanation of this is given in the verse below:

وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَا مِن قَبْلِكَ مِن رَّسُولٍ وَلَا نَبِيٍّ إِلَّا إِذَا تَمَنَّىٰ أَلْقَى الشَّيْطَانُ فِي أُمْنِيَّتِهِ

(surah 22 hajj,verse 52)

Transaltion: Never did We send a messenger or a prophet before thee, but, when he framed a desire, Satan threw some (vanity) into his desire.

From this verse it is clearly mentioned that these two are two separate terms and the root difference between both of them is as follow:

1) before arrival of a prophet the news of his arrival is delivered to the already present messengers and they announce it.but for a nabii(messenger) it is not important (he does not do like this).

2)every rasool /messenger brings new religious jurisprudence and built a new nation but nabii/prophet is sent upon to the nation of rasool prior to him so that he can do the corrections in the ruined up teachings of that rasool and to make corrections in the character of that nation.

3) from the evil of people rasools/messengers are protected by Allah but anbiaa prophets( اَنبيَاء plural of nabi) were killed in past unjustly.

3)maliikaah مَلٰئِكة : the root of this is laak(لأك )” اَلاَكةُ الئٰ فلان “ means deliver my massage to him/her.

“اَلكني الئٰ فلانٍ “ means give my message to him and ملْاَك malaak or مَلَك malak

means angel who delivers messages Munjid - منجد.malik مَلَك means angel and its plural is malika مَلٰئِكة is also used a Islamic jurisprudence terminology. And it is not necessary that a all the malik مَلَك has to be a messenger. The angel who is assigned to deliver the message of Allah to messengers and prophets (nabi and rasool) is jabrail (a.s),ALLAH mentioned him with the title of Rooh (SPIRIT) and also as rooh al amen(the trustworthy spirit).however other angels also delivers the messages

as Allah says:

إِذْ قَالَتِ الْمَلَائِكَةُ يَا مَرْيَمُ إِنَّ اللَّهَ يُبَشِّرُكِ بِكَلِمَةٍ مِّنْهُ اسْمُهُ الْمَسِيحُ عِيسَى ابْنُ مَرْيَمَ

(surah 3 ali imran,verse number 45)

Translation: Behold! the angels said: "O Mary! Allah giveth thee glad tidings of a Word from Him: his name will be Christ Jesus, the son of Mary.

Summary:

1)Nabi: prophet is the one who receives the news of unknown/unseen (the hidden knowledge) by Allah through revelation and according to those revelation make correction in prior teachings and character of prior nations .

2)rasool:is the messenger who has been authorized with religious jurisprudence and formulate a new nation and convey Allah’s message to others.

3)malikaa: are the angels that also delivers messages.
