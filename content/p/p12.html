To get old

بَلِيَ, قَدِيم, عَتِيق vocabulary for getting old.

1.بَلِيَOldness/deterioration of any usable thing after long usage. بَلِيَ الثوب means ragged/worn out clothes. Ref (20:120)

2.قَدِيم means to be of old times or to be ancient. Its antonym is جَدِيد. قَدمto move forward and قَدُم is to get old. Ref (12:95)

3.عَتِيقis from العتق means thoroughbred/pureblooded. عَتِيقis a thing which is despite of being old and ancient still has dignity and purity in it. That’s why بيت الله is also called as بيت العتِيق. Ref (22:29)
