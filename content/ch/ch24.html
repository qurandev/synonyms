24.  CHUPANA      [ to hide something]

SYNONYMS: KATAMA, AWRAA and WARAA, AKANNA, AKHFAA, ASARRA, KHABA’A

24.1  KATAMA (كَتَمَ) : to consciously hide something in the heart. This word is commonly used for hiding the truth, faith or martyrdom. Its opposite is اَبَدَ

_______________________________________

Surah Al-Baqarah (The Cow)

2:140

وَمَنْ أَظْلَمُ مِمَّن كَتَمَ شَهَادَةً عِندَهُ مِنَ اللّهِ

And who is more unjust than he who conceals the testimony he has from Allâh?

______________________________________

Surah Al-Baqarah (The Cow)

2:159

إِنَّ الَّذِينَ يَكْتُمُونَ مَا أَنزَلْنَا مِنَ الْبَيِّنَاتِ

Verily, those who conceal the clear proofs, evidences and the guidance, which We have sent down.

_______________________________________

Surah Ghafir  (The Forgiver or The Believer)

40:28

وَقَالَ رَجُلٌ مُّؤْمِنٌ مِّنْ آلِ فِرْعَوْنَ يَكْتُمُ إِيمَانَهُ

And a believing man of Fir'aun's (Pharaoh) family, who hid his faith said.

_______________________________________

24.2  WAARAA (وَارٰى) : the root meaning of وَارٰى is used in two ways. 1) to kindle or light a fire. He made the fire come out from pyrites. 2) to hide the truth. Here only the second meaning is relevant.

_______________________________________

Surah Al-A'raf (The Heights)

7:20

فَوَسْوَسَ لَهُمَا الشَّيْطَانُ لِيُبْدِيَ لَهُمَا مَا وُورِيَ عَنْهُمَا مِن سَوْءَاتِهِمَا

Then Shaitân (Satan) whispered suggestions to them both in order to uncover that which was hidden from them of their private parts.

_______________________________________

And يُوَارِىْ وَارٰى means to remove the truth from the sight of people by hiding it behind a veil.

_______________________________________

Surah Al-Ma'idah (The Table Spread with Food)

5:31

فَبَعَثَ اللّهُ غُرَابًا يَبْحَثُ فِي الأَرْضِ لِيُرِيَهُ كَيْفَ يُوَارِي سَوْءةَ أَخِيهِ

Then Allâh sent a crow who scratched the ground to show him to hide the dead body of his brother.

_______________________________________

24.3  AKANNA (اَكَنَّ) : according to its root meaning, two things are found: (1) to hide (2) protection, or to place something in a safe place to protect it. To hide something in the heart or to hide it somewhere else. كنّ means a small cave in a mountain where refuge can be found, and its plural is اكنان . And اكنّة is a veil, in the presence of which nothing can come out, or come inside.

_______________________________________

Surah Al-An'am (The Cattle)

6:25

وَجَعَلْنَا عَلَى قُلُوبِهِمْ أَكِنَّةً أَن يَفْقَهُوهُ

We have set veils on their hearts, so they understand it not.

_______________________________________

The word اَكَنَّ is commonly used in the Qur’an in an implied form:

Surah Al-Baqarah (The Cow)

2:235

وَلاَ جُنَاحَ عَلَيْكُمْ فِيمَا عَرَّضْتُم بِهِ مِنْ خِطْبَةِ النِّسَاء أَوْ أَكْنَنتُمْ فِي أَنفُسِكُمٌ

And there is no sin on you if you make a hint of betrothal to a woman, or conceal it in yourself.

________________________________________

And for this safe place, the words قلوب صدور نفس and نفوس are used in the Qur’an.

________________________________________

24.4  ASARRA (اَسَرَّ) : سِرّ means a secret, and its plural is اسرار . And اَسَرَّ means to hide a secret in the heart, whether it is the truth or not. Instead, it is hidden ******************. Its opposites are جهر and عَلَن. And سارَّ means to whisper or to discuss a secret with each other. In the Qur’an is word is used both in its apparent and implied form.

______________________________________

Surah Yusuf (Prophet Joseph)

12:19

قَالَ يَا بُشْرَى هَذَا غُلاَمٌ وَأَسَرُّوهُ بِضَاعَةَ

He said: "What good news! Here is a boy." So they hid him as merchandise (a slave).

____________________________________

Surah At-Tahrim (The Prohibition)

وَإِذْ أَسَرَّ النَّبِيُّ إِلَى بَعْضِ أَزْوَاجِهِ حَدِيثًا

And (remember) when the Prophet (SAW) disclosed a matter in confidence to one of his wives (Hafsah).
