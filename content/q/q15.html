15 QUWWAT DAINA [to empower, strengthen]

Synonyms: AYYAD, AAZARA, AZZAZA

15.1 AYYAD (اَیَّدَ): AL-AID (الاید) means intense power. And AYYAD (اَیَّدَ) means to help someone tremendously, to strengthen, to aid. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al Tawbah (The repentance): سورۃ التوبھ 9:40

فَأَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ سَڪِينَتَهُ ۥ عَلَيۡهِ وَأَيَّدَهُ ۥ بِجُنُودٍ۬ لَّمۡ تَرَوۡهَا

God made His tranquility descend upon him, and supported him with forces you did not see

15.2 AAZARA (اٰزَرَ): AL-IZR (اَلْاِزْر) means root or TEHBAND (تہبند) (a cloth wrapped around the bottom half). AL-IZAAR (اَلْاِزَار) means a shawl, tehband, a drape or quay. AAZAR-AL-BANAAT (اٰزَرَالْبَنَات) means intertwining of plants. AZZARA (اَزَّرَ) means to strengthen, or to give power (dictionary Al Manjad). Thus AAZARA (اٰزَرَ) means  something giving itself power. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Fath (The victory): سورۃ الفَتْح  48:29

كَزَرۡعٍ أَخۡرَجَ شَطۡـَٔهُ ۥ فَـَٔازَرَهُ ۥ فَٱسۡتَغۡلَظَ فَٱسۡتَوَىٰ عَلَىٰ سُوقِهِ

 Like a plant that sprouts, becomes strong, grows thick, and rests on its stem, 

 

15.3 AZZAZA (عَزَّزَ): the opposite of AZZA (عَزَّ) is DALLA (ذَلَّ). AZZ (عَزَّ) means to be superior, AAZZA (اَعَزَّ) means to honor and AZZAZA (عَزَّزَ) means to empower or strengthen an inferior so much so that he becomes a noble and dignified person. Dignity means a state which saves a man from humiliation and inferiority (dictionary Al Manjad). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Yaseen (Yaseen): سورۃ ٰیٰس 36:14

 إِذۡ أَرۡسَلۡنَآ إِلَيۡہِمُ ٱثۡنَيۡنِ فَكَذَّبُوهُمَا فَعَزَّزۡنَا بِثَالِثٍ۬

When We (first) sent to them two messengers, they rejected them: But We strengthened them with a third

Summary:

15.1 AYYADA: help someone tremendously, to strengthen

15.2 AAZARA: Giving power to itself and strengthen

15.3 AZZAZA: Empower or strengthen an inferior so he no longer remains inferior.

Also see MADAD KERNA [to help], and MAZBOOT KARNA [to fortify]
