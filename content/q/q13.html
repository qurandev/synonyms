13 QALA’ [castle, stronghold]

Synonyms: HUSOON, SAYASEE, BUROOJ, MAHAREEB

13.1 HUSOON (حُصُوْن): (singular HISN (حِصْن)) HISN (حِصْن) refers to any place that can give protection, it can be surrounded and where one can take refuge. Castle with barricade so one can take refuge, protect oneself and face the enemy. In the Quran

Surah Al Hashr (The Exile): سورۃ الحَشر 59:2

وَظَنُّوٓاْ أَنَّهُم مَّانِعَتُهُمۡ حُصُونُہُم مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ

while they deemed that their strongholds would protect them from Allah.

 

13.2 SAYASEE (صَیَاصِی): (singular SISIYAH (صَیْصِیَہ)) Anything that is used for protection. The horns of a bull are also called SISIYAH (صَیْصِیَہ) because it is used by the bull to protect itself. Protective place or a castle, anything resembling a castle, the battle strongholds of Jews. Allah says in the Quran

Suran Al-Ahzaab (The Clans): سورۃ الأحزَاب 33:26

 وَأَنزَلَ ٱلَّذِينَ ظَـٰهَرُوهُم مِّنۡ أَهۡلِ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبِ مِن صَيَاصِيهِمۡ وَقَذَفَ فِى قُلُوبِهِمُ ٱلرُّعۡبَ

 And He brought down from their strongholds those of the People of the Book who backed them, and He threw terror into their hearts.

13.3 BUROOJ (بُرُوْج): (singular BURJ (بُرْج)) BARAJA (بَرَجَ) means to appear, to emerge. BARRAJ (بَرَّج) mean to construct a BURJ (بُرْج), and BURJ (بُرْج)means any dome shaped tall building, a castle or a palace. Allah says in the Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:78

 أَيۡنَمَا تَكُونُواْ يُدۡرِككُّمُ ٱلۡمَوۡتُ وَلَوۡ كُنتُمۡ فِى بُرُوجٍ۬ مُّشَيَّدَةٍ۬‌ۗ

"Wherever you are, death will find you out, even if you are in towers built up strong and high!"

 

13.4 MAHAREEB (مَحَارِیْب): (singular MEHRAAB (مَحْرَاب)) MEHRAAB (مَحْرَاب) means the area of entry of a house, an arch, a place for people to gather. Also it means contentious, litigious or brave (dictionary Al Manjad), and place where a battle is planned or battle equipment is made. Therefore can be used to refer to a stronghold. In the Quran

Surah Saba (Sheba): سورۃ سَبَإ 34:13

يَعۡمَلُونَ لَهُ ۥ مَا يَشَآءُ مِن مَّحَـٰرِيبَ وَتَمَـٰثِيلَ

They worked for him as he desired, (making) arches, images,

 

Summary:

13.1 HUSOON: A castle that can be surrounded can be used for protection and refuge.

13.2 SAYASEE: Protective place or a castle, anything resembling a castle, the battle strongholds of Jews.

13.3 BUROOJ: Dome shaped tall building, a castle or a palace

13.4 MAHAREEB: Place where a battle is planned and is safe.
