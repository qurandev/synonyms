5- DOL [Bucket]

 

Qur’an has referred it with the words DALU (دلو ) ZANUB (ذنوب ).

 

5.1   DALU (دلو ) : Pot used to take out water from the well, bucket etc. if it is free of water. It is mentioned in Qur’an:

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

12:19

 وَجَاۗءَتْ سَيَّارَةٌ فَاَرْسَلُوْا وَارِدَهُمْ فَاَدْلٰى دَلْوَهٗ   ۭ

And there came a company of travellers; then they sent their water drawer, and he let down his bucket.

 

5.2   ZANUB (ذنوب ): Pot used to take out water from the well bucket etc. if it is full with water. Allah says:

Surat Adh-Dhāriyāt (The Winnowing Winds) - سورة الذاريات 51:59

 فَاِنَّ لِلَّذِيْنَ ظَلَمُوْا ذَنُوْبًا مِّثْلَ ذَنُوْبِ اَصْحٰبِهِمْ فَلَا يَسْتَعْجِلُوْنِ

And indeed, for those who have wronged their bucket is filled just like their companions.

 

It means that these wrongdoers have also reached in the doldrums and their survival parameter is filled just like the other wrongdoers similar to them.

 

 

Summary:

If the pot is full of water then it is ZANUB (ذنوب ). If it is filled with a little bit of water then it is SIJAL (سجل ) and if it is totally empty then it is DALU (دلو ).
