ڈبونا- to drown- dip

 These words    اغرق  صبغ    have been used in the Quran

1.          اغرق -  غرق- any non-aquatic creature which drowns and dies اغرق to drown someone.

 

In Surah Baqarah: (2:50):

 

وَأَغْرَقْنَا آلَ فِرْعَوْنَ وَأَنتُمْ تَنظُرُونَ

 

and drowned the people of Pharaoh while you were looking on.

 

2.      صبغ  - صبغ يده فى الماء  - to immerse your hands in water, and اصبغت بالخل - I ate chappati dipped in vinegar, صبغ الثوب  - to colour material, to immerse material in coloured water, صبغ (sibg) means colour and curry, and صبغة- means colour and صابغ person who colours, صباغ (sabbaag) means ‘a dyer’(MF, MJD).  This word has been used in the Quran for both meanings.

 

In Surah Baqarah: (2:138):

 

صِبْغَةَ اللَّهِ ۖ وَمَنْ أَحْسَنُ مِنَ اللَّهِ صِبْغَةً ۖ

 [And say, "Ours is] the religion of Allah . And who is better than Allah in [ordaining] religion?  Not quite happy with this translation doesn’t fit in with the discussion?

And in Surah Muminoon: (23:20):

تَنبُتُ بِالدُّهْنِ وَصِبْغٍ لِّلْآكِلِينَ

which produces oil and food same here? for those who eat.

 

Summary:

1.          اغرق- to drown someone in water so that he dies.

2.         صبغ – to dip a morsel in curry or to immerse cloth in coloured water.
