7- DHANKNA AUR DHAANPNA [Cover and Clothe]

Qur’an has referred them by the words JANA (جن ), GHASHIA (غشي ) AGHSHA (أغشى ) GHASHA (غشى ) TAGHASHA (تغشى ) and ARHAQA (ارهق ).

 

 

7.1    JUNA (جن) : to hide something by covering it so that it remains hidden from sight. Jin is the creation that remains hidden from sight and JANEEN (جنين ) is the baby inside mother’s womb. It is mentioned in Qur’an:

Surat Al-'An`ām (The Cattle) - سورة الأنعام 6:77

 فَلَمَّا جَنَّ عَلَيْهِ الَّيْلُ رَاٰ كَوْكَبًاَ  

 So when the night covered him [with darkness], he saw a star.

 

7.2   GHASHIA (غشي): to cover one thing from the other in a way that it is not completely hidden. Allah says:

Surat Luqmān (Luqman) - سورة لقمان 31:32

 وَاِذَا غَشِيَهُمْ مَّوْجٌ كَالظُّلَلِ دَعَوُا اللّٰهَ مُخْلِصِيْنَ لَهُ الدِّيْنَ

  And when waves come over them like canopies, they supplicate Allah , sincere to Him in religion.

 

AGHSHA (أغشى ) GHASHA (غشى ) TAGHASHA (تغشى ) are all plurals of GHASHA (غشى ).

Surat Ar-Ra`d (The Thunder) - سورة الرعد 13:3

 يُغْشِي الَّيْلَ النَّهَارَ        

 He causes the night to cover the day.

 

Surat Al-'Anfāl (The Spoils of War) - سورة الأنفال 8:11

 اِذْ يُغَشِّيْكُمُ النُّعَاسَ  ۭ  

 [Remember] when He overwhelmed you with drowsiness.

 

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف 7:189

 فَلَمَّا تَغَشّٰىهَاَ  

And when man covers the woman.

 

7.3    RAHIQA(رهق ): Rahaqahu-al-Almr: means some matter chocked him by oppression force and Rahiqa and Arhaqa both has same meanings. And La-Arhaq-Allaho means Allah may not put you in trouble and hardship. Allah says:

Surat Al-Qalam (The Pen) - سورة القلم 68:43

   اشِعَةً اَبْصَارُهُمْ تَرْهَقُهُمْ ذِلَّةٌ

Their eyes humbled, humiliation will cover them.

 

Summary:

7.1  JANA: Hide from sight by covering.

7.2  AGHSHA: to cover in a way that it is not completely hidden.

7.3  RAHAQ: by force one thing oppress and covers the other.
