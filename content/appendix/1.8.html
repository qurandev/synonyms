8 PAHAAR,WADIYAAN AUR AMAKAN [Mountain,Valleys and Sites]

those mountains and fields or valleys whose name occur in the Holy Quran are as follows

1-2 SAFA(صفا ) and MARWAH(مروة): near House of ALLAH are two famous mountains.between those HAGAR to and fro 7 times in search of water. Nowadays those mountains are cutdown to get rid of the middle valley.only the signs of the mountains remain.Every pilgrim had to walk swiftly in between them as essential part of HAJJ and UMRAH

ALLAH said:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:158

إِنَّ الصَّفَا وَالْمَرْوَةَ مِن شَعَائِرِ اللَّهِ ۖ فَمَنْ حَجَّ الْبَيْتَ أَوِ اعْتَمَرَ فَلَا جُنَاحَ عَلَيْهِ أَن يَطَّوَّفَ بِهِمَا ۚ وَمَن تَطَوَّعَ خَيْرًا فَإِنَّ اللَّهَ شَاكِرٌ عَلِيمٌ

Indeed, as-Safa and al-Marwah are among the symbols of Allah . So whoever makes Hajj to the House or performs 'umrah - there is no blame upon him for walking between them. And whoever volunteers good - then indeed, Allah is appreciative and Knowing.

2.JUDIYY( جُوْدِيّ):Noah's Storm continues,consecutively for 40 days. meaning it keeps raining and also earth keeps releasing water from beneath it. in the mean time Noah's Ark keep on gaining height continuously. and that time this storm came on all visible earth where there was population of mankind. later on when water start receding then it took 150 days for this process to complete. and when water start receding then Ark rest at Mount Judiyy. Judiyy is (Mount Judi - Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia) one of the mountain of mountains of Ararat.and is famous with the same name. ALLAH said:

Surat Hūd (Hud) - سورة هود

11:44

وَقِيلَ يَا أَرْضُ ابْلَعِي مَاءَكِ وَيَا سَمَاءُ أَقْلِعِي وَغِيضَ الْمَاءُ وَقُضِيَ الْأَمْرُ وَاسْتَوَتْ عَلَى الْجُودِيِّ ۖ وَقِيلَ بُعْدًا لِّلْقَوْمِ الظَّالِمِينَ

And it was said, "O earth, swallow your water, and O sky, withhold [your rain]." And the water subsided, and the matter was accomplished, and the ship came to rest on the [mountain of] Judiyy. And it was said, "Away with the wrongdoing people."

4-6 SINA( سينا),SINAIN(سينين),TWR(طور) and TWY(طوي): SINA and SINAIN are two names of one thing. SINA is a high mountain whose height is 7359 foot which is in the path between MADIAN and EGYPT in the country of SYRIA . At this place Moses(PBUH) was bestowed twice the opportunity to talk with ALLAH.One of the peak of this mountain is called TWR. and in the base of this mountain is a valley named TWY which is also called Holy Valley and Blessed place. When Moses (PBUH) took Children of Israel out of EGYPT then he went past this path. from this relation TWR Mountain is also called TWRI SINAIN(). ALLAH said

Surat At-Tīn (The Fig) - سورة التين

95:1

وَالتِّينِ وَالزَّيْتُونِ

By the fig and the olive

Surat At-Tīn (The Fig) - سورة التين

95:2

وَطُورِ سِينِينَ

And [by] Mount Sinai

7 3RFAT(عرفات):is a mountain ,25 Kilometer away from MAKKAH which is called JABLI 3RFAT(جبل عرفات). and the base or valley of this mountain is also called 3RFAT.after the forenoon on 9th of ZILHAJJ ,pilgrims supplicate here till evening .Staying at 3RFAT is major pillar of Hajj.and day of 3RFAH is 9th of ZILHAJJ which is the day of preparation to reach 3RFAT. and 3ARRAFA ALHUJJAJ(عَرَّفَ الحُجّاج) meaning pilgrims stayed in 3RFAT

8 MASH3R ALHARAM(مشعر الحرام): MASH3R(مشعر) plural MSHA3R(مشاعر) comes in the meaning of SH3AIR(شعائر). meaning reminder of religion(something which make conscious of religion).and MASH3R ALHARAM is the name of a mountain near MUZDALFA(مزدلفه). on 9th of ZILHAJJ,whne pilgrims return from 3RFAT stays overnight and supplicate here. and from here pick up stones for RAMI(رَمي) with them. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:198

لَيْسَ عَلَيْكُمْ جُنَاحٌ أَن تَبْتَغُوا فَضْلًا مِّن رَّبِّكُمْ ۚ فَإِذَا أَفَضْتُم مِّنْ عَرَفَاتٍ فَاذْكُرُوا اللَّهَ عِندَ الْمَشْعَرِ الْحَرَامِ ۖ وَاذْكُرُوهُ كَمَا هَدَاكُمْ وَإِن كُنتُم مِّن قَبْلِهِ لَمِنَ الضَّالِّينَ

There is no blame upon you for seeking bounty from your Lord [during Hajj]. But when you depart from 'Arafat, remember Allah at al- Mash'ar al-Haram. And remember Him, as He has guided you, for indeed, you were before that among those astray.

9 BADR(بدر):a field 80 miles away from medina . which came on one side of the path from Medina to Makkah . at this place occurred the famous campaign of BADR in 2 Hijri . ALLAH said

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران

3:123

وَلَقَدْ نَصَرَكُمُ اللَّهُ بِبَدْرٍ وَأَنتُمْ أَذِلَّةٌ ۖ فَاتَّقُوا اللَّهَ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُونَ

And already had Allah given you victory at [the battle of] Badr while you were few in number. Then fear Allah ; perhaps you will be grateful.

10 HUNAYN(حُنَيْن): name of a vally between Makkah and Taif where afte the Conquest of Makkah in month of Shawwal 8th Hijri occurred the battel of HUNAYN with People of HAWAZIN. HAWAZIN were master archers. Muslims strength was 12,000 and a bit more than

disbelievers. partly due to effect of Conquest of Makkah ,and partly pride of majority in numbers. the result was that Muslim got defeated in the begining. then when army was reinforced again then by ALLAH's favoured them and turn this defeat in to Victory. ALLAH said

Surat At-Tawbah (The Repentance) - سورة التوبة

9:25

لَقَدْ نَصَرَكُمُ اللَّهُ فِي مَوَاطِنَ كَثِيرَةٍ ۙ وَيَوْمَ حُنَيْنٍ ۙ إِذْ أَعْجَبَتْكُمْ كَثْرَتُكُمْ فَلَمْ تُغْنِ عَنكُمْ شَيْئًا وَضَاقَتْ عَلَيْكُمُ الْأَرْضُ بِمَا رَحُبَتْ ثُمَّ وَلَّيْتُم مُّدْبِرِينَ

Allah has already given you victory in many regions and [even] on the day of Hunayn, when your great number pleased you, but it did not avail you at all, and the earth was confining for you with its vastness; then you turned back, fleeing.

11 MASJID ALAQSA(مسجِد الاقصَي):lexical meaning , a mosque far away. and it means BAIT UL MUQADDAS (بيت المقدس) which was the first QIBLA(قبله) of muslim(direction of prayer).on earth after KA3BAH or House of ALLAH was this mosque built. and it is called MASJID AQSA because it is situated far awa from MASJID ALHARAM(KA3BAH). later on this name became known in usage. ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء

17:1

سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي أَسْرَىٰ بِعَبْدِهِ لَيْلًا مِّنَ الْمَسْجِدِ الْحَرَامِ إِلَى الْمَسْجِدِ الْأَقْصَى الَّذِي بَارَكْنَا حَوْلَهُ لِنُرِيَهُ مِنْ آيَاتِنَا ۚ إِنَّهُ هُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْبَصِيرُ

Exalted is He who took His Servant by night from al-Masjid al-Haram to al-Masjid al- Aqsa, whose surroundings We have blessed, to show him of Our signs. Indeed, He is the Hearing, the Seeing.

12 SIDRATUL MUNTAHA(سِدْرَةُ الْمُنْتَهَي): SIDRAH means berry tree and MUNTAHA meaning at the very limit of situated a berry tree.This is a place in the Heavens where Information of Lower World ends and elaborations of Upper world descends down from here. at this place Holy Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) saw Angel Gabriel(AS) in his original form the second time. and also means a berry tree on the right side of the throne of ALLAH, which is the last limit of the reach of Angels etc. The place where Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) was especially bestowed with ALLAH's Blessings and Hefty Prizes. and all these discourses are proven from SURAH NAJM(53) ayah 5-18. ALLAH said

Surat An-Najm (The Star) - سورة النجم

53:14

عِندَ سِدْرَةِ الْمُنتَهَىٰ

At the Lote Tree of the Utmost Boundary -

Surat An-Najm (The Star) - سورة النجم

53:15

عِندَهَا جَنَّةُ الْمَأْوَىٰ

Near it is the Garden of Refuge -

13 KWCR(كوثر): people of lexicon wrote its meaning as excessive good. and this is proven from lots of Hadith(Prophet's Sayings). that KWCR is a stream in Heave which was granted to Prophet Muhammad(PBUH). there is a narration from Hazrat ANS(RA) that Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) was overtaken by sleep.when he awake he smiled and told me the reason of smile that just now a surah was revealed on me , then he recited Surah KWCR and said "Do you know what is KWCR". Companions(RA) replied "ALLAH and HIS messenger (PBUH) knows best" . then Prophet Muhammad (PBUH ) said "It's a stream which ALLAH has given me in the Paradise and it contains excessive good". ALLAH said

Indeed, We have granted you, [O Muhammad], al-Kawthar.

14 SALSABEEL(سلسبيل): people of lexicon write it as sweet and pleasant water which is easily drinkable down the mouth. and SALSABEEL is define by ALLAH HIMSELF as this is a spring in the paradise in drink of which contains mixture of dry ginger. ALLAH said

KAFWR(كافور):In the drinks of paradise apart from mixing of ZANJABYL , there also be mixing of KAFWR as told in the Quran. KAFWR(Camphor) is a famous aromatic,white herb which is obtained from the tree of KAFWR . in its effect its quite cold . ALLAH said
