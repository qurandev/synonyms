9 AQWAM [Nations]

Those nations which are mentioned in the Quran. From those,

1 Nation of Noah,

2 Ād,

3 Thamud,

4 Lot,

5 Moses,

6 Pharaoh,

are already described in the section of Prophets. Apart from there are some other Nations that are mentioned which are

7 QWM IRAM(قوم اِرَم): is the other attribute name or title of Nation of Ād. IRAM literally means sign made of stones and it refers to those high and decorated pillars which Nation of Ād or Thamud (Ād successor) made in the capacity of master stone sculpturer. also IRAM is the first person of Nation of Ād or the royal family of nation of Ād. Those people are dwellers in the area of YEMEN. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Fajr (The Dawn) - سورة الفجر

89:6

أَلَمْ تَرَ كَيْفَ فَعَلَ رَبُّكَ بِعَادٍ

Have you not considered how your Lord dealt with 'Aad -

Surat Al-Fajr (The Dawn) - سورة الفجر

89:7

إِرَمَ ذَاتِ الْعِمَادِ

[With] Iram - who had lofty pillars,

8 QWM TUBBA3(قوم تُبَّع): TUBBA3 was the title of the kings of HAMYR tribe. TUBBA3's nation was actually a branch of Nation of SABA which in 115 B.C in the are of SABA (situated in YEMEN). and remain in power till 300 A.D . ALLAH said

Surat Ad-Dukhān (The Smoke) - سورة الدخان

44:37

أَهُمْ خَيْرٌ أَمْ قَوْمُ تُبَّعٍ وَالَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِهِمْ ۚ أَهْلَكْنَاهُمْ ۖ إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا مُجْرِمِينَ

Are they better or the people of Tubba' and those before them? We destroyed them, [for] indeed, they were criminals.

9 QRYSH(قريش): Messenger of ALLAH (PBUH) ,11th generation predecessor was FHR(فهر) who became famous with the title of QRYSH. QRYSH is actually saod to Whale which is the biggest and most powerful of all the marine animals. In time of FHR, one ruler of YEMEN named HASSAN had attacked Makkah with the intention of taking away KABAH ' stone from there to YEMEN to built a House of ALLAH there. FHR fights him and arrested him. due to this reason he became famous with the title of QRYSH. FHR's offspring is also called QRYSH, QRYSH is also Messenger of ALLAH (PBUH)'s own nation, which was highly reputed in the entire Arabia but this nation oppose the Messenger of ALLAH (PBUH) most. The stength of this opposition subsides on the day of conquest of Makkah and this nation accepted faith. then most Caliphs of Islam were also from this nation. ALLAH said

Surat Quraysh (Quraysh) - سورة قريش

106:1

لِإِيلَافِ قُرَيْشٍ

For the accustomed security of the Quraysh -

Surat Quraysh (Quraysh) - سورة قريش

106:2

إِيلَافِهِمْ رِحْلَةَ الشِّتَاءِ وَالصَّيْفِ

Their accustomed security [in] the caravan of winter and summer -

10 YAJUJ(ياجوج) and MAJUJ(ماجوج) aka Gog Magog:Those warrior and wild nations of Northern and Eastern Asia which used to attack,decimate civilized countries. They are classified from the offspring of Noah(PBUH)'s son YAFC(يافث). Their areas are Russia, Moscow etc. King ZULQARNAIN(ذوالقرنين) which was mentioned before , had built a hurdle/wall in order to get rid of their attacks. This wall was built in between the trough area or valley of two mountain ranges from where this wild nation used to emerge for attack. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف

18:94

قَالُوا يَا ذَا الْقَرْنَيْنِ إِنَّ يَأْجُوجَ وَمَأْجُوجَ مُفْسِدُونَ فِي الْأَرْضِ فَهَلْ نَجْعَلُ لَكَ خَرْجًا عَلَىٰ أَن تَجْعَلَ بَيْنَنَا وَبَيْنَهُمْ سَدًّا

They said, "O Dhul-Qarnayn, indeed Gog and Magog are [great] corrupters in the land. So may we assign for you an expenditure that you might make between us and them a barrier?"
