6 Some Idioms

1 HIJRAN MAHJURAN(حِجْراً مَّحْجُوْراً):HIJARAH حِجَارَة meaning stone. and HIJR حِجْر is said to all those things which are hard like stone and can also serves as shield or protection. people of arab had this habit that when they saw such an enemy from which they feel the danger of having trouble or some other calamity . like we said GOD save use from this. so normally the one listening wouldnot harm the one saying it. QURAN has also used this idiom.

Surat Al-Furqān (The Criterian) - سورة الفرقان

25:22

يَوْمَ يَرَوْنَ الْمَلَائِكَةَ لَا بُشْرَىٰ يَوْمَئِذٍ لِّلْمُجْرِمِينَ وَيَقُولُونَ حِجْرًا مَّحْجُورًا

The day they see the angels - no good tidings will there be that day for the criminals, and [the angels] will say, "Prevented and inaccessible."

2 ADLA(DLW)ALA FULAN اَدْلَي (دلو) الَي فلان: DALWدَلْو meaning empty well bucket. and ADLAادْلَي means to hang down empty well bucket in the well to fill it up. and DLWT BFULAN(دلوت بفلان) meaning I made FULAN(Mr X)my recommender.and ADLA BIQARABATIH(اَدْلَي بِقَرَبَتِهِ) meaning to get intercession of some relative.to make him a recommender. and ADLA ILA FULAN اَدْلَي الَي فلان meaning to take one's dispute to someone. and it is used when using false means ,one intend to usurp someone else's right. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:188

وَلَا تَأْكُلُوا أَمْوَالَكُم بَيْنَكُم بِالْبَاطِلِ وَتُدْلُوا بِهَا إِلَى الْحُكَّامِ لِتَأْكُلُوا فَرِيقًا مِّنْ أَمْوَالِ النَّاسِ بِالْإِثْمِ وَأَنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ

And do not consume one another's wealth unjustly or send it [in bribery] to the rulers in order that [they might aid] you [to] consume a portion of the wealth of the people in sin, while you know [it is unlawful].

3 RABATA 3LA ALQALB(رَبَطَ عَلَي القَلْب):RABATرَبَطَ meaning strongly tied knot and RABT ALLAH 3LA ALQALBربط الّله علي القلب meaning ALLAH tying knot on unsettling and anxiety of some one's heart.and to grant him success/support of patience. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص

28:10

وَأَصْبَحَ فُؤَادُ أُمِّ مُوسَىٰ فَارِغًا ۖ إِن كَادَتْ لَتُبْدِي بِهِ لَوْلَا أَن رَّبَطْنَا عَلَىٰ قَلْبِهَا لِتَكُونَ مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ

And the heart of Moses' mother became empty [of all else]. She was about to disclose [the matter concerning] him had We not bound fast her heart that she would be of the believers.

4 RADD ILA ALHAFIRAH(رَدّ اِلَي الْحَافِرَة): HAFARA(حَفَرَ) meaning to dig a hole and HUFRAH(حُفْرَة) meaning hole and grave as well. and HAFIRAHحَافِرَة is feminine of HAFIR(حافر) meaning dugged up earth and inital state/condition as well. and RADD FI ALHAFIRAH رَدّ في الْحَافِرَة meaning from where started ,going back there.[urdu poetry roughly translates as "dust return to settle where it belongs" refers to grave of a person]. In the Quran

Surat An-Nāzi`āt (Those who drag forth) - سورة النازعات

79:10

يَقُولُونَ أَإِنَّا لَمَرْدُودُونَ فِي الْحَافِرَةِ

They are [presently] saying, "Will we indeed be returned to [our] former state [of life]?

5 RYBA ALMNUN(ريبَ المنُون):RYBريب is such a doubt which includes anxiety and restlessness.and MANIYYAHمَنِيَّة meaning death,divine predestined(plural is MANAYAمَنَايَا and MANUENمَنُوْن) and RYBA ALMNUN's idiom is said when some person desires on someone other to be cursed with calamities and tribulation and want him dead. and to await for his bad end or time going against him. ALLAH said

Surat Aţ-Ţūr (The Mount) - سورة الطور

52:30

أَمْ يَقُولُونَ شَاعِرٌ نَّتَرَبَّصُ بِهِ رَيْبَ الْمَنُونِ

Or do they say [of you], "A poet for whom we await a misfortune of time?"

6 SUQITA FIYADIHI(سُقِطَ فِيْ يَدِهِ): SAQATAسَقَط meaning something fall down and SAQAT FI ALKALAMسقط في الكلام meaning to mistake while talking ,or to say inappropriate thing. and SUQITAسُقِطَ or USQITQ FI YADIHIاُسْقِطَ فِيْ يَدِهِ means to repent or humiliated in front of people owing to disclosure of one's discourse or proof. In the Quran

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف

7:149

وَلَمَّا سُقِطَ فِي أَيْدِيهِمْ وَرَأَوْا أَنَّهُمْ قَدْ ضَلُّوا قَالُوا لَئِن لَّمْ يَرْحَمْنَا رَبُّنَا وَيَغْفِرْ لَنَا لَنَكُونَنَّ مِنَ الْخَاسِرِينَ

And when regret overcame them and they saw that they had gone astray, they said, "If our Lord does not have mercy upon us and forgive us, we will surely be among the losers."

7 USHRIBA HUBBU ALFULAN(اُشْرِبَ حُبُّ الفلان): SHARABAشَرَبَ meaning to drink, and ASHRABAاشْرَبَ meaning to make some one drink, and USHRIBA HUBBU ALFULAN's is an idiom meaning when someone's heart is occupied by love of something.engulf it,encompass it. In the Quran

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:93

وَإِذْ أَخَذْنَا مِيثَاقَكُمْ وَرَفَعْنَا فَوْقَكُمُ الطُّورَ خُذُوا مَا آتَيْنَاكُم بِقُوَّةٍ وَاسْمَعُوا ۖ قَالُوا سَمِعْنَا وَعَصَيْنَا وَأُشْرِبُوا فِي قُلُوبِهِمُ الْعِجْلَ بِكُفْرِهِمْ ۚ قُلْ بِئْسَمَا يَأْمُرُكُم بِهِ إِيمَانُكُمْ إِن كُنتُم مُّؤْمِنِينَ

And [recall] when We took your covenant and raised over you the mount, [saying], "Take what We have given you with determination and listen." They said [instead], "We hear and disobey." And their hearts absorbed [the worship of] the calf because of their disbelief. Say, "How wretched is that which your faith enjoins upon you, if you should be believers."

8 SHAGHAFA HUBBAHU(شَغَفَ حُبَّهُ): SHAGHFشَغْف is veil or peel of heart. and SHAGHAFA شَغَفَmeaning to hit the veil of heart. and SHAGHAFA HUBBAHU means such a love and passion which hit the heart and keep it unsettled. In the QURAN

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

12:30

وَقَالَ نِسْوَةٌ فِي الْمَدِينَةِ امْرَأَتُ الْعَزِيزِ تُرَاوِدُ فَتَاهَا عَن نَّفْسِهِ ۖ قَدْ شَغَفَهَا حُبًّا ۖ إِنَّا لَنَرَاهَا فِي ضَلَالٍ مُّبِينٍ

And women in the city said, "The wife of al-'Azeez is seeking to seduce her slave boy; he has impassioned her with love. Indeed, we see her [to be] in clear error."

9 ISHTA3ALA ALRR-S(اِشتَعَلَ الرَّأس): SHU3LAH( شُعلة) meaning flame of fire, common word and ISHTA3ALاشْتَعَل meaning someine got provoked with anger. and ISHTA3ALA ALRR-S SHAYBAN(اِشتَعَلَ الرَّأسُ شَيْباً) is an idiom. meaning whitenss of oldage spread in head's hair. meaning those head hair getting white which gets white after the whitening of beard's hair. In the Quran

Surat Maryam (Mary) - سورة مريم

19:4

قَالَ رَبِّ إِنِّي وَهَنَ الْعَظْمُ مِنِّي وَاشْتَعَلَ الرَّأْسُ شَيْبًا وَلَمْ أَكُن بِدُعَائِكَ رَبِّ شَقِيًّا

He said, "My Lord, indeed my bones have weakened, and my head has filled with white, and never have I been in my supplication to You, my Lord, unhappy.

10 DARABA 3ALA ALUZUN( ضَرَبَ عَلَي الاُذُن): meaning to hit some one on ears but as per idiom it means to make some sleep by gently tapping on the ear . ALLAH said

Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف

18:11

فَضَرَبْنَا عَلَىٰ آذَانِهِمْ فِي الْكَهْفِ سِنِينَ عَدَدًا

So We cast [a cover of sleep] over their ears within the cave for a number of years.

11 DARABA FI ALARD(ضَرَبَ فِي الْاَرضِ): literal meaning to hit in the ground,or moreover to hit feet. but in idiom it means such a journey which is purposeful. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Muzzammil (The Enshrouded One) - سورة المزمل

73:20

إِنَّ رَبَّكَ يَعْلَمُ أَنَّكَ تَقُومُ أَدْنَىٰ مِن ثُلُثَيِ اللَّيْلِ وَنِصْفَهُ وَثُلُثَهُ وَطَائِفَةٌ مِّنَ الَّذِينَ مَعَكَ ۚ وَاللَّهُ يُقَدِّرُ اللَّيْلَ وَالنَّهَارَ ۚ عَلِمَ أَن لَّن تُحْصُوهُ فَتَابَ عَلَيْكُمْ ۖ فَاقْرَءُوا مَا تَيَسَّرَ مِنَ الْقُرْآنِ ۚ عَلِمَ أَن سَيَكُونُ مِنكُم مَّرْضَىٰ ۙ وَآخَرُونَ يَضْرِبُونَ فِي الْأَرْضِ يَبْتَغُونَ مِن فَضْلِ اللَّهِ ۙ وَآخَرُونَ يُقَاتِلُونَ فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ ۖ فَاقْرَءُوا مَا تَيَسَّرَ مِنْهُ ۚ وَأَقِيمُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَآتُوا الزَّكَاةَ وَأَقْرِضُوا اللَّهَ قَرْضًا حَسَنًا ۚ وَمَا تُقَدِّمُوا لِأَنفُسِكُم مِّنْ خَيْرٍ تَجِدُوهُ عِندَ اللَّهِ هُوَ خَيْرًا وَأَعْظَمَ أَجْرًا ۚ وَاسْتَغْفِرُوا اللَّهَ ۖ إِنَّ اللَّهَ غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ

Indeed, your Lord knows, [O Muhammad], that you stand [in prayer] almost two thirds of the night or half of it or a third of it, and [so do] a group of those with you. And Allah determines [the extent of] the night and the day. He has known that you [Muslims] will not be able to do it and has turned to you in forgiveness, so recite what is easy [for you] of the Qur'an. He has known that there will be among you those who are ill and others traveling throughout the land seeking [something] of the bounty of Allah and others fighting for the cause of Allah . So recite what is easy from it and establish prayer and give zakah and loan Allah a goodly loan. And whatever good you put forward for yourselves - you will find it with Allah . It is better and greater in reward. And seek forgiveness of Allah . Indeed, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful.

15 NABAZA 3ALA SAWAAI(نَبَذَ عَلَي سَوَآءٍ): NABZ(نبذ) meaning to throw away something considering it uselessand NABZ AL3HD(نبذ العهد) meaning to break or dishonour some agreement's conditions and NABAZA 3ALA SAWAAI meaning to throw away the agreement on the level of equality meaning both the party to the agreement return back to the condition and level and state of preagreement. and thus terms and conditions of agreement being void. ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Anfāl (The Spoils of War) - سورة الأنفال

8:58

وَإِمَّا تَخَافَنَّ مِن قَوْمٍ خِيَانَةً فَانبِذْ إِلَيْهِمْ عَلَىٰ سَوَاءٍ ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يُحِبُّ الْخَائِنِينَ

If you [have reason to] fear from a people betrayal, throw [their treaty] back to them, [putting you] on equal terms. Indeed, Allah does not like traitors.

16 NAKASA 3ALA RASIN(نكَسَ عَلَي رَاسٍ): NKS(نكسَ) meaning to turn upside down and NAKASA RASAHU( نَكَسَ رَاسَهُ) meaning to lower head,to give up, and NAKASA 3ALA RASIN meaning to lower one head in embarrasment and to think. In the Quran

Surat Al-'Anbyā' (The Prophets) - سورة الأنبياء

21:65

ثُمَّ نُكِسُوا عَلَىٰ رُءُوسِهِمْ لَقَدْ عَلِمْتَ مَا هَٰؤُلَاءِ يَنطِقُونَ

Then they reversed themselves, [saying], "You have already known that these do not speak!"

17 WALAJ FI SAMMI ALKHIYAT(وَلَج فِيْ سَمِّ الْخِيَاط):WALAJA وَلَج meaning to enter a narrow place and HATTA YLIJA ALJAMALU FI SAMMI ALKHIYAT(حَتَّي يَلِجَ الْجَمَلُ فِيْ سَمِّ الْخِيَاط) literally means 'till camel enter needle hole' and this idiom is spoken at that time when something is absolutely impossible. ALLAH said

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف

7:40

إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِنَا وَاسْتَكْبَرُوا عَنْهَا لَا تُفَتَّحُ لَهُمْ أَبْوَابُ السَّمَاءِ وَلَا يَدْخُلُونَ الْجَنَّةَ حَتَّىٰ يَلِجَ الْجَمَلُ فِي سَمِّ الْخِيَاطِ ۚ وَكَذَٰلِكَ نَجْزِي الْمُجْرِمِينَ

Indeed, those who deny Our verses and are arrogant toward them - the gates of Heaven will not be opened for them, nor will they enter Paradise until a camel enters into the eye of a needle. And thus do We recompense the criminals.
