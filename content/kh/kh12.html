12 KHALQAT-MAKHLUWQ [Creature-Creation]

SYNONYMS:BARIYYAH (BA-RA-AA)(بَرِيَّة (برئ)ANAM اَنَام JIBILLAN(JA-BA-LA)(جِبِلّاً (جبل)

12.1 BARIYYAH: is derived from BARA-A بَرَءَ.meaning to bring in to existence something from nothing.thus every such thing which exist is included in BARIYYAH.All Creation.All Universe Earth and Skies,other planets/celestial bodies are also included in it.In the QURAN

Surat Al-Bayyinah (The Clear Proof) - سورة البينة

98:7

إِنَّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ أُولَٰئِكَ هُمْ خَيْرُ الْبَرِيَّةِ

Indeed, they who have believed and done righteous deeds - those are the best of creatures.

12.2 ANAM:is used for every type of creationg present on earth.whether living or non living.ALLAH said

Surat Ar-Raĥmān (The Beneficent) - سورة الرحمن

55:10

وَالْأَرْضَ وَضَعَهَا لِلْأَنَامِ

And the earth He laid [out] for the creatures.

and ALWARAاَلْوَرَي meaning that creation which was present on earth at one time.it doesn't include past and future generation.but this word is not used in the HOLY QURAN.and its plural is WARAYAوَرَايَا.

12.3 JIBILLAN:plural of JIBLAHجبلّة -its meaning is as per JBL or hugeness of mountain.meaning very big group.ALLAH said

Surat Yā-Sīn (Ya Sin) - سورة يس

36:62

وَلَقَدْ أَضَلَّ مِنكُمْ جِبِلًّا كَثِيرًا ۖ أَفَلَمْ تَكُونُوا تَعْقِلُونَ

And he had already led astray from among you much of creation, so did you not use reason?

SUMMARY:

12.1 BARIYYAH:for all creation

12.2 ANAM:for creation present on earth

12.3 JIBILLAN:for a big part of creation that is subject to shariah
