6 HD SAY KAM KARNA [To lessen than the limit]

SYNONYMS: FARRATA فَرَّطَ QASARA قَصَرَ

6.1 FARRATA:FRT فرط is from intrinsic antonym.FART means as explained before. is to go pass limit without giving due thought or in a hurry.and FARRATA means to stay behind the limits or to lessen or to be negligent without due thought or in a rush.In the QURAN

Surat Az-Zumar (The Troops) - سورة الزمر

39:56

أَن تَقُولَ نَفْسٌ يَا حَسْرَتَا عَلَىٰ مَا فَرَّطتُ فِي جَنبِ اللَّهِ وَإِن كُنتُ لَمِنَ السَّاخِرِينَ

Lest a soul should say, "Oh [how great is] my regret over what I neglected in regard to Allah and that I was among the mockers."

6.2 QASARA:to lessen or be lessened some from appointed limit.QASAR ASSAHM( قَصَرَ السَّهْم) meaning arrow not reaching the target.QASR ASSALAH(قصر الصلوة) to not offer complete prayer but to reduce some from it.QASAR ASSHA3R(قَصَّرَ الشَّعْر) meaning to trim some part of hairs and QASURA قَصُرَ meaning to be small.from this QASUR قصور ,TAQSIYR تقصير and QASIR قاصر are words used in URDU. In the QURAN

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء

4:101

وَإِذَا ضَرَبْتُمْ فِي الْأَرْضِ فَلَيْسَ عَلَيْكُمْ جُنَاحٌ أَن تَقْصُرُوا مِنَ الصَّلَاةِ إِنْ خِفْتُمْ أَن يَفْتِنَكُمُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا ۚ إِنَّ الْكَافِرِينَ كَانُوا لَكُمْ عَدُوًّا مُّبِينًا

And when you travel throughout the land, there is no blame upon you for shortening the prayer, [especially] if you fear that those who disbelieve may disrupt [or attack] you. Indeed, the disbelievers are ever to you a clear enemy.

SUMMARY:

6.1 FARRATA:is used for not performing some matter properly and for being negligent.

6.2 QASARA:to lessen up/reduce quantity of something
