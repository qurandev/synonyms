2      HAAZIR HONA

         [to be present]

 

SYNONYMS: HADARA, SHAHIDA, and 3ATADA.

 

2.1 HADARA (حَضَرَ) : it is the opposite of غَابَ . It means something becoming present, coming forward.

Refer to 46:29

__________________________________

2.2 SHAHIDA (شَهِدَ) : this word is used for several meanings. But according to the subject it has the two following meanings:

1) to be present. Refer to 37:150

2) to be present and see something with one’s own eyes, and then to report it in front of a judge ie bearing witness. Refer to 2:282

__________________________________

2.3 3ATADA (عَتَدَ) : means to prepare. And اَعْتَدَ means to make something prepared. عَتِيْد means that which is already prepared and is present.

Refer to 50:23

See “TAYYAR HONA”

__________________________________

SUMMARY

HADARA: means to be present

SHAHIDA: to be present. To see and witness.

3ATADA: something which is prepared from before, and is present.
