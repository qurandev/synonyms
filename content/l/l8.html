8 KANPNA [To Shiver]

Synonyms:ZALZAL زَلْزَل MARA(MA-WA-RA)(مَارَ (مور) RAJJA رَجَّ RAJAFA رَجَفَ

8.1 ZALZAL:ZALLA زَلَّ meaning slipping off step.and ZALZAL contains recurrence of word.that recurrence which alludes to meaning(the physical action)[repetition in words implies repetition in action/meaning].meaning repetitively keep on slipping here and there.and ZALZALAH زَلْزَلَة meaning jolt-earth having impulses.moving here and there.and shivering.this word is specific for jolting (earthquake).ALLAH said

Surat Az-Zalzalah (The Earthquake) - سورة الزلزلة

99:1

إِذَا زُلْزِلَتِ الْأَرْضُ زِلْزَالَهَا

When the earth is shaken with its [final] earthquake

8.2 RAJJA:to displace something slightly.to jolt.this is the inital stage of earth quake.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Wāqi`ah (The Inevitable) - سورة الواقعة

56:4

إِذَا رُجَّتِ الْأَرْضُ رَجًّا

When the earth is shaken with convulsion

8.3 RAJAFA:basic meaning is intense restlessness.BAHRUN RAJJAFUN بَحْرٌ رَجَّافٌ meaning 'rough sea'.

Surat Al-'Aĥzāb (The Combined Forces) - سورة الأحزاب

33:60

لَّئِن لَّمْ يَنتَهِ الْمُنَافِقُونَ وَالَّذِينَ فِي قُلُوبِهِم مَّرَضٌ وَالْمُرْجِفُونَ فِي الْمَدِينَةِ لَنُغْرِيَنَّكَ بِهِمْ ثُمَّ لَا يُجَاوِرُونَكَ فِيهَا إِلَّا قَلِيلًا

If the hypocrites and those in whose hearts is disease and those who spread rumors in al-Madinah do not cease, We will surely incite you against them; then they will not remain your neighbors therein except for a little.

meaning the one causing suspense,causing waves of unrest.and when it refers to earth then it means intense jolts.condition of intense earthquake

in the QURAN

Surat Al-Muzzammil (The Enshrouded One) - سورة المزمل

73:14

يَوْمَ تَرْجُفُ الْأَرْضُ وَالْجِبَالُ وَكَانَتِ الْجِبَالُ كَثِيبًا مَّهِيلًا

On the Day the earth and the mountains will convulse and the mountains will become a heap of sand pouring down.

8.4 MARA(MA-WA-RA):basic concept is of movement and swiftness.ANNAQATU TAMUWRU FI SAYRIHA اَلنَّاقَةُ تَمُوْرُ فِي سَيْرِهَا meaning she camel spreading dust by speeding swiftly.and MAWR مَوْر meaning fly in air by becoming dust.and MAR ASHSHAYEE مار الشئ meaning something moving to and fro owing to swift speed and to vibrate- to lose balance.

Surat Aţ-Ţūr (The Mount) - سورة الطور

52:9

يَوْمَ تَمُورُ السَّمَاءُ مَوْرًا

On the Day the heaven will sway with circular motion

Summary:ZALZALA,RAJJA and RAJAFA all three of them are used commonly for EarthQuake.ZALZAL is common.RAJJA is initial condition and RAJF is intense condition. MARA is vibration and disorientation owing to swift speed in any thing.
