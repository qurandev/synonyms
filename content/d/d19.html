To make far-or to be far -19

Synonyms are:

1)    بَعُدَ (باعَدَ)

2)    قَصَا

3)    نَاى

4)    كفَّرَ

 

1)baouda بَعُدَ : means to be far. Use of this is general. And can be used for all kind of distance. Distance in location, time, and status. To travel or to go at a far place Fiqhul lughat 11  ALLAH says in Quran:

وَلَٰكِن بَعُدَتْ عَلَيْهِمُ الشُّقَّةُ ۚ

(surah 9 at tawbah verse 42)

Translation: but the distance was long, (and weighed) on them.

Baaaada باعَدَ means to make far Mufraddat - مفردات.

Allah says:

فَقَالُوا رَبَّنَا بَاعِدْ بَيْنَ أَسْفَارِنَا

(surah 34 saba verse 19)

Transaltion: But they said: "Our Lord! Place longer distances between our journey-stages.

 

2)qassa  قَصَا  (from yaqsoo يَقْصُوَا):it means getting far and can only be used as adverb of place. and it gives the meaning of  getting far from a specific place and resident of that place. aqsal madenah اقصَى المدينة means other brim/edge of the city(other corner of the city). Mosque of Aqsa(proper noun) means the mosque that is far.

ALLAH SAYS in quran:

فَحَمَلَتْهُ فَانتَبَذَتْ بِهِ مَكَانًا قَصِيًّا

(surah 19 maryum verse 22)

Translation: So she conceived him, and she retired with him to a remote place.

3)naa نَاى :means to get far.and naiyaah نَائِيَة means one who stays away Maqaayeesul-lughat  and to reach near a far destination Alfurooqul-lughwiyyah 11.only used as adverb of time. And according to one phrase of imam raghib “to get away from sumone because of arrogance Mufraddat – مفردات  .in Quran this word is used in two places and both of its  meanings are justified.

As Allah says in Quran:

وَإِذَا أَنْعَمْنَا عَلَى الْإِنسَانِ أَعْرَضَ وَنَأَىٰ بِجَانِبِهِ

(surah  17 al israa verse 83)

Transaltion: Yet when We bestow Our favours on man, he turns away and becomes remote on his side (instead of coming to Us).

 

And in another place:

وَهُمْ يَنْهَوْنَ عَنْهُ وَيَنْأَوْنَ عَنْهُ ۖ وَإِن يُهْلِكُونَ إِلَّا أَنفُسَهُمْ وَمَا يَشْعُرُونَ

(surah 6 anaam verse 26)

Transalation: Others they keep away from it, and themselves they keep away; but they only destroy their own souls, and they perceive it not.

4)kafara كفَّرَ:from kaafaraa which means to hide.and kufraah كُفرَة  means the darkness of night. and kafaraah كفارَة means an act after its performance will  cover up the sins  . And if kafara كفَّرَ  is attributed to Allah then it means wiping away of sins. And kafara is related to bad deeds as ghafara (forgivness) is to  zanoob(sins).ALLAH SAYS

لَكَفَّرْنَا عَنْهُمْ سَيِّئَاتِهِمْ

(surah 5 al maidah verse 65)

Translation: We should indeed have blotted out their iniquities

4)qazaa  قَضٰى :its dictionary definition is not to take away but if it is attributed to humans then it means to fulfill a responsibility :

Allah says

فَلَمَّا قَضَىٰ مُوسَى الْأَجَلَ

(surah 28 al qasas verse 29)

Transaltion: Now when Moses had fulfilled the term.

However in the following verse as per the Arabic idioms it means to take away. Because, it is also a part of accomplish the task.

ثُمَّ لْيَقْضُوا تَفَثَهُمْ وَلْيُوفُوا نُذُورَهُمْ

(surah 22 hajj verse 29)

Transaltion: Then let them end their untidiness and fulfill their vows.

 

Summary:

1)Bood:it’s a usual word can be used for all kinds of distance.

2)qassa:distance from a specific place citizens

3)naai:its adverb of time and it means to stay away from sum one out of arrogance.

4)kafara: Allah wiping away of sins due to good deeds.

5)qazaa: after completing  a big task to do something as an additional task(appendage).
