Hell and its different names

Synonyms are:

1)    النّار

2)    جَهنَّمَ

3)    جَحِيمَ

4)    سَقْر

5)    سَعير

1)annar النّار: from the word nor. It means fire.its a common word.

When the word naar is preceded  by Al then it will become a proper noun and if this word has no other way to distinguish it than the meaning of annar النّار will be always hell.

Allah says:

إِنَّهُمْ صَالُو النَّارِ

(surah 38 saad verse 59)

Transaltion: they shall burn in the Fire.

 

2)jahanum  جَهنَّمَ: this word is arabicize from the farsi word jehnaam. Means place of penalties. House of punishment or penalties Mufraddat - مفردات. People of lexicon puts it under the root of جہم(frown)

 

Allah says in quran:

جَهَنَّمَ يَصْلَوْنَهَا فَبِئْسَ الْمِهَادُ

(surah 38 saad verse 56)

Transaltion: Hell!- they will burn therein, - an evil bed (indeed, to lie on)!

3)jaheem جَحِيمَ:jahama  جَحَمَ means stare of lion and lion staring with sharp eyes. Jaham جحم means blazing fire and jaheem  جحيم  means fire that blaze sharply  Mufraddat - مفردات and jaheem means  a sharply ignited fire in a pit. Very hot place

Allah says:

 خُذُوهُ فَاعْتِلُوهُ إِلَىٰ سَوَاءِ الْجَحِيمِ

(surah 44 ad dukhaan verse 47)

Translation:A voice will cry: "Seize ye him and drag him into the midst of the Blazing Fire!

 

4)saqara سَقْر: means sun scorching something and asaqrah اَلسّقرَةُ means very severe heat of sun. hot winds and saqar سَقّر means extremely hot temperature that can scorch the body.

Allah says:

وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا سَقَرُ لَا تُبْقِي وَلَا تَذَرُ لَوَّاحَةٌ لِّلْبَشَرِ

(surah 74 al mudassir verse 27-29)

Transaltion: And what will explain to thee what Hell-Fire is. Naught doth it permit to endure, and naught doth it leave alone!- Darkening and changing the colour of man!

 5)saiieer  سَعير: meaning to ignite fire and make it severe And because of this attribute hell is also called saieer means all the time blazing fire.

Allah says:

وَسَيَصْلَوْنَ سَعِيرًا

(surah 4 an nisa verse 10)

Translation: They will soon be enduring a Blazing Fire!

Some level of hells

 

1)ghaii غَيّ: ghawaa  غوَىٰ  means to be lost and take a wrong way. this is the next level after ضلال zalal.and ghai is  to get astray because of own faults and ghai is also a level  or  valley of hell where misled people will be thrown.

Allah says:

فَخَلَفَ مِن بَعْدِهِمْ خَلْفٌ أَضَاعُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَاتَّبَعُوا الشَّهَوَاتِ ۖ فَسَوْفَ يَلْقَوْنَ غَيًّا

(surah 19 maryum verse 59)

Translation: But after them there followed a posterity who missed prayers and followed after lusts soon, then, will they face Destruction.

2)hawiaa هَاوِيَه:hawa هوَىٰ  and yahwii يَهْوِىْ means to fall from a height at ground and uhwiaa  اُهويَّة  is a deep well and uhwiaah  اُهويَّة s deep pit Munjid - منجد and hawiaah  هَاوِيَه: ia a deep pit in hell.

Allah says:

فَأُمُّهُ هَاوِيَةٌ وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا هِيَهْ نَارٌ حَامِيَةٌ

 (surah  101 al qariyaah verse 9-11)

Translation: Will have his home in a (bottomless) Pit. And what will explain to thee what this is? (It is) a Fire Blazing fiercely!

 

3)hutamah الحُطَمَة: حَطَمَ hatma means trample,to grind.and hatmah is a level in hell.

Allah says in quran:

وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا الْحُطَمَةُ نَارُ اللَّهِ الْمُوقَدَةُ

(surah 104 al humaza verse 5-6)

Translation: And what can make you know what is the Crusher? It is the fire of Allah, [eternally] fueled.
