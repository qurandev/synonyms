27. DHUAAN [smoke, copper, coal]

Synonyms: DUKHAAN, NUHAAS, YAHMOOM

 

27.1 DUKHAAN (دُخَان ): Smoke. Imam Raghib says it is the smoke from flames of fire.

 

Surat Fuşşilat (Explained in Detail) - فصلت سورة

41:11

ثُمَّ اسْتَوَىٰ إِلَى السَّمَاءِ وَهِيَ دُخَانٌ

Then He directed Himself to the heaven while it was smoke

 

27.2 NUHAAS  ( نُحَاس )  means copper. Also, copper colored fire i.e. smoke wrapped with copper colored fire flames; extremely hot and red smoke. Allah says in the quran:

 

Surat Ar-Raĥmān (The Beneficent) - الرحمن سورة

55:25

يُرْسَلُ عَلَيْكُمَا شُوَاظٌ مِّن نَّارٍ وَنُحَاسٌ فَلَا تَنتَصِرَانِ

There will be sent upon you a flame of fire and smoke, and you will not defend yourselves.

 

27.3 YAHMOOM (يَحْمُوْم )   Hamma ( حَمَّ ) means to be extremely black. Humma ( حُمَّة )  means coal; ashes and anything burnt from fire. Yahmoom  (يَحْمُوْم ) is the smoke which is hot, black and contaminated.

Allah says in the Quran:

Surat Al-Wāqi`ah (The Inevitable) - الواقعة  سورة

56:43

وَظِلٍّ مِّن يَحْمُومٍ

And a shade of black smoke

 

Summary:

27.1 DUKHAAN: Smoke

27.2 NUHAAS: Red smoke emerging from copper colored fire

27.3 YAHMOOM: Contaminated black smoke
