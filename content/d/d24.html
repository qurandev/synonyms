Friend-24

Synonyms are:

1)    قَرِيْن

2)    رَفِيْق

3)    وَلِى (مَوَالِى)

4)    صَدِيْق (صِدِّيْق)

5)    خَلِيْل

6)    حَمِيْم

7)    وَلِيْجَة (ولج)

8)    بَطَانَة

9)    خُذُوْل

10)                        اَخْدَان  (خدن)

 

1)qareen قَرِيْن:alqaran (الْقَرَن):a rope through which two camels are tied. And alqaran (الْقَرَن ) means peer, opponent. Match in bravery or knowdlege Munjid - منجد this word can be applied for living things and non living things Alfurooqul-lughwiyyah 235. and qiraan us sadiin قِرْاٰنُ السَّعْدَيْن means,two saad stars accumulating in one zodiac. And as per the wordings of imam raghib, qareen Is that person who is same in age to other person or in bravery, power and in other qualities is equal to the other  Mufraddat - مفردات. This word is usually used in negative sense

Allah says:

حَتَّىٰ إِذَا جَاءَنَا قَالَ يَا لَيْتَ بَيْنِي وَبَيْنَكَ بُعْدَ الْمَشْرِقَيْنِ فَبِئْسَ الْقَرِينُ

 (surah 43 az zukhraf verse 38)

Translation: Until, when he comes to Us [at Judgement], he says [to his companion], "Oh, I wish there was between me and you the distance between the east and west - how wretched a companion."

 

2)rafeeq رَفِيْق:arrifq اَلرِّفْق  means soft behavior. a kind way of treating and that thing from which one can take help. Mirfaqah مِرْفَقَة means small pillow/cushion  Munjid - منجد and rafeeq means sympathetic companion. Soft hearted. One who is Compatible or one who stays near Maqaayeesul-lughat. And the antonym is  anaff عنف and it means hardness/harshness  or stone-hearted Maqaayeesul-lughat.

Allah says:

 

فَأُولَٰئِكَ مَعَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمَ اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِم مِّنَ النَّبِيِّينَ وَالصِّدِّيقِينَ وَالشُّهَدَاءِ وَالصَّالِحِينَ ۚ وَحَسُنَ أُولَٰئِكَ رَفِيقًا

(surah 4 An Nisa verse 69)

Translation: those will be with the ones upon whom Allah has bestowed favor of the prophets, the steadfast affirmers of truth, the martyrs and the righteous. And excellent are those as companions.

 

3)wali وَلِى:aLwlaa الولاء means love, friendship, closeness and kinship Mufraddat - مفردات and affinity Maqaayeesul-lughat.

and wali وَلِى means helper, one who share sorrows, one who help at the times of ups and downs.

Allah says in quran:

اللَّهُ وَلِيُّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا يُخْرِجُهُم مِّنَ الظُّلُمَاتِ إِلَى النُّورِ

(surah 2 Al baqra verse 257)

Translation: Allah is the ally of those who believe. He brings them out from darknesses into the light

An alwalaa لولاء meaning custody (or sainthood) ,heirship of inheritance and mawali مَوَالِى  

(singular مَولى ) means the inheritor of legacy  .for more explanation check “inheritance”

Allah says

وَلِكُلٍّ جَعَلْنَا مَوَالِيَ مِمَّا تَرَكَ الْوَالِدَانِ وَالْأَقْرَبُونَ 

 

(surah 4 An nisa verse 33)

Translation: And for all, We have made heirs to what is left by parents and relatives

 

4)Sadiiq صَدِيْق :true and faithful friend. One who fulfill the ties of friendship. And sadaqqat صداقت means hearts to agree on friendship/amity. Fiqhul lughat - فقه اللغة 98/236

Allah says in Quran:

وَلَا عَلَىٰ أَنفُسِكُمْ أَن تَأْكُلُوا مِن بُيُوتِكُمْ أَوْ بُيُوتِ آبَائِكُمْ أَوْ بُيُوتِ أُمَّهَاتِكُمْ أَوْ بُيُوتِ إِخْوَانِكُمْ أَوْ بُيُوتِ أَخَوَاتِكُمْ أَوْ بُيُوتِ أَعْمَامِكُمْ أَوْ بُيُوتِ عَمَّاتِكُمْ أَوْ بُيُوتِ أَخْوَالِكُمْ أَوْ بُيُوتِ خَالَاتِكُمْ أَوْ مَا مَلَكْتُم مَّفَاتِحَهُ أَوْ صَدِيقِكُمْ

 (surah 24 An nor verse 61)

Translation: nor upon yourselves when you eat from your [own] houses or the houses of your fathers or the houses of your mothers or the houses of your brothers or the houses of your sisters or the houses of your father's brothers or the houses of your father's sisters or the houses of your mother's brothers or the houses of your mother's sisters or [from houses] whose keys you possess or [from the house] of your friend

And sadeeq صِدِّيْق  It’s a superlative noun it means veracious or true friend.

Allah says:

يُوسُفُ أَيُّهَا الصِّدِّيقُ أَفْتِنَا فِي سَبْعِ بَقَرَاتٍ سِمَانٍ يَأْكُلُهُنَّ سَبْعٌ عِجَافٌ

(surah 12 yousaf verse 46)

Translation: [He said], "Joseph, O man of truth, explain to us about seven fat cows eaten by seven [that were] lean.

 

5)khaliil خَلِيْل :khulah  خُلَّةَ  or khilal  خِلال  means heart piercing friendship(heart pervading friendship) Mufraddat - مفردات. strong and deep friendship and khaleel means  honest and true friend and its plural isikhlaa اَخِلَّاء.

Allah says:

وَاتَّخَذَ اللَّهُ إِبْرَاهِيمَ خَلِيلًا

(Surah 4 An nisa verse 124)

Tranlstion: And Allah took Abraham as an intimate friend.

6)hameem حَمِيْم:basically used for three things a) getting black/dark b)getting hot c)to be close  Maqaayeesul-lughat and hama  حمَّ means  to make hot and ham azahira حمَّ الظَّهِيْره  means very severe heat at afternoon Munjid - منجد. Zealous friend or a very close friend.

Allah says:

وَلَا يَسْأَلُ حَمِيمٌ حَمِيمًا يُبَصَّرُونَهُمْ

(surah 70 al mariaj verse 10-11)

Translation: And no friend will ask [anything of] a friend, They will be shown each other

 

7)walijah وَلِيْجَة :walaja وَلَجَ  means to enter. A friend who act as a advisor for sumone matters. One who can be trusted /confidential /reliable. Or another meaning is a person who is attach to other nation also Munjid - منجد. Both the meanings are almost same.

Allah says:

وَلَمَّا يَعْلَمِ اللَّهُ الَّذِينَ جَاهَدُوا مِنكُمْ وَلَمْ يَتَّخِذُوا مِن دُونِ اللَّهِ وَلَا رَسُولِهِ وَلَا الْمُؤْمِنِينَ وَلِيجَةً

(surah 9 at tawbah verse 16)

Translation: while Allah has not yet made evident those among you who strive [for His cause] and do not take other than Allah, His Messenger and the believers as intimates?

8)batanah بَطَانَة :batan بطن means stomach and the inner part of anything. And batanah بطانة   means secret. Lining under the cloth. a person’s family members relatives  and elites Munjid - منجد.. A friend with whom one can share secrets and a friend who is aware of personal matters Mufraddat - مفردات

Allah says in Quran:

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَتَّخِذُوا بِطَانَةً مِّن دُونِكُمْ لَا يَأْلُونَكُمْ خَبَالًا

(surah 3 Al imraan verse 118)

Translation: O you who have believed, do not take as intimates those other than yourselves, for they will not spare you [any] ruin.

9)khuzool خُذُوْل:khazal خَذَل means to not help someone and to leave the partnership. And khuzool خُذُوْل is a type of friend who verbally sings praises of friendship but at the  time of need they leave the friendsMunjid - منجد..  One who betrays.

Allah says in Quran:

وَكَانَ الشَّيْطَانُ لِلْإِنسَانِ خَذُولًا

(surah 25 al furqaan verse 29)

Translation: And ever is Satan, to man, a deserter.

 

10)akdaan اَخْدَان :is the plural of khdaan خدن  or khdnaah خدنة   and it means  person who have lots of friends or fellowship, philanderer,mate ,intimate friend, adulterer/lewd one who hides his friendship and one who wants to fulfill his sexual desires. Used for both masculine and feminine

Allah says:

مُحْصَنَاتٍ غَيْرَ مُسَافِحَاتٍ وَلَا مُتَّخِذَاتِ أَخْدَانٍ

(surah  4 An nisa verse 25)

Transaltion: They should be] chaste, neither [of] those who commit unlawful intercourse randomly nor those who take [secret] lovers.

Summary:

 

1)qareen:peer and same in status and same in age.

2)rafeeq: sympathetic and soft hearted friend

3)sadiqq:the one who fulfill the ties of friendship ,compatible with heart. sadiiq honest friend or the one who fulfill the ties of friendship.

4)wali:helper and the one who share sorrows and defender . mawali the one who is close relative and the inheritor of legacy.

5)khaliil :true and honest friend .whom one  love heartedly.

6)hameem:the one who shows warmth.

7)walijah: advisor, confidential/trustworthy.

8)batanah: with whom one can share secrets. Confidant

9)khuzool:one who betrays at the time of need.

10)akhdaan: intimate friend ,bad friend, or lewd friend.
