9. To correct, rectify (DURUST KARNA)

Synonyms: ASLAHA, SAWWAA

 

9.1 ASLAHA (اَصْلَحَ): The opposite of Islaah is fasaad (incorrectness; the state of decaying, morally corruption) and Aslaha means to correct, repair, or reform the incorrect state.  It also means to reconcile. In Quran:

 

Surat Hūd (Hud) - هود سورة

11:88

إِنْ أُرِيدُ إِلَّا الْإِصْلَاحَ مَا اسْتَطَعْتُ

I only intend reform as much as I am able

 

9.2 SAWWAA (سَوّٰى): Sawaa yaswee (يَسْوِى سَوٰى) means to level (off), even.  Sawwaa means to put something to a proper, correct, upright position. According to Ibn Alfaris it means ‘ِ الْاِعْتِدَال وَ  اَلْاِسْتِقَامَة’ which gives the same meaning as described earlier. Allah says in the Quran:

 

Surat Al-'A`lá (The Most High) - الأعلى سورة

87:2

الَّذِي خَلَقَ فَسَوَّىٰ

Who created and proportioned

 

Summary:

9.1 ASLAHAA: To correct moral corruption

9.2 SAWWAA: Used for correction of creative disorders
