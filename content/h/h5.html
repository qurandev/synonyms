5 HIDAYAT DEYNA -PANA [To give Guidance-To get Guidance]     page 883

Synonyms: HADA هَدَي IHTADA اِهْتَدَي RASHADA رَشَدَ

5.1 IHTADA: HA-DA-YA هدي meaning to guide someone with subtleity and gentleness.to show path of goodness and it's antonym is DALLA ضَلَّ and ADALLA اَضَلَّ.meaning to make someone forget the path-or to misguide it.to make the path of goodness lost or to hide it.(HIDAYAT هدايت antonym DALALAT ضلالت).word HIDAYAT comes in three meanings.

a) natural guidance which ALLAH has intrinsically bestowed on every thing.like a new born baby moving to mother's breast to feed off. said in the QURAN.

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha) - سورة طه

20:50

قَالَ رَبُّنَا الَّذِي أَعْطَىٰ كُلَّ شَيْءٍ خَلْقَهُ ثُمَّ هَدَىٰ

He said, "Our Lord is He who gave each thing its form and then guided [it]."

b) to turn a person's mind from misguidance towards guidanc or from disbelief to belief in Islam or from disobedience to the obedience of ALLAH. this task is done by means of prophets and messengers but the actual act of turning is only by ALLAH's authority/will.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص

28:56

إِنَّكَ لَا تَهْدِي مَنْ أَحْبَبْتَ وَلَٰكِنَّ اللَّهَ يَهْدِي مَن يَشَاءُ ۚ وَهُوَ أَعْلَمُ بِالْمُهْتَدِينَ

Indeed, [O Muhammad], you do not guide whom you like, but Allah guides whom He wills. And He is most knowing of the [rightly] guided.

c)those people who comes to ALLAH's obedience or towards Islam,to keep on showing them the 'straight path' or to keep them following right path.this actual responsibility is of prophets and messengers. then other muslims also share this.yet this task also happens as per the will of ALLAH. as said

Surat Yūnus (Jonah) - سورة يونس

10:43

وَمِنْهُم مَّن يَنظُرُ إِلَيْكَ ۚ أَفَأَنتَ تَهْدِي الْعُمْيَ وَلَوْ كَانُوا لَا يُبْصِرُونَ

And among them are those who look at you. But can you guide the blind although they will not [attempt to] see?

at another place said

Surat Ash-Shūraá (The Consultation) - سورة الشورى

42:52

وَكَذَٰلِكَ أَوْحَيْنَا إِلَيْكَ رُوحًا مِّنْ أَمْرِنَا ۚ مَا كُنتَ تَدْرِي مَا الْكِتَابُ وَلَا الْإِيمَانُ وَلَٰكِن جَعَلْنَاهُ نُورًا نَّهْدِي بِهِ مَن نَّشَاءُ مِنْ عِبَادِنَا ۚ وَإِنَّكَ لَتَهْدِي إِلَىٰ صِرَاطٍ مُّسْتَقِيمٍ

And thus We have revealed to you an inspiration of Our command. You did not know what is the Book or [what is] faith, but We have made it a light by which We guide whom We will of Our servants. And indeed, [O Muhammad], you guide to a straight path -

And IHTADA meaing to find guidance,to follow the right path.ALLAH said

Surat Yūnus (Jonah) - سورة يونس

10:108

قُلْ يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ قَدْ جَاءَكُمُ الْحَقُّ مِن رَّبِّكُمْ ۖ فَمَنِ اهْتَدَىٰ فَإِنَّمَا يَهْتَدِي لِنَفْسِهِ ۖ وَمَن ضَلَّ فَإِنَّمَا يَضِلُّ عَلَيْهَا ۖ وَمَا أَنَا عَلَيْكُم بِوَكِيلٍ

Say, "O mankind, the truth has come to you from your Lord, so whoever is guided is only guided for [the benefit of] his soul, and whoever goes astray only goes astray [in violation] against it. And I am not over you a manager."

5.2 RASHADA:(antonym GHAWA غوَي) meaning that person who comes to the right path and also adheres to good character.and RUSHD رُشُد (antonym GHIYY غيّ) meaning have determination on true path-adherence to it.In the QURAN

Surat Hūd (Hud) - سورة هود

11:78

وَجَاءَهُ قَوْمُهُ يُهْرَعُونَ إِلَيْهِ وَمِن قَبْلُ كَانُوا يَعْمَلُونَ السَّيِّئَاتِ ۚ قَالَ يَا قَوْمِ هَٰؤُلَاءِ بَنَاتِي هُنَّ أَطْهَرُ لَكُمْ ۖ فَاتَّقُوا اللَّهَ وَلَا تُخْزُونِ فِي ضَيْفِي ۖ أَلَيْسَ مِنكُمْ رَجُلٌ رَّشِيدٌ

And his people came hastening to him, and before [this] they had been doing evil deeds. He said, "O my people, these are my daughters; they are purer for you. So fear Allah and do not disgrace me concerning my guests. Is there not among you a man of reason?"

Summary:IHTADA: to be/come/join on the right path.and RASHADA after accepting/following the right path,adhering to good habits and ethics and stay firm on it.
