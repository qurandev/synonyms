۱۰- عقل۔۔۔ عقلمند

Synonyms:

حِجْرٍ, عَقَل, حِلم, أُولِي الْأَلْبَابِ, أُولِي الْأَبْصَارِ, اولِي النُّهَىٰ

 

1. حِجْرٍ : means stone. Any solid and hard thing to be used as a shield/barrier. Also means understanding/perception/sense. حَجَرَmeans to create a boundary around something to keep it safe and stop others from owning it. حِجْرٍ also means ‘sense’ because it helps prevent harmful things.( م-ل) حِجْرٍ  also means barrier, denial, sense (منجد) And ذی حِجْرٍ  means sensible person. (See details in آڑ)

Example: هَلْ فِي ذَٰلِكَ قَسَمٌ لِّذِي حِجْرٍ

Truly in that there is an oath for those who possess understanding. (Al-Fajr 89:5)

 

2. عَقل : عَقَلَ means one with understanding, able to realize/sense one’s mistakes. عَقَلَ also means to stop or refuse. عِقال is the hamper used to tie a camel.( م-ف) Hence sense is used to distinguish between benefit and loss, to adopt ways of avoiding loss.  (فق-ل-۶۵) And be ever ready to accept useful things and knowledge (ilm). Then ponder over it.( م-ف)

Example: وَقَالُوا لَوْ كُنَّا نَسْمَعُ أَوْ نَعْقِلُ مَا كُنَّا فِي أَصْحَابِ السَّعِيرِ

And they will say, "If only we had been listening or reasoning, we would not be among the companions of the Blaze." [Al-Mulk 67:10]

 

3. حِلم : حَلُمَ means to forbear, gentleness.  حَلیم means to be temperate, gentle. حِلم means soberness, calmness. To control oneself when angry and not spill anger.( ج احلام) ( منجد مف ) Therefore حِلم is the sense which keeps one’s temper in control and stops one from flaring up.

Example: أَمْ تَأْمُرُهُمْ أَحْلَامُهُم بِهَٰذَا ۚ أَمْ هُمْ قَوْمٌ طَاغُونَ

Do their minds command them to do this, or are they an outrageous folk? [At-Tur 52:32]

 

4. أُولِي الْأَلْبَابِ : الباب is the plural of لُبّ, which means gist of something, pure essence. (فق ل ۶۶) لبیب means wise. And أُولِي الْأَلْبَابِ are people who when listen to something can immediately perceive its idea. Men of intellect.

Example: إِنَّ فِي ذَٰلِكَ لَذِكْرَىٰ لِأُولِي الْأَلْبَابِ

Indeed in that is a reminder for those of understanding. [Az-Zumar 39:21]

 

5. أُولِي الْأَبْصَارِ : بَصُرَ means to see with eyes and heart both. (See details in دیکھنا) And أُولِي الْأَبْصَارِ means people of vision. People who think deeply whether they are intellects or not.

Example: فَاعْتَبِرُوا يَا أُولِي الْأَبْصَارِ

So take warning, O people of vision. [Al Hashr 59:2]

 

6. اولِي النُّهَىٰ : نھیmeans to reach the limit of something. (م-ل) And نَھُوَ یَنھُوا means to be completely intelligent. And نُھیٰ is the plural ofنُھیہ. اولِي النُّهَىٰ means man of intellect. People of good opinion, experienced.

Example: إِنَّ فِي ذَٰلِكَ لَآيَاتٍ لِّأُولِي النُّهَىٰ

Indeed, in that are signs for those of intelligence. [Taha 20:54]

 

Summary:

 

1. حِجْرٍ: sense that prevents one from harmful things.

2. عَقل: sense that prevents one from harmful things and lets one adopt beneficial things.

3.حِلم : sense that helps control oneself when angry and to not spill anger.

4.أُولِي الْأَلْبَابِ : Intellectual people, who can quickly get the idea of anything told to them.

5.أُولِي الْأَبْصَارِ : People who think deeply.

6. اولِي النُّهَىٰ: Intellectual people with deep perception.
