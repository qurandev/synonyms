عاجز آنا

Synonyms:

عَجَز, عَيَّ, استکان

1. عَجَز: to become unable to do something. Fail to do something. (fa-laam 214). Opposite of عَجَز is قدرت (ability to do something).

Example: قَالَ يَا وَيْلَتَا أَعَجَزْتُ أَنْ أَكُونَ مِثْلَ هَٰذَا الْغُرَابِ

He said, "O woe to me! Have I failed to be like this crow and hide the body of my brother?" [Al-Maidah 5:31]

2. عَيَّ: to become too exhausted to work anymore and unable to fulfill it the second time. (fa-laam 214) Or unable to correctly. (munjad) داء عیاء is a disease which is incurable.(meem fa) عیاء is inferior than عَجَز (munjad) because in عَجَز one is able to work in the start.

Example: أَفَعَيِينَا بِالْخَلْقِ الْأَوَّلِ ۚ بَلْ هُمْ فِي لَبْسٍ مِّنْ خَلْقٍ جَدِيدٍ

Did We fail in the first creation? But they are in confusion over a new creation. [Qaf 50:15]

3. استکان فلانٌ : meaning to express submission. As if he gave up (meem faa) and to become defeated by enemy. To submit (munjad)

Example: فَمَا وَهَنُوا لِمَا أَصَابَهُمْ فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ وَمَا ضَعُفُوا وَمَا اسْتَكَانُوا

But they never lost assurance due to what afflicted them in the cause of Allah , nor did they weaken or submit. [Ali Imran 3:146]

Summary:

1. عَجَز : Unable to do anything

2. عَيَّ : to become too exhausted to work anymore or unable to fulfill it the second time.

3. استکان : to become defeated by the opposite party and express submission.
