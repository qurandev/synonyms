5- ZALEEL KERNA [To Humiliate]

Qur’an has referred them by the words AZALLA (أذل ) AHAANA (أهان ) AKHZA (أخزى ) FAZAHA (فضح ) AZDARAA (اذدری ) and KAYATA (كبت ).

 

 

5.1   AZALLA (  أذلة ): (Opposite of AAZA (اعز)) to make someone superior/Great and there is a strong aspect of being under someone and only a superior can make an insignificant/low, powerless: Allah says

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران 3:26

 وَتُعِزُّ مَنْ تَشَاۗءُ وَتُذِلُّ مَنْ تَشَاۗءُ  

 You honor whom You will and You humble whom You will.

5.2   AAHAANA (اهان ): consider someone low or weak or make someone low or weak because of hatred and insult him and this can be done by the person of same class or equality and as well as superior. Allah says:

Surat Al-Ĥaj (The Pilgrimage) - سورة الحج 22:18

 وَمَنْ يُّهِنِ اللّٰهُ فَمَا لَهٗ مِنْ مُّكْرِمٍ

And he whom Allah humiliates - for him there is no bestower of honour.

 

5.3   AKHZA  (أخزى ): to make humiliated and disgraced which is because of man’s own evil deeds and people know them. It is in Qur’an:

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران 3:194

   وَلَا تُخْزِنَا يَوْمَ الْقِيٰمَةِ

 And do not disgrace us on the Day of Resurrection.

 

5.4   FATHAHA   (  فظح ): to describe someone's defects. To make him fall in others eyes. To make him disgraced before others. It is in Qur’an

 

Surat Al-Ĥijr (The Rocky Tract) - سورة الحجر 15:68

قَالَ اِنَّ هٰٓؤُلَاۗءِ ضَيْفِيْ فَلَا تَفْضَحُوْنِ           

 [Lot] said, "Indeed, these are my guests, so do not shame me.

5.5   AZDARAA  (  ازدری ): to consider someone worthless and contemptible. And AZDARAA (اذدری ) the person who is so much worthless that cannot be counted.   It is in Qur’an

Surat Hūd (Hud) - سورة هود 11:31

  وَّلَآ اَقُوْلُ لِلَّذِيْنَ تَزْدَرِيْٓ اَعْيُنُكُمْ لَنْ يُّؤْتِيَهُمُ اللّٰهُ خَيْرًا    

Nor do I say of those upon whom your eyes look down that Allah will never grant them any good.

5.6   KABATA  (  کبت ): means to return with severity, humiliation, and means to humiliate, to return, to turn, to reject with anger, to kill. This word is used to humiliate and disgrace in the state of anger.  It is in Qur’an

 

Surat Al-Mujādila (The Pleading Woman) - سورة المجادلة 58:5

  اِنَّ الَّذِيْنَ يُحَاۗدُّوْنَ اللّٰهَ وَرَسُوْلَهٗ كُبِتُوْا كَـمَا كُبِتَ الَّذِيْنَ مِنْ قَبْلِهِمْ            

Indeed, those who oppose Allah and His Messenger are abased as those before them were abased.

Summary:

5.1          AZALA: When superior makes the low one under him or disgrace him.

5.2          AHAAN:  To insult due to hatred, humiliate and disgrace. This can be done by the person of equal class.

5.3          AKHZA: To humiliate someone because of his apparent defects.

5.4          FATHAHA: To make someone fall in others eyes by quoting his evil deeds.

5.5          RADI: To assume someone very low or cheap.

5.6          KABAT: To humiliate and disgrace someone in the state of anger.
