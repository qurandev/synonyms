38 KAWLNA [to boil]

Synonyms: ANAA, GHALAA

38.1 ANAA (اَنٰی), YANEE (یَانْیِ): Means boiling of water etc. after reaching an extreme temperature. Boiling of a liquid thing due to extreme temperature. In the Quran

Surah Al-Ghashiya (The overwhelming): سُوۡرَةُ الغَاشِیَة 88:5

تُسۡقَىٰ مِنۡ عَيۡنٍ ءَانِيَةٍ۬

The while they are given, to drink, of a boiling hot spring,

38.2 GHALAA (غَلٰی): Boiling and rising of water due to extreme temperature. To boil. GHAL-AL-QADAR (غلی القدر) means boiling pot. Rising of items in a pot. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Ad-Dukhan (The Smoke): سُوۡرَةُ الدّخان44:45

يَغۡلِى فِى ٱلۡبُطُونِ كَغَلۡىِ ٱلۡحَمِيمِ

boiling inside the bellies. Like the boiling of seething water.
