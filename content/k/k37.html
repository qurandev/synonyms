37 KHARA KERNA – HONA [to stand up]

Synonyms: QAAMA, AQAAMA, WAQAFA, NATAQA, NASHAZA

37.1QAAMA (قَامَ): Means stood up (Opposite JALASA (جَلَسَ) and QA’ADA (قَعَدَ)). A very famous word. Allah says in the Quran.

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:142

 وَإِذَا قَامُوٓاْ إِلَى ٱلصَّلَوٰةِ قَامُواْ كُسَالَىٰ

And when they stand for prayer, they stand lazily,

And AQAMA (اَقَامَ) Means to make someone stand, to raise someone or something, to make stand upright. To arrange a thing vertically. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Kahf (The Cave): سورۃ الکھف 18:77

فَوَجَدَا فِيہَا جِدَارً۬ا يُرِيدُ أَن يَنقَضَّ فَأَقَامَهُ

They found there a wall on the point of falling down, but he set it up straight.

37.2 WAQAFA (وَقَفَ): Means to stop while walking or doing something. To stand without moving. To halt or halt someone.  Used as both transitive and intransitive verb. Allah says in the Quran

(As intransitive verb)

Surah Al-Ana’am (The Cattle): سورۃ الانعام 6:30

 وَلَوۡ تَرَىٰٓ إِذۡ وُقِفُواْ عَلَىٰ رَبِّہِمۡ‌ۚ

If you could but see when they will be made to stand before their Lord.

(As transitive verb)

Surah As-Saaffat (The Aligners) : سُوۡرَةُ الصَّافات37:24

 وَقِفُوهُمۡ‌ۖ إِنَّہُم مَّسۡـُٔولُونَ

 And stop them. They are to be questioned.”

37.3 NATAQA (نَتَقَ): To move or raise or spread something (dictionary al Manjad). Also to stretch something and make it lose. Or to move or shake something so forcefully that it leans to a side without breaking from its roots. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Araf (The heights): سورۃ الأعرَاف 7:171

وَإِذۡ نَتَقۡنَا ٱلۡجَبَلَ فَوۡقَهُمۡ كَأَنَّهُ ۥ ظُلَّةٌ۬ وَظَنُّوٓاْ أَنَّهُ ۥ وَاقِعُۢ بِہِمۡ

And when We suspended the mountain over them, as if it was an umbrella, and they thought it would fall on them

37.4 NASHAZA (نَشَزَ): To get on feet, to stand up. NASHAZ-AR-RUJAL (نشز الرجل) means the man who was sitting got up on his feet. NASHAZ-AN-MAKANA (نشز عن مکانہ) means he got up and started to move. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Mujadila (The Pleading Women) سُوۡرَةُ المجَادلة 58:11

وَإِذَا قِيلَ ٱنشُزُواْ فَٱنشُزُواْ

And when you are told to rise up, rise up

Summary:

37.1 QAAMA: (Opposite JALASA and QA’ADA) a popular word for standing up., AQAAMA to make something upright,

37.2 WAQAFA: To stand up without moving. To stop for a moment while walking, to halt

37.3 NATAQA: To cause something to bend.

37.4 NASHAZA: To get up on feet and move. Stand up
