35 KHILANA [to feed]

Synonyms:  AT’AMA, RAZAQA

35.1 AT’AMA (اَطْعَمَ): To feed a human.  In the Quran

Surah Ash-Shura’ (The Poets): صورۃ الشُّعْراء 26:79

 وَٱلَّذِى هُوَ يُطۡعِمُنِى وَيَسۡقِينِ

He who feeds me, and waters me.

35.2 RAZAQA (رَزَقَ): Used with a wide range of meanings. And refers to feeding both physical body or soul. (For further detail see KHAWRAAQ [food]). Allah says in the Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:5

 وَٱرۡزُقُوهُمۡ فِيہَا وَٱكۡسُوهُمۡ

but feed and clothe them therewith
