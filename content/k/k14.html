14. KARWA [bitter, painful]

Synonyms: AMARRA, UJAAJ, KHAMT

14.1 AMARRA(اَمَرَّ) : MARRA (مَرَّ) (MARARAH(مَرَارَۃ)) means bitter or painful, MURRUN (مُرٌ) means bitter. AMARRA (اَمَرَّ) means extremely bitter or intolerable (dictionary Al Manjad). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Qamar (The moon): سورۃ القَمَر 54:46

بَلِ ٱلسَّاعَةُ مَوۡعِدُهُمۡ وَٱلسَّاعَةُ أَدۡهَىٰ وَأَمَرُّ

The Hour is their appointed time—the Hour is more disastrous, and most bitter.

14.2UJAAJ (اَجَاج): Means bitter or saline water. AJJ-AL-MAA’ (اَجَّ الْمَاءَ) means to make water bitter or saline. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Waqiah (The Inevitable): سُوۡرَةُ الواقِعَة 56:70

لَوۡ نَشَآءُ جَعَلۡنَـٰهُ أُجَاجً۬ا فَلَوۡلَا تَشۡكُرُونَ

If We will, We can make it salty. Will you not be thankful?

14.3 KHAMT (خَمْط): means sour and acerbic, and also a thorny and bitter tree. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Saba (Sheba): سورۃ سَبَإ 34:16

 وَبَدَّلۡنَـٰهُم بِجَنَّتَيۡہِمۡ جَنَّتَيۡنِ ذَوَاتَىۡ أُڪُلٍ خَمۡطٍ۬

 We substituted their two gardens with two gardens of bitter fruits

Synonyms:

14.1 AMARRU: Purely bitter or extremely bitter.

14.2 UJAAJ: Saline or bitter water

14.3 KHAMT: Bitter or sour, unsavory
