10. KITAAB [Book]

Synonyms: KITAB, ASFAAR, SIJIL, NUSKHAH, ZUBUR, SUHUF

10.1 KITAB (کِتَاب): Means a written object, letter, manuscript of deed, renowned word for book (plural KUTUB (کتب) ). In the Quran

Surah An-Naml (The ant): سورۃ النَّمل 27:28

ٱذۡهَب بِّكِتَـٰبِى هَـٰذَا

Go with this letter of mine

 

10.2 ASFAAR (اَسْفَار): (singular SIFR (سَفْر)) SIFR (سِفْر) means a big book. One of the volumes of Torah (dictionary Al Manjad). SUFR (سُفْر) means to declare or reveal something. SIFR (سِفْر) is a book that reveals the truth. A large scholia or explanation of Torah. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Jumua (Friday): سُوۡرَةُ الجُمُعَة 62:5

مَثَلُ ٱلَّذِينَ حُمِّلُواْ ٱلتَّوۡرَٮٰةَ ثُمَّ لَمۡ يَحۡمِلُوهَا كَمَثَلِ ٱلۡحِمَارِ يَحۡمِلُ أَسۡفَارَۢا‌ۚ

 The example of those who were entrusted with the Torah, but then failed to uphold it, is like the donkey carrying works of literature

 

10.3 SIJIL(سِجِل): Means a register of instructions, a register of laws and claims which are kept by a judge. A judicial register. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Anbiya (the prophets): سورۃ الانَبیاء 21:104

يَوۡمَ نَطۡوِى ٱلسَّمَآءَ ڪَطَىِّ ٱلسِّجِلِّ لِلۡڪُتُبِ‌ۚ

 On the Day when We fold the heaven, like the folding of a book.

 

10.4 NUSKHAH (نُسْخَة): NASAKH (نَسَخَ) means to erase or reject. NASAKH-AL-KITAAB (نسخ الکتاب) means to copy a book word-by-word. NUSKHAH (نُسْخَة) means anything that can be copied from and which is also copied. Every NUSKHAH (نُسْخَة) is a book but every book is not a NUSKHAH. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Araf (The heights): سورۃ الأعرَاف 7:154

 وَفِى نُسۡخَتِہَا هُدً۬ى وَرَحۡمَةٌ۬ لِّلَّذِينَ هُمۡ لِرَبِّہِمۡ يَرۡهَبُونَ

 In their transcript is guidance and mercy for those in awe of their Lord.

 

10.5 ZUBUR (زُبُر): ZUBRAH (زِبْرۃ) means a large sheet of iron (plural ZUBAR (زُبَر) ), ZABAR-AL-KITAB (زَبَرالکِتَاب) means to write a book in large font. ZIBR (زِبْر) is actually a verbal noun but is also used as a noun and gives the meaning of a noun and means a book (plural ZUBUR (زُبُر)). ZABOOR (زَبُور) is the Holy Book sent on DAVID (Hazrat Daood A.S). MIZBAR (مِزْبر) means a pen (dictionary Al Manjad). ZUBARA (زُبَرَ) means to write on a stone and basically refers to thickness and grossness. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Ash-Shura’ (The Poets): صورۃ الشُّعْراء 26:196

 وَإِنَّهُ ۥ لَفِى زُبُرِ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ

And it is in the scriptures of the ancients.

 

10.6 SUHUF (صُحُف): (singular SAHEEFAH(صَحِیْفَۃ)) SAHEEFA (صَحِیْفَۃ) means outspread thing that can write or  be written on. SAHEEFA (صَحِیْفَۃ) also means a written paper or leaf. SIHAFA (صِحَافَة) means journalism and ISHAAF (اَصْحَفَ) means to compile written papers into a book. MUSHAF (مُصْحَف) means a book. A bound scripture. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Ala (The Most High): سُوۡرَةُ الاٴعلی 87:18-19

 إِنَّ هَـٰذَا لَفِى ٱلصُّحُفِ ٱلۡأُولَىٰ صُحُفِ إِبۡرَٲهِيمَ وَمُوسَىٰ

This is in the former scriptures. The Scriptures of Abraham and Moses.

 

Summary

10.1 KITAAB: A written object. Famous word for book.

10.2 ASFAAR: Scholia and explanation of large books (Torah)

10.3 SIJIL: Written notes or books that can be kept as a record.

10.4 NUSKHAH: A copied book or one that can be copied from

10.5 ZUBUR: A book written in large font

10.6 SUHUF: Written leaves of paper that can be compiled into a book.
