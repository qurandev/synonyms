SAAD

1: Saaf Karna (To clean)

 

Synonyms: Mahhsa,Tahra,saffa,masah

 

1.1  Massaha: To clean such thing in which synthesis is absorbed. It is used to separate the compounds in Madi things. Like (Mahasa dhab).To remove away the mixture and Alaish of other compounds from the gold after putting into cruset. Literally it means to clean and purify it after someone makes to suffer from grief and sorrows. Allah (swt) said:

 

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran)

3:141

وَلِيُمَحِّصَ اللَّهُ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَيَمْحَقَ الْكَافِرِينَ

And that Allâh may test (or purify) the believers (from sins) and destroy the disbelievers

 

1.2  Tahar: (its opposite is Nasa means uncleanliness) means to wash away the uncleanliness Tuhur means pure from uncleanliness and impurities. Clean-Translucent-Distilled .Allah (swt) said:

Surat-Al-Furqan:

25:48

وَأَنْزَلْنَا مِنَ السَّمَاءِ مَاءً طَهُورًا

and We send down pure water from the sky.

 

1.3  Saffa: To clean such things from mixture Alaish which is possible without chemical way means mixture, like to distill the honey from the hive and Moom and to separate the salt and water after boiling the salty water. Allah (swt) said:

Surat Muhammad:

47:15

وَأَنْهَارٌ مِنْ عَسَلٍ مُصَفًّى

And rivers of clarified honey (clear and pure)

 

1.4  Masaha: means to remove the dust with hand, to dust , to wipe away, as it is said:

Surat-Saad:

38:33

فَطَفِقَ مَسْحًا بِالسُّوقِ وَالْأَعْنَاقِ

Then he (Prophet Suleman (a) ) began to pass his hand over their legs and their necks (till the end of the display).

And in Juristic meaning masah means to rub or put pure clay or water then remove it by dusting.Allah (swt) said:

 

Surat-al-Maidah:

5:6

 

فَتَيَمَّمُوا صَعِيدًا طَيِّبًا فَامْسَحُوا بِوُجُوهِكُمْ وَأَيْدِيكُمْ مِنْهُ

 

Summary:

1: Mahhsa: to remove the mixture from the compounds and clean it

2:Tahhara: to clean the external impurities with water, etc

3: Saffa: To remove the mixture from Ameza

4:Masha: to wipe away the dust or Alaish with hand-to dust-to clean
