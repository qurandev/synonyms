5 Sullah (Reconciliation)

 

Synonyms: Suluh,Salama

 

5.1 : Suluh ( pl. islah) its opposite is Fassad means demolition and Islah means to correct the demolition.& suluh is mutual co-operation in  between those two groups who had demolition , & dispute earlier. Allah (SWT) Said:

 

Soorat-An-Nisa :4:128

 

وَإِنِ امْرَأَةٌ خَافَتْ مِنْ بَعْلِهَا نُشُوزًا أَوْ إِعْرَاضًا فَلَا جُنَاحَ عَلَيْهِمَا أَنْ يُصْلِحَا بَيْنَهُمَا صُلْحًا ۚ وَالصُّلْحُ خَيْرٌ

If a wife fears cruelty or desertion on her husband's part, there is no blame on them if they arrange an amicable settlement between themselves; and such settlement is best;

 

 

5.2:Salam: Assalaam, means peace ,obediently and salaam means to get rid off ,be saved, the reconciler is also called Salaam, and also that reconciliation and co-operation which is aroused before the dispute and demolition. Be obedient, Allah (swt) said:

 

Soorat-An-Nisa :4:90

 

فَإِنِ اعْتَزَلُوكُمْ فَلَمْ يُقَاتِلُوكُمْ وَأَلْقَوْا إِلَيْكُمُ السَّلَمَ فَمَا جَعَلَ اللَّهُ لَكُمْ عَلَيْهِمْ سَبِيلًا

therefore if they withdraw from you but fight you, not and (instead) send you (guarantees of) peace, then Allah hath opened no way for you (to war against them).

 

Summary: the co-operation before the demolition and dispute is called Salam and the word sulah is used for the mutual co-operation after the demolition.
