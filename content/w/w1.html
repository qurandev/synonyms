Translation: pages 871,872 and part of 873.

و

1.كثير (katheer):opposite:قليل (qaleel).meaning:abundant.

It is used for quantity as well as volume,and in both physical/material

as well as literal sense.Allah SWT says:

and whoever has been given wisdom has certainly been given much good.(2:269)

In the verse quoted aboveكثير (katheer) has been used for volume. In the segment of the verse quoted below it has been used in terms of quantity:

So We gave the ones who believed among them their reward, but many of them are defiantly disobedient.57:27

Whereas in كوثر (kauthar) there is a lot of exaggeration. تکاثر الشئی means abundance of something and رَجُلُن كَاثِرُن means a rich man and also خِير كَثِير (much goodness).كوثر isalso the name of a stream in Jannah (Mf).Allah SWT says:

Indeed, We have granted you, [O Muhammad], al-Kawthar.:108:1

2.جمّ: meaning:collection of something in one place in a large quantity (M-L)جمّ البِثَر means A well with abundant water.And جمَّ المِكيَالmeaning to fill the measuring cup to the brim (Mnjd).This is used in both physical/material and literal senses.Allah SWT says:

And you love wealth with immense love.:89:20

3.مَركُوم : is fromرَكَمَ meaning tofold something on itself.Then to fold it a third time is also considered the same.Similarly when something is collected in a huge quantity or is in a pile then it is رُكَام and مَركُوم and رَكَمَ السّحَاب means the cloud has thickened (M-Q) andسحَابُ مَركُوم means thick cloud.Allah SWT says:

Page 872

[This is] so that Allah may distinguish the wicked from the good and place the wicked some of them upon others and heap them all together:8:37

4.لُبِدَ:andلِبد and لبده mean neatly folded hair or wool.Woolen felt rug.

مَالٌ لُبدٌ:meaning abundant wealth.And لَبَّدَ شعرُهُ means

gluing together hair to make like a woolen felt rug.

لبد الشَىء:means something like a woolen felt rug.(Mnjd)

That is, to gather something that is scattered and make it compact.Allah SWT says:

He says, "I have spent wealth in abundance.":90:6

And when it is referred to popular opinion,it would mean:

To be over crowded.Allah Swt says:

And that when the Servant of Allah stood up supplicating Him, they almost became about him a compacted mass."72:19

5. رَغَدَ: is only used for Provision or Food.To eat freely/sufficiently or to get sufficient provisions/livelihood.(M-L) Allah SWT says:

and eat therefrom in [ease and] abundance from wherever you will. 2:35

6. غَدَقَ :meaning to rain great quantity and as a blessing.

(M-L)Allah SWT says:

And [ Allah revealed] that if they had remained straight on the way, We would have given them abundant provision:72:1

7. ثَجّاج

: ثَجَّ meaning heavy downpour and flowing of water.(Mf) and

ثَجّاج meaning surge of water.(M-Q).Allah SWT says:

And sent down, from the rain clouds, pouring water.78:14

8. موفُور

:وَفَر :is used for completion or abundance of something.(M-L)and

وَفَّر means to complete,to increase (Mnjd) and وَفَرَ جَزَاءُهُ

meaning paid him back in full measure and gave

even more. (Mf).Allah SWT says:

[ Allah ] said, "Go, for whoever of them follows you, indeed Hell will be the recompense of you - an ample recompense.17:63.

Summary:

1. كثير : it is used quite commonly.The abundance in terms of

volume or quantity in anything.

2. جمّ : collection of something in a large quantity in one place.

3. مَركُوم : folded up into a pile.

4. لُبِدَ : the gathering of scattered pieces of something into a pile in

one place.

5. رَغَدَ : only for abundance of food and provisions.

6. غَدَقَ : for abundance of water,when it is also beneficial.

7. ثَجّاج : continous and abundant downpour and flowing of water.

8. موفُور : is used for completion with some addition.
