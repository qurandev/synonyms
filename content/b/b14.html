14 BANJH [Infertile]

Synonyms 3AQIR(عَاقِر), and 3AQIYM(عَقِيْم)

14.1 3AQIR(عَاقِر):root 3QR(عقر)contains the meaning of inflicting injury or wound.ALKALBU AL3AQUWR(الْكَلْبُ الْعَقُوْر ) is a dog that bites.and 3AQAR ANNAKHLAH(عَقَرَ النَّخْلَة) means to cut palm tree from root.In the Quran

Surat Ash-Shams (The Sun) - سورة الشمس

91:14

فَكَذَّبُوهُ فَعَقَرُوهَا فَدَمْدَمَ عَلَيْهِمْ رَبُّهُم بِذَنبِهِمْ فَسَوَّاهَا

But they denied him and hamstrung her. So their Lord brought down upon them destruction for their sin and made it equal [upon all of them].

and AL3AQR(اَلْعَقْر)is also said to someone's last child after which there is no child.so 3AQIR is used for such a woman who was giving birth before but after wound in womb or menopause or some other disease,is unable to give birth now.also this word is used for both man and woman.In the Quran

Surat Maryam (Mary) - سورة مريم

19:5

وَإِنِّي خِفْتُ الْمَوَالِيَ مِن وَرَائِي وَكَانَتِ امْرَأَتِي عَاقِرًا فَهَبْ لِي مِن لَّدُنكَ وَلِيًّا

And indeed, I fear the successors after me, and my wife has been barren, so give me from Yourself an heir

14.2 3AQIYM(عَقِيْم):like word 3AQIR is also used for both male and female. 3AQIYM is also said to that sandy part or patch of earth which is infertile. and RIYHUN 3AQIYM( الرِّيحَ الْعَقِيمَ) is such a wind which is devoid of good. In that meaning this word is used in the Holy QURAN:

Surat Adh-Dhāriyāt (The Winnowing Winds) - سورة الذاريات

51:41

وَفِي عَادٍ إِذْ أَرْسَلْنَا عَلَيْهِمُ الرِّيحَ الْعَقِيمَ

And in 'Aad [was a sign], when We sent against them the barren wind.

and 3AQIYM is that man who doesn't have sperm or his sperm cells are dead.likewise 3AQIYM is such a women who doesn't accept man's sperm at all.so 3AQIYM is that man or woman who doesn't have any children at all. ALLAH said

Surat Ash-Shūraá (The Consultation) - سورة الشورى

42:50

أَوْ يُزَوِّجُهُمْ ذُكْرَانًا وَإِنَاثًا ۖ وَيَجْعَلُ مَن يَشَاءُ عَقِيمًا ۚ إِنَّهُ عَلِيمٌ قَدِيرٌ

Or He makes them [both] males and females, and He renders whom He wills barren. Indeed, He is Knowing and Competent.

Summary

3AQIR who used to have children but stop having them later on.and 3QIYM is that who absolutley had no child born
