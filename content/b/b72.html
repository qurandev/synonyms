72:Disinclintion:

 

 

Raghiba unn   رَغِبَ عن,hasoor  حَصُور,zaahid   زاهِد are the words used in the Quran:

1.        Raghiba unn   رَغِبَ عن :raghib   رَغِبَmeans to have a desire for  something or an inclination towards something. When this word is connected to unn  عن, then its means disinclination.

Allah says in the Quran. 2/130: Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

 

وَمَن يَرْغَبُ عَن مِّلَّةِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ إِلَّا مَن سَفِهَ نَفْسَهُ ۚ وَلَقَدِ اصْطَفَيْنَاهُ فِي الدُّنْيَا ۖ وَإِنَّهُ فِي الْآخِرَةِ لَمِنَ الصَّالِحِينَ

 

And who would be averse to the religion of Abraham except one who makes a fool of himself. And We had chosen him in this world, and indeed he, in the Hereafter, will be among the righteous.

 

2.        Hasoor  حَصُور: hasar  حصرmeans to engulf someone after coming closer. Hasir حَصِر means to keep a distance or stay away from something. Hasoor   حَصُورis such a person who has no inclination towards females & stays away from them.

Allah says in the Quran: 3/39: Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران

 

فَنَادَتْهُ الْمَلَائِكَةُ وَهُوَ قَائِمٌ يُصَلِّي فِي الْمِحْرَابِ أَنَّ اللَّهَ يُبَشِّرُكَ بِيَحْيَىٰ مُصَدِّقًا بِكَلِمَةٍ مِّنَ اللَّهِ وَسَيِّدًا وَحَصُورًا وَنَبِيًّا مِّنَ الصَّالِحِينَ

 

So the angels called him while he was standing in prayer in the chamber, "Indeed, Allah gives you good tidings of John, confirming a word from Allah and [who will be] honorable, abstaining [from women], and a prophet from among the righteous."

 

3.        Zaahid   زاهِد: zahad  زَهَدَ means to be selfless & leave something. Zaheed  زهيد means something that holds very less value. Zaahid fil shaee   زاهد فى الشئ means to have no inclination towards something or be content with something little.

 

Quran says: 12/20: Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

 

 

وَشَرَوْهُ بِثَمَنٍ بَخْسٍ دَرَاهِمَ مَعْدُودَةٍ وَكَانُوا فِيهِ مِنَ الزَّاهِدِينَ

 

And they sold him for a reduced price - a few dirhams - and they were, concerning him, of those content with little.

 

Summary:

 

1.        Raghib unn   رَغِبَ عن: common word used for disinclination

2.        Hasoor   حَصُور: a person who has no desire for females

3.        Zaahid   زاهِد: someone who does not have an inclination towards something or has no desire for something because he thinks that its of no value.
