30 BADLAH DAYNA [To Give Reward]

Synonyms: JAZA(جَزَا),CAWWABA(ثَوَّبَ),ACABA(اثَابَ), and 3AZZABA(عَذَّبَ) and DANA(دَانَ)

30.1-2 JAZA(جَزَي) as JAZA-(جزاء) and ACAB and CAWWABA have been mentioned in BADLAH[Reward],now 3AZZABA and DANA are detailed as follows:

30.3 3AZZABA(عَذَّبَ):and 3AZUBA(عَذُبَ)meaning to stop eating food because of intense thirst and 3AZB(عَذْب) means sweet and pleasant drink or food and MA-UB 3AZBUN(مَاءٌ عَذْبٌ)meaning cold and pleasant water and A3ZAB(اَعْذَبْ) meaning to reach sweet water.but this word when in BAB TAF3YL(باب تفعيل) left its basic meanning and 3AZZABA meaning agonizing pain or to give punishment or torment.as per Abu Halal its definition is ALALAMA ALMUSTAQIRR(الالَمَ الْمُسْتَقِّرّ ) i.e 'solid and sustained grief'. ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء

17:15

مَّنِ اهْتَدَىٰ فَإِنَّمَا يَهْتَدِي لِنَفْسِهِ ۖ وَمَن ضَلَّ فَإِنَّمَا يَضِلُّ عَلَيْهَا ۚ وَلَا تَزِرُ وَازِرَةٌ وِزْرَ أُخْرَىٰ ۗ وَمَا كُنَّا مُعَذِّبِينَ حَتَّىٰ نَبْعَثَ رَسُولًا

Whoever is guided is only guided for [the benefit of] his soul. And whoever errs only errs against it. And no bearer of burdens will bear the burden of another. And never would We punish until We sent a messenger.

30.4:DANA(دَانَ):ADDYN(الدّين) is used in vast and wide meaning.in short its has four meaning a)Complete Mastery b)Complete Slavery and obedience. In the QURAN

أَلَا لِلَّهِ الدِّينُ الْخَالِصُ ۚ وَالَّذِينَ اتَّخَذُوا مِن دُونِهِ أَوْلِيَاءَ مَا نَعْبُدُهُمْ إِلَّا لِيُقَرِّبُونَا إِلَى اللَّهِ زُلْفَىٰ إِنَّ اللَّهَ يَحْكُمُ بَيْنَهُمْ فِي مَا هُمْ فِيهِ يَخْتَلِفُونَ ۗ إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يَهْدِي مَنْ هُوَ كَاذِبٌ كَفَّارٌ

Unquestionably, for Allah is the pure religion. And those who take protectors besides Him [say], "We only worship them that they may bring us nearer to Allah in position." Indeed, Allah will judge between them concerning that over which they differ. Indeed, Allah does not guide he who is a liar and [confirmed] disbeliever.

so aforementioned ayah contains both the meaning.

Summary

30.1 JAZA:to give complete,just reward

30.2 ACAB and CWB:generally good reward of good deeds

30.3 3AZZABA:for stern punishment

30.4 DAN:To emphasize the aspect of Master and Slave in the context of reward and punishment.
