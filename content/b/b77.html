77.Unlucky:

 

 

Mehroom    محروم& shaqqiyyan  شقيًّا are the words used.

 

1.        Mehroom  محروم : haram  حرام is something forbidden.Sometimes it is because of shariah reasons,sometimes forceful (by force) or sometimes تسخيرى   .Mehroom   محروم is such a person who is deprived of well being or prosperity & wealth. Mehroom  محروم is probably used in the Quran three times & every time it is used for poverty.

Allah says in the Quran:70/24-25: Surat Al-Ma`ārij (The Ascending Stairways) - سورة المعارج

 

وَالَّذِينَ فِي أَمْوَالِهِمْ حَقٌّ مَّعْلُومٌ لِّلسَّائِلِ وَالْمَحْرُومِ

And those within whose wealth is a known right , For the petitioner and the deprived -

 

2.        Shaqqiyyan شقيًّا : The  opposite of shaqee   شقى is saeed  سعيد . Saeed   سعيد is gifted by goodness/good luck. & shaqee   شقى is a person who is deprived of good luck.

The quran says: 19/4: Surat Maryam (Mary) - سورة مريم

 

قَالَ رَبِّ إِنِّي وَهَنَ الْعَظْمُ مِنِّي وَاشْتَعَلَ الرَّأْسُ شَيْبًا وَلَمْ أَكُن بِدُعَائِكَ رَبِّ شَقِيًّا

He said, "My Lord, indeed my bones have weakened, and my head has filled with white, and never have I been in my supplication to You, my Lord, unhappy.

 

Summary:

Mehroom محروم is used for an unlucky person who is deprived of wealth & prosperity & shaqqiyyan   شقيًّا is used for being deprived of all the goodness.
