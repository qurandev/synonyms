31 BADLHA LAYNA [To Take Revenge]

Synonyms: 3AQABA(عَاقَبَ),INTAQAMA(اِنتَقَمَ),INTASARA(اِنْتَصَرَ)

31.1 3AQABA(عَاقَبَ):and 3QB(عقب) means heel and behind and 3QB means to follow in pursuit and 3AQAB means to follow in pursuit of someone and to subject them to accountability and to punish. ALLAH said

Surat An-Naĥl (The Bee) - سورة النحل

16:126

وَإِنْ عَاقَبْتُمْ فَعَاقِبُوا بِمِثْلِ مَا عُوقِبْتُم بِهِ ۖ وَلَئِن صَبَرْتُمْ لَهُوَ خَيْرٌ لِّلصَّابِرِينَ

And if you punish [an enemy, O believers], punish with an equivalent of that with which you were harmed. But if you are patient - it is better for those who are patient.

31.2 INTAQAMA(اِنتَقَمَ):NQM(نقم) means to conside something bad and to find flaws in it. In the QURAN

Surat Al-Burūj (The Mansions of the Stars) - سورة البروج

85:8

وَمَا نَقَمُوا مِنْهُمْ إِلَّا أَن يُؤْمِنُوا بِاللَّهِ الْعَزِيزِ الْحَمِيدِ

And they resented them not except because they believed in Allah , the Exalted in Might, the Praiseworthy,

and INTEQAM(انتقام) means to punish or take revenge from someone owing to being angry at their unpleasant deeds.and as per Abu Hilal 3skari antonym of INTEQAM is IN3AM(انعام)[Gift/Prize] and INTEQAM means to seize the gift/prize

Surat As-Sajdah (The Prostration) - سورة السجدة

32:22

وَمَنْ أَظْلَمُ مِمَّن ذُكِّرَ بِآيَاتِ رَبِّهِ ثُمَّ أَعْرَضَ عَنْهَا ۚ إِنَّا مِنَ الْمُجْرِمِينَ مُنتَقِمُونَ

And who is more unjust than one who is reminded of the verses of his Lord; then he turns away from them? Indeed We, from the criminals, will take retribution.

31.3 INTASARA(اِنْتَصَرَ):NASARA(نَصَرَ) meaning to help someone to repel attrocities and injustice. and INTASARA(اِنْتَصَرَ) means to take revenge for repeling harm and of attorcities and injustice done to someone. ALLAH said:

Surat Ash-Shūraá (The Consultation) - سورة الشورى

42:39

وَالَّذِينَ إِذَا أَصَابَهُمُ الْبَغْيُ هُمْ يَنتَصِرُونَ

And those who, when tyranny strikes them, they defend themselves,

at another place Noah(AS) supplicate humbly to his Lord.

Surat Al-Qamar (The Moon) - سورة القمر

54:10

فَدَعَا رَبَّهُ أَنِّي مَغْلُوبٌ فَانتَصِرْ

So he invoked his Lord, "Indeed, I am overpowered, so help."

Summary

31.1 3AQABA:To take revenge from someone by following in pursuit

31.2 INTIQAM:To punish or take revenge or take back gift from someone owing to being angry on their bad deeds

31.3 INTISAR:To take revenge of attrocity and injustice. whether took by the victim of injustice or by someone else who is helping the victim.
