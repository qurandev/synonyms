75: Anxious/restless/disquiet:

 

Faragha  فَرَغَ,jazeea  جَزِعَ,fazeea  فَزِعَ  ,karub  كَرُب,hal’a  هَلْع,iztarra   اِضطَرَّ,istafazza   استَفَزَّ are the words used.

 

1.        Faragha  فَرَغَ:means being idle(not doing anything) . its opposite is shaghal  شَغَلَ ,means being busy in some work. Qalbul faarigh قلبُ فَارِغَ  means such a state when the person is not involved in any activity. Meaning lose patience & become restless or anxious.

Allah says in the Quran: 28/10: Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص

 

وَأَصْبَحَ فُؤَادُ أُمِّ مُوسَىٰ فَارِغًا ۖ إِن كَادَتْ لَتُبْدِي بِهِ لَوْلَا أَن رَّبَطْنَا عَلَىٰ قَلْبِهَا لِتَكُونَ مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ  

And the heart of Moses' mother became empty [of all else]. She was about to disclose [the matter concerning] him had We not bound fast her heart that she would be of the believers.

 

2.        Jazee’a  جَزِعَ:  This word means to be  impatient .its opposite is patience. This word is used when a person starts saying sad things rather than have patience on sad or unpleasant occurance/situation.

 

Allah says in the Quran: 14/21: Surat 'Ibrāhīm (Abrahim) - سورة ابراهيم

 

وَبَرَزُوا لِلَّهِ جَمِيعًا فَقَالَ الضُّعَفَاءُ لِلَّذِينَ اسْتَكْبَرُوا إِنَّا كُنَّا لَكُمْ تَبَعًا فَهَلْ أَنتُم مُّغْنُونَ عَنَّا مِنْ عَذَابِ اللَّهِ مِن شَيْءٍ ۚ قَالُوا لَوْ هَدَانَا اللَّهُ لَهَدَيْنَاكُمْ ۖ سَوَاءٌ عَلَيْنَا أَجَزِعْنَا أَمْ صَبَرْنَا مَا لَنَا مِن مَّحِيصٍ

 

And they will come out [for judgement] before Allah all together, and the weak will say to those who were arrogant, "Indeed, we were your followers, so can you avail us anything against the punishment of Allah ?" They will say, "If Allah had guided us, we would have guided you. It is all the same for us whether we show intolerance or are patient: there is for us no place of escape."

 

3.        Fazee’a   فَزِعَ: means to be frightened. According to Imaam raaghib, it is a type of Jazee’a  جَزِعَ. Meaning when the feeling of jazee’a  جَزِعَ includes anxiety, then it is the feeling of fazeea’a  فَزِعَ

 

Allah says in the Quran: 27/89 Surat An-Naml (The Ant) - سورة النمل

 

مَن جَاءَ بِالْحَسَنَةِ فَلَهُ خَيْرٌ مِّنْهَا وَهُم مِّن فَزَعٍ يَوْمَئِذٍ آمِنُونَ

 

Whoever comes [at Judgement] with a good deed will have better than it, and they, from the terror of that Day, will be safe.

 

4.        Karub كَرُب : such restlessness, which has the element of sadness in it as well is called karub. Anxiety,restlessness, disquiet.

Allah says in the Quran:21/76: Surat Al-'Anbyā' (The Prophets) - سورة الأنبياء

 

وَنُوحًا إِذْ نَادَىٰ مِن قَبْلُ فَاسْتَجَبْنَا لَهُ فَنَجَّيْنَاهُ وَأَهْلَهُ مِنَ الْكَرْبِ الْعَظِيمِ

 

And [mention] Noah, when he called [to Allah ] before [that time], so We responded to him and saved him and his family from the great flood.

 

5.        Halaa’a  هَلْع: According to the people of dictionary, this is the extreme form of jazee’a جَزِعَ .  To be impatient & lose hope.

 

In the Quran: 70/19-20: Surat Al-Ma`ārij (The Ascending Stairways) - سورة المعارج

 

إِنَّ الْإِنسَانَ خُلِقَ هَلُوعًا إِذَا مَسَّهُ الشَّرُّ جَزُوعًا

 

Indeed, mankind was created anxious: When evil touches him, impatient,

 

6.        Iztarra’a   اِضطَرَّ: zarra ضَرَّ & zurra  ضُرَّ means to harm someone. Izraah alalamar   اضره  على الامر means to force someone to do something. Iztarr  اِضطَر means to force someone. To make someone needy. (dependant on someone). So iztaraar   اضطرار is such a restlessness that associates needs with it.

 Allah says in the Quran 2/173: Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

 

إِنَّمَا حَرَّمَ عَلَيْكُمُ الْمَيْتَةَ وَالدَّمَ وَلَحْمَ الْخِنزِيرِ وَمَا أُهِلَّ بِهِ لِغَيْرِ اللَّهِ ۖ فَمَنِ اضْطُرَّ غَيْرَ بَاغٍ وَلَا عَادٍ فَلَا إِثْمَ عَلَيْهِ ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ

He has only forbidden to you dead animals, blood, the flesh of swine, and that which has been dedicated to other than Allah . But whoever is forced [by necessity], neither desiring [it] nor transgressing [its limit], there is no sin upon him. Indeed, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful.

 

7.        Istaffazza  استَفَزَّ : fazza فزَّ means to frighten someone,to make someone anxious & throw out from a group etc. Istafazza  استَفَزَّ means to make someone restless,to belittle them & think of them as valueless.(of no importance)

 

Allah says in the Quran:17/64: Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء

 

وَاسْتَفْزِزْ مَنِ اسْتَطَعْتَ مِنْهُم بِصَوْتِكَ وَأَجْلِبْ عَلَيْهِم بِخَيْلِكَ وَرَجِلِكَ وَشَارِكْهُمْ فِي الْأَمْوَالِ وَالْأَوْلَادِ وَعِدْهُمْ ۚ وَمَا يَعِدُهُمُ الشَّيْطَانُ إِلَّا غُرُورًا

And incite [to senselessness] whoever you can among them with your voice and assault them with your horses and foot soldiers and become a partner in their wealth and their children and promise them." But Satan does not promise them except delusion.

 

Summary:

 

1.        Faragha فَرَغَ : a heart empty/void of  peace & patience.

2.        Jazee’a  جَزِعَ  : to scream,moan or say sad things after the occurance of a clamity.

3.        Fazee’a   فَزِعَ: anxiety because of fright of something.

4.        Karub  كَرُب: That anxiety which includes the element of sadness in it.

5.        Halaa’a  هَلْع: extreme form of jazee’a  جَزِعَ.

6.         Iztarra   اِضطَرَّ:to be restless/anxious to fullfill some need.

7.        Istaffazza  استَفَزَّ : to make someone restless/anxious.
