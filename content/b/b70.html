70 – Wife

 

In the Quran, no specific word has been used for ‘wife’, instead metaphorical words have been used.  These words are: زوج, حلائل, اِمرَأَةٌ, نِسَاءٌ, صاحبة and اَهل.

 

70.1 زوج: refers to pair or couple.  In a couple, both are زوج to each other, the husband is the زوج of the wife and the wife is the زوج of the husband.  For example,

1.      For a woman:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - البقرة سورة

2:35

 وَقُلۡنَا يَـٰٓـَٔادَمُ ٱسۡكُنۡ أَنتَ وَزَوۡجُكَ ٱلۡجَنَّةَ (﻿٣٥﻿)

And We said, "O Adam, dwell, you and your wife, in Paradise (35)

 

2.      For a man:

Surat Al-Mujādila (The Pleading Woman) - المجادلة سورة

58:1

(﻿١﻿)زَوۡجِهَا فِى تُجَـٰدِلُكَ ٱلَّتِى قَوۡلَ ٱللَّهُ سَمِعَ قَدۡ

Certainly has Allah heard the speech of the one who argues with you, [O Muhammad], concerning her husband (1).

 

زوج is also used to describe the couple together.

________________________________________

 

70.2 حلائل: حلائل is the plural of حليلهّ (feminine) and حليل (masculine), from حلال, a woman who one is lawfully married to or wife.  حليل is not used in the Quran but حليلهّ is:

 

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - النساء سورة

4:23

 وَحَلَـٰٓٮِٕلُ أَبۡنَآٮِٕڪُمُ ٱلَّذِينَ مِنۡ أَصۡلَـٰبِڪُمۡ (﻿٢٣﻿)

And [also prohibited are] the wives of your sons who are from your [own] loins (23)

70.3 اِمرَأَةٌ: used to describe an ordinary woman but has also been used to refer to a wife, for example:

Surat At-Taĥrīm (The Prohibtiion) -  التحريم سورة

66:10

 ضَرَبَ ٱللَّهُ مَثَلاً۬ لِّلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ ٱمۡرَأَتَ نُوحٍ۬ وَٱمۡرَأَتَ لُوطٍ۬‌(﻿١٠﻿)

Allah presents an example of those who disbelieved: the wife of Noah and the wife of Lot (10).

________________________________________

 

70.4 نِسَاءٌ: it is the broken plural of اِمرَأَةٌ, used to describe ordinary women.  This term has also been used to describe wives.  Allah the exalted says:

 

Surat Al-'Aĥzāb (The Combined Forces) - الأحزاب سورة

33:32

 يَـٰنِسَآءَ ٱلنَّبِىِّ لَسۡتُنَّ ڪَأَحَدٍ۬ مِّنَ ٱلنِّسَآءِ (﻿٣٢﻿)

O wives of the Prophet, you are not like anyone among women (32).

________________________________________

 

70.5 صاحبة: صاحب refers to a companion or friend, its feminine صاحبة has been used in the meaning of wife.  Allah (the exalted) says:

 

Surat `Abasa (He Frowned) - عبس سورة

81:34-36

 يَوۡمَ يَفِرُّ ٱلۡمَرۡءُ مِنۡ أَخِيهِ (﻿٣٤﻿) وَأُمِّهِۦ وَأَبِيهِ (﻿٣٥﻿) وَصَـٰحِبَتِهِۦ وَبَنِيهِ (﻿٣٦﻿)

On the Day a man will flee from his brother (34) and his mother and his father (35) and his wife and his children (36).

________________________________________

 

70.6 اَهل: الرِّجال اَهل refers a people who are of the same origin (background) as one or people who live in the same dwelling.  It is also used for referring to one’s wife.  In the Quran we find:

 

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) -  يوسف سورة

12: 25

قَالَتۡ مَا جَزَآءُ مَنۡ أَرَادَ بِأَهۡلِكَ سُوٓءًا (﻿٢٥﻿)

She said, "What is the recompense of one who intended evil for your wife (25)
