21 BICHANA [To Lay/Spread]

Synonyms:DAHA(DHW)[(دَحَي (دحو)],TAHA(THW)[طَحَي (طحو)],SATAHA(سَطَحَ),FARASHA(فَرَشَ),MAHADA(مَهَدَ)

21.1-2 DAHA دَحَي and TAHA طَحَي :both these words are used for the same meaning.only differnce is of pronounciation.making of different dialects. in the HOLY QURAN both these word are used only once and comes in one meaning.It is said DAHA ALMATARU ALHASA(دَحَي المَطَرَ الْحَصَي) rain flooded small stones far and wide. and DAHA ARRAJUL(دَحَي الرَّجُل) meaning this person travelled the entire country (meaning far and wide).so DAHA and TAHA means lay or spread out something far and wide. both the related ayahs of Holy QURAN are

Surat Ash-Shams (The Sun) - سورة الشمس

91:5

وَالسَّمَاءِ وَمَا بَنَاهَا

And [by] the sky and He who constructed it

Surat Ash-Shams (The Sun) - سورة الشمس

91:6

وَالْأَرْضِ وَمَا طَحَاهَا

And [by] the earth and He who spread it

Surat An-Nāzi`āt (Those who drag forth) - سورة النازعات

79:30

وَالْأَرْضَ بَعْدَ ذَٰلِكَ دَحَاهَا

And after that He spread the earth.

and from modern research it is known that DAHA's meaning include the concept of roundness in it.UDHIYYAHTUN(اِدْحِيَّةُ)  meaning the place in sand  where ostrich lays its egg. and UDHIY ALNNA3AM(اُدْحِيُ النَّعَام) meaning the place in sand  where ostrich lays its egg, and DHWAH(دحوة) is said for ostrich's egg. and word DAHA can be used to prove roundness of earth [side note:ostrich egg is rather elongated eliptical so is the earth, in other words true earth shape is the closest thing to ostrich egg' shape]

21.3 SATAHA(سَطَحَ):basic meaning is a)spread and then b)to level.SATAHA ALBAYT(سَطَحَ الْبَيْتَ) to level house's roof. and MASTAH(مَسْطَحْ) is the tool used for leveling.the upper part of house and roof is called SATAH(سطح). in Imam Raghib's word ASSATAH A3LA ALBAYTI JU3ILA SAWIYYAN(اَلسَّطْحُ اَعْلَي الْبَيْتِ جُعِلَ سَوِيّاً) . In the QURAN

Surat Al-Ghāshiyah (The Overwhelming) - سورة الغاشية

88:20

وَإِلَى الْأَرْضِ كَيْفَ سُطِحَتْ

And at the earth - how it is spread out?

21.4 FARASHA( فَرَشَ):meaning cloth etc spreading,preparing bed. and every thing that can be spread out is FRSH(فَرَشَ) and FIRASH(فِراش)[meaning 'spreading']. basic meaning of FRSH is to spread something and to open it meaning to spread something in it's entirely. and FARASHA ADDAR(فَرَشَ الدّار) meaning to pave floor.to lay bricks and stones.

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:22

الَّذِي جَعَلَ لَكُمُ الْأَرْضَ فِرَاشًا وَالسَّمَاءَ بِنَاءً وَأَنزَلَ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ مَاءً فَأَخْرَجَ بِهِ مِنَ الثَّمَرَاتِ رِزْقًا لَّكُمْ ۖ فَلَا تَجْعَلُوا لِلَّهِ أَندَادًا وَأَنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ

[He] who made for you the earth a bed [spread out] and the sky a ceiling and sent down from the sky, rain and brought forth thereby fruits as provision for you. So do not attribute to Allah equals while you know [that there is nothing similar to Him].

21.5 MAHADA(مَهَدَ):as per author of MANJAD, as SATAHA is used to spread and level the roof,likewise for earth MAHADA is used. but MAHD(مَهْد) is also mother's lap which includes the aspect of upbringing. and MAHD ALARD(مَهَدَ الارْض ) would mean that spreading earth in such a manner that appropriate provisions would be availabe to it's inhabitants. and from the Holy QURAN it gets verified. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Muddaththir (The Cloaked One) - سورة المدثر

74:14

وَمَهَّدتُّ لَهُ تَمْهِيدًا

And spread [everything] before him, easing [his life].

Surat Al-Muddaththir (The Cloaked One) - سورة المدثر

74:15

ثُمَّ يَطْمَعُ أَنْ أَزِيدَ

Then he desires that I should add more.

51:48

وَالْأَرْضَ فَرَشْنَاهَا فَنِعْمَ الْمَاهِدُونَ

And the earth We have spread out, and excellent is the preparer.

also got proved that MAHAD contains more vastness than FARASHA.meaning readiness of relevant stuff before its need

Summary

21.1-2 DAHA and TAHA: To take something from its place and to spread it out far and wide

21.3 SATAHA: spread out or laid out and then to level it

21.4 FARASHA: to spread out something entirely and level it

21.5 MAHAD:spread as well as provide in it resources of training
