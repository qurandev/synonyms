22.1 ZAJARA:to say blustering talk to some one in a loud voice.to scold.to rebuke.to reprimand.and ZAJRATUN(زَجْرَةٌ) meaning admonish.In the QURAN

Surat An-Nāzi`āt (Those who drag forth) - سورة النازعات

79:13

فَإِنَّمَا هِيَ زَجْرَةٌ وَاحِدَةٌ

Indeed, it will be but one shout,

Surat Aş-Şāffāt (Those who set the Ranks) - سورة الصافات

37:2

فَالزَّاجِرَاتِ زَجْرًا

22.2 IZDAJARA:meaning to buzz off someone by scolding.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Qamar (The Moon) - سورة القمر

54:9

كَذَّبَتْ قَبْلَهُمْ قَوْمُ نُوحٍ فَكَذَّبُوا عَبْدَنَا وَقَالُوا مَجْنُونٌ وَازْدُجِرَ

The people of Noah denied before them, and they denied Our servant and said, "A madman," and he was repelled.

22.3 NAHARA:meaning strong flow of some thing liquid-NAHAR ASSA-IL نَهَرَ السّائِل meaning to scold the one asking(of some need).to be stern in rebuking and scolding-AZZAJR ALGHALIYZ(الزّجر الغليظ ). ALLAH said

SUMMARY:

22.1 ZAJARA:Scolding,Rebuking

22.2 IZDAJARA:To scram someone by rebuking

22.3 NAHARA:to rebuke strongly.
