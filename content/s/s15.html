15 SARKASHI KARNA [rebellious, insubordinate]

Synonyms: TAGHA, A’TA, A’LA, MARADA

15.1 TAGHA (طَغٰی): means to surpass all the limits in disobedience, extremely rebellious, TAGH-AL-MAA’ (طَغَاالْمَاء) means flooding of water, inundation (dictionary Al Manjad). TAGHIYAH (طَاغِیَۃ) means a violent storm. A huge thunderstorm. And TAGHOOT (طَاغُوْت) means something you offer the service to, except Allah, that can be a person or system. Therefore TUGHYANI (طُغیانی) refers to both surpassing limits and wrath, predominance. Allah says in the Holy Quran.

Surah Al-Alaq (The Clot): سورۃ العَلق 96:6

كَلَّآ إِنَّ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنَ لَيَطۡغَىٰٓ  أَن رَّءَاهُ ٱسۡتَغۡنَىٰٓ

 In fact, man oversteps all bounds. When he considers himself exempt.

15.2 A’TA (عَتَا): ATAWIDAL-ALA-ASTAKBRAM (عتویدل علی استکبارام) means defiance due to pride. A person considering himself powerful and disobeys Allah’s orders. In the Quran

Surah Al-Mulk (The sovereignty): سُوۡرَةُ المُلک 67:21

 بَل لَّجُّواْ فِى عُتُوٍّ۬ وَنُفُورٍ

Yet they persist in defiance and aversion.

15.3 A’LA (عَلَا): To rise, to dominate, to be arrogant (dictionary Al Manjad). To insubordinate and unruly after gaining power and authority. In the Quran

Surah Al-Israa (The Night Journey): سورۃ الٕاسرَاء  17:4

 لَتُفۡسِدُنَّ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ مَرَّتَيۡنِ وَلَتَعۡلُنَّ عُلُوًّ۬ا ڪَبِيرً۬ا

That twice would they do mischief on the earth and be elated with mighty arrogance (and twice would they be punished)!

15.4 MARADA (مَرَدَ): means to be disobedient and unruly, to surpass your peers (dictionary Al Manjad). Thus MARADA (مَرَدَ) means to surpass everyone in disobedience and insubordination. Allah says in the Holy Quran.

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:117

 وَإِن يَدۡعُونَ إِلَّا شَيۡطَـٰنً۬ا مَّرِيدً۬ا

 In fact, they invoke none but a rebellious devil.

Summary:

15.1 TAGHA: Encroach with dominance and wrath

15.2 A’TA: Disobedience due to arrogance

15.3 A’LA: Unruly after gain power and authority

15.4 MARADA: To surpass in being rebellious
