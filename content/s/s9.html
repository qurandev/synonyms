9 SACH [Truth]

Synonyms: SIDQ, HAQQ

9.1 SIDQ (صِدْق): SADAQA (صَدَقَ) means to speak the truth (opposite KAZAB کذب), And not tell anything that deviates from or perverts the actual incident. A well-known word. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Az-Zumar (The groups): سورۃ الزُّمَر 39:32

فَمَنۡ أَظۡلَمُ مِمَّن ڪَذَبَ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ وَكَذَّبَ بِٱلصِّدۡقِ إِذۡ جَآءَهُ ۥۤ‌ۚ

Who is more evil than he who lies about God, and denies the truth when it has come to him?

9.2 HAQQ (حَقّ): (opposite BATAL باطل) HAQQ (حَقّ) is a fact that has been verified and proven through experiments and contemplation, truth. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Fussilat (Explained in Detail): سورۃ فُصّلَت 41:53

 سَنُرِيهِمۡ ءَايَـٰتِنَا فِى ٱلۡأَفَاقِ وَفِىٓ أَنفُسِہِمۡ حَتَّىٰ يَتَبَيَّنَ لَهُمۡ أَنَّهُ ٱلۡحَقُّ‌ۗ

 We will show them Our proofs on the horizons, and in their very souls, until it becomes clear to them that it is the truth.

Summary:

9.1 SIDQ: Truth

9.2 HAQQ: a proven fact, Truth.

The Truth 

In the Quran, the words صِدقand حَقّhave been used for this meaning.

1- صِدق

صَدَقَ means to tell the truth (Ant: كَذَبَ) meaning not saying something fictitious. It is a common word.

فَمَنۡ أَظۡلَمُ مِمَّن ڪَذَبَ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ وَكَذَّبَ بِٱلصِّدۡقِ إِذۡ جَآءَهُ 

“And who doth greater wrong than he who telleth a lie against Allah, and denieth the truth when it reacheth him? “ 39/32

2- حَقّ(Ant: بَاطِل)

It is everything that has been proven correct after close observation. In other words, it is the empirical truth rather than theoretical truth. 

سَنُرِيهِمۡ ءَايَـٰتِنَا فِى ٱلۡأَفَاقِ وَفِىٓ أَنفُسِہِمۡ حَتَّىٰ يَتَبَيَّنَ لَهُمۡ أَنَّهُ ٱلۡحَقُّ*ۗ 

“We shall show them Our portents on the horizons and within themselves until it will be manifest unto them that it is the Truth “41/53
