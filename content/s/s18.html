18 SUSTI KARNA, SUST HONA [ to be lazy]

Synonyms: KASAL, WAHAN, WANAA, ISTAHSARA, BATTA’A

18.1 KASAL (کَسَلَ): To be grievous due to lack of interest and heart. To be lazy and slow. Allah says in the Holy Quran  

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:142

 وَإِذَا قَامُوٓاْ إِلَى ٱلصَّلَوٰةِ قَامُواْ كُسَالَىٰ

And when they stand for prayer, they stand lazily,

18.2 WAHANA (وَھَنَ): To be lazy due to physical weakness. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:104

 وَلَا تَهِنُواْ فِى ٱبۡتِغَآءِ ٱلۡقَوۡمِ‌ۖ

And slacken not in following up the enemy:

18.3 WANAA (وَنٰی): to neglect, to be lazy, to be sloppy (dictionary Al Manjad). To make no efforts. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Ta-Ha (Ta-ha): سورۃ طٰھٰ  20:42

ٱذۡهَبۡ أَنتَ وَأَخُوكَ بِـَٔايَـٰتِى وَلَا تَنِيَا فِى ذِكۡرِى

Go, you and your brother, with My signs, and do not neglect My remembrance.

18.4 ISTAHSAR (اِسْتَحْسَرَ): HASARA (حَسَرَ) means to get tired, weary, HASAR-AL-BASAR (حَسَرَالْبَصَرَ) means a weary sight, weak eyesight. HASEER (حَسِیْر) means tired and weak (dictionary Al Manjad). HASAR (حسر) is to be lazy due to fatigue, tedium. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Anbiya (the prophets): سورۃ الانَبیاء 21:19

 وَمَنۡ عِندَهُ ۥ لَا يَسۡتَكۡبِرُونَ عَنۡ عِبَادَتِهِۦ وَلَا يَسۡتَحۡسِرُونَ

Even those who are in His (very) Presence are not too proud to serve Him, nor are they (ever) weary (of His service):

18.5 BATTA’A (بَطَّاَ): AL-BATU’U (اَلبَطْؤُ) to be lazy, to delay while walking, IBTA’UN(اِبْطَاءٌ) means to walk lazily. And BATTA’A (بَطَّاَ) means utmost laziness, and delay, to lag. In the Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:72

 وَإِنَّ مِنكُمۡ لَمَن لَّيُبَطِّئَنَّ

 Among you is he who lags behind

18.1 KASAL: Laziness due to lack of interest

18.2 WAHANA: Laziness due to physical weakness

18.3 WANAA: Laziness due to carelessness

18.4 ISTAHSARA: Laziness due to fatigue

18.5 BATTA’A: Be lazy and cause delay, laziness
