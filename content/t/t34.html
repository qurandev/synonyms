34. To frown/scowl:

 

Abasa عَبَسَ  ,kalaha كَلَحَ ,basara بَسَرَ are the words used.

1.        Abasa عَبَسَ : Means to knit the brows/eye brows,to have wrinkles on the forehead between both eyes. This is the first stage/degree

 

As per the Quran:80/1: Surat `Abasa (He Frowned) - سورة عبس

 

عَبَسَ وَتَوَلَّىٰ

The Prophet frowned and turned away

2.        Kalaha كَلَحَ : This is the second/next degree of abasa عَبَسَ . Have wrinkles on the face that it doesn’t look nice. According to some,it means moving lips up & down in such a manner that teeth starts showing & face doesn’t look nice.

 

As per Quran: 23/104: Surat Al-Mu'minūn (The Believers) - سورة المؤمنون

 

تَلْفَحُ وُجُوهَهُمُ النَّارُ وَهُمْ فِيهَا كَالِحُونَ

The Fire will sear their faces, and they therein will have taut smiles.

 

3.        Basara بَسَرَ: This is the last degree of abasa عَبَسَ . When frowning reaches its maximum limit,then it is called basara بَسَرَ. To frown/scowl.

 

As per the Quran: 74/21-22: Surat Al-Muddaththir (The Cloaked One) - سورة المدثر

ثُمَّ نَظَرَ ثُمَّ عَبَسَ وَبَسَرَ

Then he considered [again]; Then he frowned and scowled;
