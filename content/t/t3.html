Fresh-3

Synonyms are:

1)    طَرِيّ

2)    رطب

3)    نَضْرَةً

(نضر)

4)    نَاعِمَةٌ

1)tarii طَرِيّ:

means fresh (unwithered

) .and is used for meat Fiqhul lughat - فقه اللغة

45. Tarawat طراوت means

freshness.

Allah says:

وَمِنْ كُلٍّ تَأْكُلُونَ لَحْمًا

طَرِيًّا وَتَسْتَخْرِجُونَ حِلْيَةً تَلْبَسُونَهَا

(surah

35 fatir verse 12)

Transaltion: And from each you eat tender meat and

extract ornaments which you wear.

2)ratab رطب

:means fresh and its opposite is yabis يَابِس

means dried out. But in common terminology the word Ratab رطب  is

fixed for  ripe and fresh dates Mufraddat – مفردات

Allah says:

 وَهُزِّي إِلَيْكِ بِجِذْعِ

النَّخْلَةِ تُسَاقِطْ عَلَيْكِ رُطَبًا جَنِيًّا

 (surah 19

maryum verse 25)

Translation: And shake toward you the trunk of the

palm tree; it will drop upon you ripe, fresh dates.

3)nadrataan نَضْرَةً

:nadara نَضَرَ means to be soft, fresh and beautiful Munjid - منجد

Maqaayeesul-lughat and nadrah نضرة means the beauty and

elegance/charm  in  complexion Maqaayeesul-lughat.the

freshness ,bloom and glow at face.

Allah says:

فَوَقَاهُمُ اللَّهُ شَرَّ ذَٰلِكَ

الْيَوْمِ وَلَقَّاهُمْ نَضْرَةً وَسُرُورًا

(surah 76 insaan verse 11)

Translation:

So

Allah will protect them from the evil of that Day and give them radiance and

happiness.

4)naayimaatun

نَاعِمَةٌ:means comfortable life,comfortable state Munjid - منجد.

freshness of face due to plenty of

blessings and comfortable condition .

Allah says:

وُجُوهٌ يَوْمَئِذٍ نَاعِمَةٌ لِسَعْيِهَا

رَاضِيَةٌ

(surah 88 ghashiyaah verse 8-9)

Transaltion: [Other] faces, that Day, will show

pleasure. With their effort [they are] satisfied.

Summary

1) tarrii usually used for the freshness of meat.

2)ratab: freshness of dates or for fresh dates

3)nadrah:glow of face and for pure environment.

4)nayyimah:used for freshness due to comfortable condition.
