30. To prepare:

Hayya هَيَّا  ,Aa’atad اَعتَد   , Aa’adda اعَدَّ ,jaahhaz   جَهَّزَare the words used.

1.        Hayya هَيَّا:  hayatan هيءةً means the perceptible state of some object . hayya  هَيَّاmeans to arrange something. Correct/improve it, prepare it. In other words, to prepare grounds to complete/do something.

Allah says in the Quran: 18/16: Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف

وَإِذِ اعْتَزَلْتُمُوهُمْ وَمَا يَعْبُدُونَ إِلَّا اللَّهَ فَأْوُوا إِلَى الْكَهْفِ يَنشُرْ لَكُمْ رَبُّكُم مِّن رَّحْمَتِهِ وَيُهَيِّئْ لَكُم مِّنْ أَمْرِكُم مِّرْفَقًا

[The youths said to one another], "And when you have withdrawn from them and that which they worship other than Allah , retreat to the cave. Your Lord will spread out for you of His mercy and will prepare for you from your affair facility."

 

2.        Aa’atad  اَعتَد  :  Al-itaad  العتاد refers to such stuff  which is prepared for some mission/objective. & atudd  عَتُدmeans to agree,to be ready. Aa’atad اَعتَد   means to prepare.

Allah says in the Quran:18/102: Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف

أَفَحَسِبَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا أَن يَتَّخِذُوا عِبَادِي مِن دُونِي أَوْلِيَاءَ ۚ إِنَّا أَعْتَدْنَا جَهَنَّمَ لِلْكَافِرِينَ نُزُلًا

Then do those who disbelieve think that they can take My servants instead of Me as allies? Indeed, We have prepared Hell for the disbelievers as a lodging.

3.        Aa’addaاعَدَّ : means to prepare or to present. Meaning to present after preparing. Adda عَدَّ means to count & aa’adda  اعَدَّmeans to present something after preparing each & everything required for its preparation.

Allah says in the Quran: 2/24: Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

فَإِن لَّمْ تَفْعَلُوا وَلَن تَفْعَلُوا فَاتَّقُوا النَّارَ الَّتِي وَقُودُهَا النَّاسُ وَالْحِجَارَةُ ۖ أُعِدَّتْ لِلْكَافِرِينَ

But if you do not - and you will never be able to - then fear the Fire, whose fuel is men and stones, prepared for the disbelievers.

Many people of dictionary take aa’tadd  اَعتَد   as adad اعدد. According to Imam Raaghib,Aa’tadna  اعتدنا was actually aa’dadna   اعددنا. When first ‘daal’ was replaced by ‘teh’,then it became aatadna  اعتدنا .In other words, according to most people of dictionary,both, aa’adda اعَدَّ  & aatad اَعتَد   have the same meaning.

 

4.        Jahazza  جَهَّزَ :Jihaaz  جِهاز  is that luggage of passengers which is ready/prepared. & jahazza    جَهَّزَmeans to load already prepared luggage & send it to its destination. Generally this word is used for preparing dead bodies & sending them to their final destination ( tajheezo takfeen  تجهيزوتكفين ) or for preparing luggage of passengers.

 

Allah says in the Quran: 12/70: Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

فَلَمَّا جَهَّزَهُم بِجَهَازِهِمْ جَعَلَ السِّقَايَةَ فِي رَحْلِ أَخِيهِ ثُمَّ أَذَّنَ مُؤَذِّنٌ أَيَّتُهَا الْعِيرُ إِنَّكُمْ لَسَارِقُونَ

So when he had furnished them with their supplies, he put the [gold measuring] bowl into the bag of his brother. Then an announcer called out, "O caravan, indeed you are thieves."

 

Summary:

1.        Hayya هَيَّا : to arrange or prepare something to make something

2.        Atada اَعتَد   :To prepare things required to make something

3.        Aa’addaاعَدَّ : Prepare completely everything required for an objective

4.        Jahazza  جَهَّزَ : to prepare luggae for passengers Or to prepare  a dead body for their destination
