Darkness prevailing -2

Synonyms are:

1)    عَسْعَسَ

2)    غَسَقَ

3)    غَطَشَ

4)    وَقَبَ

5)    سَجٰى

6)    اَظْلَمَ

1)aasassa عَسْعَسَ: meaning evening

fading away (to night).spreading of darkness , soon after the sun set(dusk)

likewise , twilight just before the sun rise(dawn) Mufraddat – مفردات

Allah says in Quran:

وَاللَّيْلِ

إِذَا عَسْعَسَ وَالصُّبْحِ إِذَا تَنَفَّسَ

(surah 81 taqweer

verse 17-18)

Translation:

And by the night as it closes in. And by the dawn when it breathes

2)

غَسَقَ ghsaqa :the darkness after twilight(afterglow)

is disappeared . Beginning of the night .The first part of night.

Fiqhul lughat - فقه اللغة 34.

Allah says in Quran:

أَقِمِ

الصَّلَاةَ لِدُلُوكِ الشَّمْسِ إِلَى غَسَقِ اللَّيْلِ وَقُرْآنَ الْفَجْرِ

(surah 17 bani israeel verse 78)

translation: Establish regular prayers -- at the

sun's decline till the darkness of the night, and the morning prayer and

reading.

3)

اَغْطَشَ

aghtasha:ghtasha غَطَشَ  (al laiil الليل) means a dark night. And ghtash غطش

means to have weak vision or blurred vision. Likewise غطاش ghtaassh

means a dark night and it also means weak

vision Munjid – منجد

that means darkness in which things can be seen blurry.

Allah says in Quran:

وَأَغْطَشَ

لَيْلَهَا وَأَخْرَجَ ضُحَاهَا

(surah 79 an naziaat  verse 29)

Transaltion:

And He darkened its night and extracted its brightness.

4)

وَقَبَ waqaba: وَقْب waqab a pit

in a mountain.and waqaba  وَقَبَ  means to enter in a pit and disappear.

وقب الشمس waqab ashamss means

sunset .waqab azzalaam  وقب الظلام  means darkness prevailing so much

that things

are  disappeared  in it. . Mufraddat – مفردات

Allah says in Quran:

وَمِنْ

شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ

(surah 113  falaq verse 3)

Translation: From the

mischief of Darkness as it overspreads.

5)

سَجٰى sajjaa : سجى اليل  sajaa alleeil means night that is desolate

and quiet. سَجَى

الميِّت saja al maiyyat means to cover the corpse with a sheet and warp it

and hide it. سجَّ مَعَائِب اخِيك  saja

maaib akheeik means to hide the faults of your brother Munjid - منجد.

so in this word (sajjaa) there is a

concept of hiding and of  calmness. Means

most portion of the night is spent so that all people fell asleep and darkness

is totally prevailed.

Allah says:

 وَالضُّحَى وَاللَّيْلِ إِذَا سَجَى

(surah 93 ad duhaa verse

1-2)

Translation: By

the morning brightness, And [by] the night when it covers with darkness.

6)اَظْلَمَ

azlam: ظَلِمَ zalima(ظُلْماً zulmaan) اَظْلَمَ الّيل azlam allaieel

means night that is dark Munjid - منجد.darkness

prevailing. This word is not fixed with night instead if in day due to cloud or

some other reason darkness prevails this word can be used. zulmaah

ظُلْمَة means darkness and its opposite is light(nor نور)and the

plurals are ظُلَم zulam, ظُلُم zulom, ظُلَمٰت zulamaat

and ظُلُمات zulomaat. Munjid – منجد

Allah says:

يَكَادُ

الْبَرْقُ يَخْطَفُ أَبْصَارَهُمْ  كُلَّمَا

أَضَاء لَهُم مَّشَوْاْ فِيهِ وَإِذَا أَظْلَمَ عَلَيْهِمْ قَامُواْ.

(surah 2 al baqrah verse 20)

Translation: The lightning almost snatches away

their sight. Every time it lights [the way] for them, they walk therein; but

when darkness comes over them, they stand [still].

Summary

1)assassa:means the twilight at the

time of morning and evening.

2)ghasaq:the beginning part of

night.

3)ghtash :so dark that the things

are not visible.

4)waqaba:when things disappear in

darkness.

5)sajaa:quietness and darkness of

late night.

6)zukumaat:a common word used for

darkness whether it be of night or due to any other reason.
