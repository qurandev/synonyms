Plank-7

Synonyms are:

1)

زُبَر

2)

اَلوَاحَ

1)zubara زُبَر:plural

of zubarah زُبَرَة and zubrah زُبَرَة means an iron plank,

sheet. Mufraddat – مفردات  Munjid – منجد.

Allah says in Quran:

 آتُونِي زُبَرَ الْحَدِيدِ

(surah 18 kahaf verse 96)

Translation: Bring

me sheets of iron

2)alwahh اَلوَاحَ: singular is looh لوح  and it means a plank of a

ship/boat etc.

وَحَمَلْنَاهُ عَلَىٰ ذَاتِ أَلْوَاحٍ وَدُسُرٍ

(surah 54

al qamar veres 13)

Translation: And we carried him on a [construction of] planks and nails,

Moreover looh لوح

is also known as a plank of wood on which something can be written

Mufraddat – مفردات

Allah says:

وَكَتَبْنَا لَهُ فِي الْأَلْوَاحِ مِن كُلِّ شَيْءٍ مَّوْعِظَةً

(surah 7

aaraaf verse 145)

Translation:

And We wrote for him on the tablets, the advice for all things.

Summary

1)zubar:usually used for iron sheets or big planks.

2)looh:plank of wood or a wooden plate on which one can write.
